Data & methodology (click to expand)

5 teams analyzed (306s–378s kill times), all running HPal/RSham/MW/PEvo. Per-player sourceID queries pulled every healer's complete cast timeline — CDs discovered from data, not assumed. Consensus scored across all 5 teams.

Frostmane (EU) 306s • Burning Blade (CN) 308s • Swift Breeze (CN) 310s • Luo Ning (CN) 329s • Golden Plains (CN) 378s

Key findings: Ascendance at opener + Upheaval 3 is universal (5/5, 3min CD fits the ~3:09 gap). SLT covers Umbral Collapse soaks (~1:55), not Upheaval directly. Revival saved for Upheaval 3 (4/5 at ~3:49). CC on strict 90s rotation covering intermissions. No DH/Warrior in data teams — our Darkness + Rally are bonus CDs for the hardest window. Zephyr barely used (1/5). 0/5 RShams run HTT. All timers verified against CD durations.
