Actionable Improvements

#1: Bursting Emptiness is the Primary Killer (6 Deaths)

Bursting Emptiness hits for up to 388K unmitigated and killed Zinnks 6 times across the session. In nearly every case, the death sequence shows Zinnks already low from Simulacrum Backlash ticks + Voidstalker Sting DoT when the burst lands. Fix: Use Obsidian Scales defensively BEFORE Bursting Emptiness casts. With 5 uses in the best pull, the CD is being used, but timing needs to be synchronized with the Bursting Emptiness pattern. Zephyr was only used once in 5:40 -- this is the raid CD and should be used more proactively during predictable burst windows. Consider also using Renewing Blaze if talented, or Emerald Blossom to pre-top before the burst.

#2: P2 Survival Rate of 33% (Only 4/12 P2 Pulls Survived to P3)

Phase 2 is the wall. Of 12 pulls that reached P2, only 4 made it through to P3. The combination of Simulacrum Backlash (steady ticking AoE), Voidstalker Sting (DoT), and periodic burst abilities (Bursting Emptiness, Silverstrike Ricochet) creates a sustained damage profile that overwhelms without proactive defensives. Fix: Map out the P2 defensive rotation. Obsidian Scales has a 2.5-minute CD -- use it for the first major burst, then again when it comes back up. Use Time Spiral to reset Hover for repositioning away from dangerous overlaps. Healthstone and Health Potion should be held specifically for P2 moments when health is low and another mechanic is incoming.

#3: Simulacrum Backlash Chip Damage is Enabling Other Deaths

Looking at death sequences for F16, F22, F24, and F28, Simulacrum Backlash appears as 20-35K ticks in the events leading up to the killing blow. These ticks individually are not lethal, but they drain health pools to the point where the next burst ability becomes fatal. Fix: Position to minimize Simulacrum Backlash overlap. If there are multiple Rift Simulacrums active, standing between their backlash zones maximizes exposure. Prioritize killing Rift Simulacrums quickly to reduce the number of active backlash sources. As the Augmentation Evoker, buffing the DPS who are cleaving Simulacrums with Prescience will help them die faster.

#4: Intermission Consistency (1 Death, But Close Calls)

F20 death at 2:34 from Stellar Emission + Orbiting Matter shows the intermission can be dangerous. Orbiting Matter hit for 320K, suggesting Zinnks was hit by a mechanic that should be dodged. Fix: The intermission is scripted and predictable. Save Obsidian Scales or Hover for the Stellar Emission channel. The Orbiting Matter hit suggests standing in a bad position during the platform transition. Use Glide (already used 6x in best pull) for positioning but verify timing relative to Orbiting Matter spawns.

#5: Zephyr Usage -- Raid CD Being Underutilized

Zephyr (AoE damage reduction for the group) was only used once in the best 5:40 pull. This is a 2-minute CD, meaning it should be available 2-3 times per pull in P2+P3. This ability reduces AoE damage taken by the group and can be coordinated with the healing team for high-damage overlaps. Fix: Discuss with the raid leader where Zephyr should be assigned. Stellar Emission in intermission, first Bursting Emptiness in P2, or a P3 overlap are all strong candidates. Using it on CD would provide significantly more raid-wide damage reduction.
