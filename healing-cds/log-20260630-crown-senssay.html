Actionable Items

#1: Reduce P1 Overheal from 50.8% to Below 35%

Over half of P1 healing output is wasted. In fight 25, Senssay produced 15.4M effective healing and 15.9M overheal in P1. P1 damage is manageable (Sentinels + Void Droplets). During P1 downtime windows, replace unnecessary Vivify casts with Soothing Mist channeling or simply let Renewing Mist ticks do the work. Each wasted Vivify in P1 costs mana that is not available for P2/P3. This compounds over an 8+ minute fight where mana becomes the constraint.

#2: Clean Up Intermission Survival (2 of 4 Early Deaths)

Two of four actionable early deaths were in Intermission: Singularity Eruption (F5, 2:31) and Orbiting Matter (F23, 2:42). The Intermission is a pure survival check during Stellar Emission channel. Pre-position before 2:14, use Fortifying Brew proactively as the transition starts, and consider saving a Life Cocoon for self-use during the overlap. Current CD data shows zero Life Cocoon or Celestial Conduit uses in Intermission -- there is an opportunity to deploy a defensive here.

#3: Reduce Vivify CPM from 11.0 toward 8.0

Vivify at 11.0 CPM with 46.8% overheal means roughly 5 Vivify casts per minute are landing on targets that do not need healing. The reference uses 7.4 CPM with a similar overheal rate -- they simply cast fewer of them. The saved globals can go to Soothing Mist channeling (which procs Invigorating Mists more efficiently) or to maintaining Renewing Mist more tightly. This also preserves mana for longer P3 pulls.

#4: CD Discipline Is Strong -- Keep It Consistent

Yu'lon in 18/20 pulls, Celestial Conduit in 16/20, Revival in 17/20 -- this is disciplined CD usage for a prog night. The CD timing consistency supports a reliable 2-3 cycle pattern as pulls get longer. As the team pushes deeper into P3, the third CD cycle will become critical -- the current pattern supports it.

#5: Coordinate Intermission CD Coverage with Team

In fight 25, only Silencio's Convoke at 2:20 covers Intermission. Senssay's first Celestial Conduit lands at 1:00 (P1) and would be available again by ~2:30 but is not used there. Discuss with the healing team whether Senssay should hold Celestial Conduit for Intermission, or deploy Revival during Stellar Emission. Currently zero CDs from 3 of 4 healers during this phase.
