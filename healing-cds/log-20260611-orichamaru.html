Bottom Line — Progression Context

This is wipe progression data on Crown of the Cosmos — a 3-phase fight the raid has not yet killed. Fight 23 (the longest pull at 4:52) is the primary analysis pull. Orichamaru died at 4:01 to Grasp of Emptiness, leaving 52s of dead time that drags the WCL-reported DPS to 155K. During alive time, Orichamaru dealt 229K DPS — actually comparable to the #5 reference Haotentwo's 224K DPS across a full 8:19 kill. Active-time rotational CPM is 44.7 vs the reference's 43.3 — the rotation is tight when casting. The biggest performance lever is not rotation execution but survival: staying alive through Phase 3 mechanics. The raid reached 0.01% boss HP on two pulls (F8 and F17) but has not closed it out. Comparing a 4:01 prog wipe to an 8:19 ranked kill is inherently unfair — the reference had double the time to use major cooldowns (8 Tyrants vs 3, 4 Imp Lords vs 2). External buff disparity is also massive: the reference received 3 Power Infusions, 23 Ebon Might, and 10 Prescience vs Orichamaru's 0 PI, 6 Ebon Might, and 1 Prescience.

External Buff Disparity

This is the elephant in the room. Haotentwo received 3 Power Infusions throughout the fight, was prioritized by an Augmentation Evoker for 23 Ebon Might and 10 Prescience applications. Orichamaru received zero PI, 6 Ebon Might, and 1 Prescience. Ebon Might alone adds roughly 5-8% DPS when maintained consistently, and Power Infusion adds another 5-8% per application window. Prescience boosts crit chance significantly. Combined, these external buffs could easily account for 15-20% of the reference's DPS. Any DPS comparison between these two players without acknowledging this gap is misleading. The raid team should consider PI and Aug Evoker buff allocation for Orichamaru — Demo Locks benefit enormously from haste (more casts during Tyrant windows) and crit (more Demonic Core procs).

Actionable Items

1. Use Dark Pact proactively and often. This is the single biggest survival improvement. Only 1 Dark Pact in 4:01 vs the reference's 7 in 8:19. Dark Pact is a 1-minute cooldown that absorbs ~200K damage. On a fight with constant Echoing Darkness ticks and periodic raid damage spikes, it should be used on cooldown or saved for known damage events (Simulacrum Backlash, Grasp of Emptiness windows). The killing blow (Grasp of Emptiness, 163K) was less damage than a single Dark Pact shield. Staying alive for the full fight is worth more DPS than any rotation optimization. Dead at 4:01 = 52s of zero DPS.
2. Prioritize Demonbolt over Shadow Bolt when Demonic Core is available. Shadow Bolt is at 6.6 CPM vs the reference's 5.7 CPM, while Demonbolt is at 5.9 vs 10.1 CPM. Shadow Bolt is the lowest-priority filler in the Demo rotation — it should only be cast when you have zero Demonic Core stacks AND are below 3 Soul Shards. Demonbolt deals 2-3x more damage, generates a Soul Shard (enabling more Hand of Gul'dan casts), and consumes Demonic Core procs that would otherwise cap. Watch for the Demonic Core buff icon and prioritize Demonbolt whenever 2+ stacks are available. This is where the current spec guide emphasizes: "Cast Demonbolt with 2+ stacks of Demonic Core" before ever touching Shadow Bolt.
3. Request Power Infusion and Aug Evoker prioritization. The external buff disparity is massive: 0 PI vs 3, 6 Ebon Might vs 23, 1 Prescience vs 10. Demo Locks are one of the best PI targets in the game because Haste directly translates to more casts during Tyrant windows, and the class scales multiplicatively with crit (more Demonic Core procs). If a Shadow Priest is in the raid, Orichamaru should be a high-priority PI target. Similarly, if an Aug Evoker is buffing other players preferentially, coordinating Ebon Might alignment with Tyrant windows would be a significant DPS increase — estimated 10-15K DPS from external buffs alone.
4. Use Demonic Circle and Gateway for Grasp of Emptiness survival. Grasp of Emptiness reduces movement speed by 60% on Mythic and killed Orichamaru. Pre-placing Demonic Circle before Phase 3 obelisk windows gives an instant repositioning tool to angle beams away without needing to walk through the slow. The reference used Demonic Circle: Teleport once — Orichamaru also used it once but died to the mechanic regardless. Combined with preemptive Dark Pact before the obelisk activates, this should be survivable.
5. Avoid Falling damage in phase transitions. Orichamaru took 411K falling damage across 2 hits — this is completely avoidable. On Crown of the Cosmos, platform transitions via Devouring Cosmos require picking up a feather or using the Warlock gateway. Falling damage suggests a missed feather or gateway mis-timing. Each 200K hit is equivalent to losing a Dark Pact's worth of effective HP. Use Glide (which Orichamaru did cast twice) or ensure feather pickup timing to eliminate this entirely.
What's Already Good

Active rotation: 44.7 active CPM matches the reference's 43.3 — there is zero meaningful rotation gap when targets are available. GCD usage during casting windows is tight with only one gap >3s outside the phase transition.

Tyrant setup: All 3 Tyrant casts were properly paired with Call Dreadstalkers (within 2-3s), and both Grimoire: Imp Lord casts aligned with Tyrant windows. This is textbook Demo Lock cooldown sequencing. Tyrant #3 was correctly held for Phase 2 targets rather than wasted during the transition.

Dreadstalker consistency: 20-23s intervals between casts, nearly on-cooldown. Average gap (excluding transition) of 21.5s vs the reference's 20.6s. Excellent.

AoE damage: Shadow Nova (4.6% vs 1.9%), Blighted Maw (5.1% vs 3.3%), and Infernal Bolt (3.8% vs 2.2%) all show higher percentage contributions than the reference, indicating smart use of multi-target abilities during Phase 1 Sentinel + Droplet windows.

Alive-time DPS: 229K DPS while alive competes with the reference's 224K across a full kill. The rotation is producing results — the improvement vector is survival, not throughput.

Summary

Orichamaru's Demonology Warlock performance is fundamentally sound from a rotation and cooldown management perspective. The active CPM, Tyrant alignment, Dreadstalker usage, and damage distribution all match or exceed the reference during active casting windows. The two primary improvement areas are: (1) Survival — Dark Pact usage (1 vs 7), falling damage avoidance (411K), and Grasp of Emptiness positioning. Staying alive for the full fight would add ~52s of casting time, equivalent to roughly 12M additional damage. (2) Demonbolt prioritization — spending Demonic Core procs on Demonbolt instead of defaulting to Shadow Bolt would increase both shard generation and direct damage. Additionally, the external buff gap (0 PI, minimal Aug Evoker support) accounts for an estimated 15-20% DPS disparity that is entirely outside Orichamaru's control. When this raid gets the kill, Orichamaru's DPS should be competitive with rankings given proper fight length and buff allocation.
