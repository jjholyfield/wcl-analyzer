Unholy DK Defensive Toolkit

Available defensives that should be planned for each pull:
  • Anti-Magic Shell (45s CD) — Absorbs magic damage. Should be used proactively on Cosmic Barrier, Void Expulsion, and Bursting Emptiness.
  • Icebound Fortitude (3 min CD) — 30% DR. Save for the most dangerous mechanic overlap in each phase.
  • Lichborne (2 min CD) — Fear immunity + self-healing via Death Coil. Strong as a recovery tool during heavy sustained damage.
  • Death Strike — Self-heal on demand. Use when below 50% HP to top off between mechanics.
  • Anti-Magic Zone (2 min CD) — Raid CD for magic damage. Coordinate with healers for Cosmic Barrier / Void Expulsion windows.

Actionable Improvements

1
Survival is Priority #1 — Plan Defensives for Every Mechanic
Bebeshakur died in every single pull and died before the wipe in 17 of 20 attempts. This is the single highest-impact improvement area. The rotation is fine — the issue is staying alive long enough to use it. Action: Before each pull, mentally map defensives to mechanics: AMS for Cosmic Barrier (every cast), IBF for the first major overlap, Death Strike when below 50%. The 45s CD on AMS means it should be available for every Cosmic Barrier cast.
2
Cosmic Barrier: 3 Deaths — Pre-Plan AMS
The #1 identified killing mechanic. Cosmic Barrier killed Bebeshakur at 3:44, 3:39, and 3:53 — all in mid-P2. This is a consistent failure point. Action: Use Anti-Magic Shell ~1 second before Cosmic Barrier completes its cast. If AMS absorbed the previous one and is still on cooldown, use Icebound Fortitude or Lichborne + Death Coil self-heal. Never eat Cosmic Barrier with no active defensive.
3
P1 Survival: 7 Early Deaths Before Intermission
35% of pulls end with Bebeshakur dying in Phase 1 (before 2:14). Most deaths here are to unknown raid damage, suggesting either poor positioning during Null Corona / Grasp of Emptiness or failing to top off between mechanics. Action: Use Death Strike more liberally in P1 when below 60% HP. Save AMS for the dangerous overlaps (Null Corona + Grasp of Emptiness around 20–25s intervals). The AMS cooldown easily allows multiple uses in P1.
4
Intermission Survival: 2 Deaths to Stellar Emission
Fights 15 and 16 both ended with Bebeshakur dying at 2:47 and 2:49 respectively — during the Intermission’s sustained AoE (Stellar Emission 2:22–2:52 + Silverstrike Barrage every 6s). Action: Use Anti-Magic Shell at the start of Stellar Emission and Icebound Fortitude if health drops below 40%. Death Strike through Silverstrike Barrage hits. Anti-Magic Zone should also be coordinated with the raid for this window.
5
Continue the Session Improvement Trend
The positive signal: DPS improved 51% from early to late pulls (36K to 55K avg), and the three longest pulls (19, 23, 25) all hit 59–63K DPS consistently. The rotation execution is improving. Action: Focus on translating this DPS consistency into survival consistency. The mechanics knowledge is being built — now the defensive CD usage needs to become muscle memory. Each pull should feel like a defensive CD rotation layered on top of the damage rotation, not an afterthought.
