Actionable Improvements

1. Talent Build Optimization (Biggest Impact)

The reference's talent build generates 16.5M more damage through three key talent choices: Splintering Ray (+6.2M passive damage alongside Ray of Frost), enhanced Frozen Orb talents (+6.8M from doubled Orb throughput), and Controlled Instincts interaction (+3.5M). Review talent choices against top-performing Frost Mages on Rotmire. The reference's build produces significantly more passive/proc damage while maintaining the same core rotation.

Impact: ~16.5M damage / ~34K DPS (accounts for ~57% of the total DPS gap)

2. Use Second Potion

Only one Potion of Recklessness was used (on pull). The reference used a second potion at 6:33, timed with a burst window in the final phase. Pre-plan the second pot for a Frozen Orb + Thermal Void window in the last 2 minutes. On an 8-minute fight, this is free damage with no downside.

Impact: ~3-5K DPS during the 30s potion window

3. Increase Flurry Casts / Brain Freeze Consumption

44 Flurry casts vs reference's 54 (adjusted for fight length: 5.4 CPM vs 7.1 CPM). Each Flurry triggers a Shatter combo that is the highest-damage window in Frost Mage's rotation. Ensure every Brain Freeze proc is consumed promptly. The gap in Flurry usage accounts for the -5.7M Shatter damage deficit. Review whether Brain Freeze procs are being held too long or dropped.

Impact: ~10 additional Flurry casts = ~1.2M more Flurry damage + ~2-3M more Shatter damage

4. Use Alter Time for Movement Optimization

The reference used Alter Time 6 times and Shimmer 9 times vs Baodabao's 0 Alter Time and 4 Shimmer. Alter Time is a powerful repositioning tool that lets you pre-position for Festering Vines, trigger it, then snap back to your original position to resume casting immediately. This is particularly valuable during the 7 Festering Vines windows across the fight.

Impact: Better uptime during movement windows, fewer Vines hits taken

5. More Counterspell Usage on Poison Burst

Only 1 Counterspell was used vs reference's 3. Poison Burst from Sporecaps is an interruptible cast (358K damage taken from 3 uninterrupted casts). Contribute more interrupts to reduce raid-wide damage and demonstrate mechanic awareness.

Impact: ~360K less raid damage taken, mechanic execution improvement
