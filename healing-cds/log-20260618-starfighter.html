Actionable Improvements

1 Use Die by the Sword on P2 Danger Windows
Impact: Survival (100% death rate)
Die by the Sword was never used across sampled fights. This is Arms Warrior's strongest personal defensive (parry + damage reduction) on a 4-minute cooldown. Plan to use it during the first major Void Expulsion + Simulacrum Backlash overlap in P2 (~3:30-4:00). With 9 deaths in P2 and 5 of those to Void Expulsion or Simulacrum Backlash, this single change could eliminate nearly half of all deaths.
2 Resolve Simulacrum Backlash Positioning (#1 killer, 4 deaths)
Impact: -4 deaths per session
Simulacrum Backlash killed Starfighter more than any other ability. This damage comes from the Rift Simulacrum when its Cosmic Barrier breaks or its cast completes. As a melee, use Spell Reflection specifically for this ability when the Simulacrum is casting, and use Defensive Stance preemptively if Spell Reflection is on cooldown. Also consider repositioning away from the Simulacrum during its channel rather than tunneling DPS on it.
3 Increase Execute Usage (+53% gap vs reference)
Impact: ~5-8K DPS increase
The reference player casts Execute at 10.9 CPM compared to Starfighter's 5.1 CPM. For Slayer Arms Warriors, Sudden Death procs give free Execute casts above 35% HP. These procs should be used immediately as they are the highest priority ability when available. Below 35% HP, Execute replaces Mortal Strike as the primary spender. Watching for the Sudden Death proc notification and pressing Execute immediately will close the single largest rotational gap.
4 Fill More GCDs — 38.3 CPM vs 51.7 Reference
Impact: 82 missed casts per fight
The 26% CPM gap is spread across all abilities, not just one. Mortal Strike (-22%), Execute (-53%), Overpower (-17%), and Colossus Smash (-21%) are all below reference. This suggests periods of inactivity where no buttons are being pressed. Possible causes: (1) GCD gaps during movement/mechanics — use Heroic Throw or Charge to maintain uptime while repositioning, (2) rage starvation — avoid overcapping rage during Colossus Smash windows, (3) death downtime — 82 missed casts includes time after death, so improving survival (items 1-2) will naturally raise this number.
5 Use Potions and Plan Cooldown Windows
Impact: ~3-5% DPS increase
No potion usage detected. The reference used 2 potions. Pre-pot on pull (Potion of Recklessness before the pull timer) and second pot at P2 start when Heroism + Power Infusion are available. Also align Avatar + Colossus Smash + Bladestorm with the Heroism window at 2:53 for maximum burst during the P2 opener. Currently using Avatar 5 times in a 6:05 fight, which is correct pacing, but aligning with external buffs would amplify each use.
