Actionable Improvements
1 Use Defensives Proactively in P1 -- 8 Deaths Before 2:14
40% of all deaths happen in P1. The causes are varied (Bursting Emptiness, Dark Hand, Ravenous Abyss, Echoing Darkness, Melee), which points to a general survivability deficit rather than one specific mechanic. Fury Warriors have limited defensives, but they need to be used preemptively:
Spell Reflection -- only used 2 times in 6:14 on fight 23. This should be used on every Void Expulsion and Grasp of Emptiness cast (every ~25s). That's 12+ potential uses per fight, not 2.
Enraged Regeneration -- not observed in cast data. This is a key survivability button.
Defensive Stance -- swap into it briefly during heavy damage windows (Grasp + Void Expulsion overlap), then swap back. Used correctly once (intermission) in fight 23.
Rallying Cry -- coordinate with healers for the most dangerous P1 overlaps.
2 Learn Cosmic Barrier and Voidstalker Sting Timing in P2
These two abilities account for 6 of 20 deaths combined. Cosmic Barrier (3 deaths at 3:38-3:43) is a raid-wide damage event in P2 -- Spell Reflection and Defensive Stance should be pre-cast for every Cosmic Barrier. Voidstalker Sting (3 deaths at 5:07-6:14) is a DoT that requires active healing or personal defensives. When Voidstalker Sting is applied, immediately use Enraged Regeneration or Impending Victory to sustain through the DoT. Call out for externals (BoSac, Ironbark) when both debuff and raid damage overlap.
3 Fix Fall Damage -- 3.9M Across 12 Pulls
Taking fall damage in 12 of 20 pulls (19 hits, 3.9M total) is a significant contributor to death. This likely comes from knockback mechanics (Interrupting Tremor, Grasp of Emptiness) pushing Starfighter off the platform or into dangerous positions. Fix: Position with your back to the center of the platform, not toward edges. Use Heroic Leap as a recovery tool immediately when knocked. Charge back to the boss to prevent falling off.
4 Dodge Bursting Emptiness Zones -- 2 P1 Deaths
Bursting Emptiness killed Starfighter at 1:33 (F11) and 1:55 (F21). This is a void zone that must be dodged. As a melee, Fury Warrior needs to be especially careful about void zone placement near the boss hitbox. When Bursting Emptiness is cast, move out immediately -- your uptime loss from dodging is far less than the DPS loss from dying 2+ minutes early. Bladestorm provides root/slow immunity if needed during the dodge.
5 Rotation is Functional -- Prioritize Survival Over Optimization
The cast profile shows correct Mountain Thane rotation: Thunder Clap as primary filler, Rampage at high frequency (71 casts in 6:14), Recklessness+Avatar paired and used on CD (~56s average gap via Anger Management), Odyn's Fury on cooldown (7 uses). The rotation is not the problem -- the 0% survival rate is. Every minute of additional survival time is worth more than any rotational optimization. Focus on the defensive usage items above before tuning DPS.
