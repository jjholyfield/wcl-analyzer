Actionable Items

1. Intermission Survival (Highest Priority)
Issue: 2 of 3 actionable deaths occurred during the Intermission phase (Orbiting Matter on fight 18, Silverstrike Barrage on fight 23). The Intermission is a pure survival check with Stellar Emission channel + Silverstrike Barrage -- no DPS required.

Fix: Pre-cast Ice Barrier before Silversunder Catastrophe ends. Save Greater Invisibility for Silverstrike Barrage if it targets nearby. Use Alter Time to snapshot safe position before the Intermission begins. Shimmer away from Orbiting Matter immediately. The Intermission only lasts 39 seconds -- burning all defensives here is fine because they'll be back up for P2.

Impact: Eliminates 2 of 3 actionable deaths. This is the single biggest survivability improvement available.
2. Eliminate Fall Damage
Issue: 343K fall damage across 2 hits on the deepest pull (fight 14). Mages have Slow Fall and Shimmer to prevent all fall damage.

Fix: Macro Slow Fall to a keybind and use it during Gravity Collapse knockups. Alternatively, Shimmer mid-air to reset momentum. This is free damage prevention.

Impact: 343K damage removed from the deepest pull. At 23.6K max HP, that's over 14x HP worth of avoidable damage over the fight.
3. Reduce Grasp of Emptiness Hits
Issue: 10 hits of Grasp of Emptiness at 48.9K average on fight 14. This is avoidable void zone damage.

Fix: Pre-position for void zones. Use Shimmer reactively when caught. With 11 Shimmers already used in fight 14, the charges are being spent -- the issue is timing, not usage rate. Prioritize Shimmer for Grasp over general repositioning.

Impact: Reducing from 10 hits to 3-4 saves ~300K damage intake.
4. Session Improvement Trend is Strong
Positive: DPS improved 21% from first half to second half of the session. Best pull (63.5K) came on fight 24 in the second half. This demonstrates clear learning and adaptation through the night. The fight is getting more comfortable with each pull.

Continue: The improvement trajectory is healthy. Focus on the survival items above -- once the raid consistently reaches P3 with all 20 alive, DPS will naturally rise from less healer stress and better CD planning.
5. P3 Execution (Future Focus)
Issue: P3 DPS averages only 11.7K with Snackznchill dying early in P3 on 3 of 4 pulls that reached it. The team hasn't had enough clean P3 practice to build muscle memory.

Fix: P3 (Devouring Cosmos) is an everything-overlaps phase. Save Alter Time + Greater Invisibility for P3 specifically. Consider holding a Frozen Orb + Icy Veins for P3 entry to push damage while the phase is fresh and raid is still alive.

Impact: This becomes relevant once the team is consistently reaching P3 with 18+ alive. For now, clean P1/Intermission transitions are the priority.
