How Vengeance DH Works

Vengeance DH is a builder-spender tank that generates Soul Fragments through Fracture and spends them with Soul Cleave and Spirit Bomb for damage and self-healing. Every Soul Fragment you consume heals you for 6% of damage taken in the last 5 seconds or 1% of max HP, whichever is higher.

The core loop: Fracture generates Soul Fragments (your main builder — spam it). Soul Cleave consumes fragments for damage and healing (primary spender). Spirit Bomb consumes 5+ fragments for AoE damage. Immolation Aura generates fragments passively and deals damage.

Demon Spikes is your active mitigation — 20% physical damage reduction with 2 charges. Keep at least one charge rolling at all times during active tanking. The goal is near-100% uptime during sustained damage.

Fiery Brand is your big defensive — 40% damage reduction for 12 seconds. With 2 charges (Down in Flames talent), you can stagger coverage. It also buffs your Fire damage.

Metamorphosis increases your max HP by 40% and armor by 200%. With the right talents, it has a very short effective cooldown — top players press it every 20-30 seconds, treating it as part of the rotation rather than a saved defensive.

Fel Devastation is a channeled damage ability that also provides healing. Use on cooldown.

The difference between good and great VDH is casting speed. Top players press Fracture 54/min, Soul Cleave 16/min. More Fractures = more fragments = more Soul Cleaves = more damage AND more self-healing. The spec rewards you for pressing buttons faster.
