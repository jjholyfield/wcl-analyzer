Smokinthisza's active DPS of ~168K across 226 seconds of uptime is solid relative to the progression context. When alive, rotational CPM is 33.9 vs 34.8 for the #3-ranked reference (a full kill) — only a 2.6% activity gap. Kill Shot vs Explosive Shot (Talent Difference)

The biggest rotation difference is talent-driven. The reference runs Kill Shot with Deathblow (which resets Kill Shot off cooldown via Aimed Shot crits), producing 15 Kill Shot casts for 5.04M damage (5.2% of their total). Smokinthisza runs Explosive Shot instead (5 casts, 0.77M damage, 1.8% of total). This single talent swap accounts for roughly 4.27M damage difference — the equivalent of ~4% DPS. Current guides recommend Kill Shot + Deathblow for raid single-target.

Steady Shot as Focus Filler

The reference casts Steady Shot 35 times (4.06 CPM) vs Smokinthisza's 4 casts (1.06 CPM). Steady Shot is the primary Focus regeneration tool — it generates 30 Focus per cast and is meant to be woven between Aimed Shot charges to prevent Focus starvation. Without sufficient Steady Shot weaving, Smokinthisza may be Focus-starved, leading to the slightly lower Rapid Fire CPM (using GCDs on nothing while waiting for Focus to regenerate naturally).

Actionable Items

1. Survive Silverstrike Barrage. Smokinthisza died at 2:31 to Silverstrike Barrage, removing 67 seconds of damage time from the fight. This single death reduced overall DPS from an estimated ~168K (active) to 130K (full fight) — a 23% DPS loss from death downtime. Silverstrike Barrage is a targeted mechanic that requires either (a) positioning away from the barrage path, (b) a defensive cooldown (Survival of the Fittest, Exhilaration, or Aspect of the Turtle), or (c) a healthstone/health potion before the hit. With 601K total damage from only 2 hits, this is a one-shot mechanic at lower ilvl. Saving Survival of the Fittest for this mechanic instead of using it in the opener would be the highest-impact change.
2. Swap Explosive Shot for Kill Shot + Deathblow. This is a talent change, not a rotation change. The reference deals 5.04M damage from Kill Shot (5.2% of total) with 15 casts across the fight thanks to Deathblow (Kill Shot resets from Aimed Shot crits). Smokinthisza's Explosive Shot contributed only 0.77M (1.8%). Current spec guides recommend Kill Shot + Deathblow for raid single-target. This swap alone would add an estimated 4–5% DPS without any rotation complexity increase, since Kill Shot is a proc-based ability that simply lights up when available.
3. Weave more Steady Shot for Focus management. Smokinthisza cast Steady Shot 4 times (1.06 CPM) vs the reference's 35 times (4.06 CPM). Steady Shot generates 30 Focus per cast and is the primary tool to prevent Focus starvation between Aimed Shot windows. Without sufficient Steady Shot weaving, the player may be sitting at low Focus waiting for natural regen, which creates the small gaps visible in the rotation. Each Steady Shot also deals damage (360K total for 4 casts — the reference did 1.07M from 35 casts). Aim for approximately 1 Steady Shot between every 2–3 Aimed Shot casts when Rapid Fire and Arcane Shot (with Precise Shots) are unavailable.
4. Use Rapid Fire on cooldown more aggressively. Rapid Fire CPM is 3.18 vs 3.83 (−17%). Over the active play time, this translates to roughly 2–3 missed Rapid Fire casts. Rapid Fire is a high-priority ability that generates Precise Shots, maintains Bulletstorm stacks, and deals strong damage (10.6% of Smokinthisza's total). It should be pressed on cooldown unless doing so would overwrite existing Bulletstorm stacks. Each missed Rapid Fire also means one fewer Precise Shots proc, cascading into slightly fewer buffed Arcane Shots.
5. Use trinket (Algeth'ar Puzzle) on cooldown. Smokinthisza used the trinket 1 time vs the reference's 4 times. On-use trinkets should be used on cooldown, ideally aligned with Trueshot windows. The reference used it 4 times in an 8:37 fight; Smokinthisza's single use in 4:53 suggests it was forgotten after the battle-rez. Stack trinket + Trueshot for maximum burst value.

Aimed Shot and Arcane Shot CPM: When alive, Aimed Shot at 9.28 CPM is 95% of the reference rate (9.76), and Arcane Shot at 11.67 CPM slightly exceeds the reference (11.50). These are the two highest-damage abilities and they are being used at near-optimal rates.

Active CPM is strong: 33.9 total CPM when alive vs 34.8 for the reference — only a 2.6% gap. There is very little true dead time (idle GCDs) in the rotation.

Post-rez response: Trueshot was used immediately after being battle-rezzed at 3:01, stacking with Heroism at 2:59. This is the correct play to maximize damage contribution after a rez.

Best-pull DPS is excellent: 335K DPS on Fight 8 and 319K DPS on Fight 17 (both near-kills) show strong burst and add-phase execution when alive for the full pull. At 269 ilvl, this is competitive performance relative to the kill requirements.

Summary

Smokinthisza's primary progression bottleneck is survivability, not rotation. When alive, CPM and ability priority are within 3–5% of the reference despite a 21-ilvl gear gap and 6 talent differences. The two actionable changes with the highest DPS impact are: (1) not dying to Silverstrike Barrage (saves 67s of damage time, ~23% effective DPS increase), and (2) swapping Explosive Shot for Kill Shot + Deathblow (+4–5% DPS from talent change alone). Secondary improvements include weaving more Steady Shot for Focus management and using Rapid Fire on cooldown more consistently.
