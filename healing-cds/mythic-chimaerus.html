Logs analyzed + condensed findings
Logs (5 teams, HPal/RSham/MW/PEvo, 348–384s kills):
Kazzak (EU) — 348s

Findings:
Consume 1 (~1:05) is the tightest consensus window — 5/5 teams stack Rewind, Ascendance, Restoral, SLT.
Consume 2 (~2:17) covered by rotating 2min CDs (AW, AM, Yu'lon, DF, VE).
SLT goes either Consume 1 or Consume 2 (3min CD, can't do both) — we put it at Consume 1.
Intermission (~2:47–4:03) is sustained, not burst — CC and DF cover, save 3min CDs.
Kill push (~5:14) brings all 3min CDs back: Asc, SLT, Rewind, Restoral.
4/5 MWs run Restoral over Revival. 0/5 PEvos run Zephyr. 0/5 RShams run HTT.
