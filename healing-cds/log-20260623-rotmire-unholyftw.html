Meanwhile, Outbreak was cast 19 times (reference: 0) and Death Coil was cast at 8.2 CPM vs reference's 6.5. In Midnight Unholy, Outbreak only needs to be used to apply or refresh plagues when they fall off — 19 casts means reapplying plagues far too frequently. Each Outbreak displaces a Scourge Strike that would deal damage and consume ghoul stacks. During Forbidden Knowledge windows, prioritize converting Runic Power into Graveyard (AoE) or Epidemic rather than single-target Death Coils.
Impact: High — Scourge Strike is the primary damage spender. Recovering even 20 of those ~40 lost casts would be a meaningful DPS increase.
2 Reduce Soul Reaper Usage Outside Dark Transformation
22 Soul Reaper casts vs reference's 14. Soul Reaper's full value comes during Dark Transformation (Commander of the Dead damage amp) and when consuming Reaping procs. Using Soul Reaper outside DT windows is a lower-value GCD that displaces Scourge Strikes. With 11 DT windows at 15 seconds each, that is ~165 seconds of DT uptime. 14 Soul Reapers (the reference's count) averages roughly 1.3 per DT window, which is the correct usage: once during DT, plus the free Reaping proc near the end. Audit Soul Reaper casts — if it is being used outside DT windows on targets above 35% health, those GCDs should be Scourge Strikes instead.
Impact: Medium — ~8 excess Soul Reapers could be converted to higher-value Scourge Strikes.
3 Tighten Dark Transformation and Army of the Dead Alignment
The player uses Dark Transformation at 0:03 (essentially pre-pull) while the reference delays to 0:32 to build ghoul stacks first. The Midnight Unholy opener recommends building 6-8 Lesser Ghoul stacks with Festering Strike before activating Dark Transformation + Army of the Dead together. Starting DT at 0:03 means the entire burst window happens before the Army ghouls and Magus of the Dead are fully ramped. The reference's 0:32 opener shows the correct pattern: 2x Festering Strike to build stacks, then Army + DT simultaneously for maximum Commander of the Dead value.
Impact: Medium — Proper opener sequencing amplifies the first burst window, which benefits from Time Warp.
4 Use More Graveyard and Epidemic During Forbidden Knowledge Windows
The reference casts Graveyard at 2.2 CPM and Epidemic at 1.4 CPM, while the player is at 1.4 and 0.6 respectively. During Forbidden Knowledge windows (triggered by Army of the Dead), Death Coil transforms into Necrotic Coil and Epidemic transforms into Graveyard. When adds are present (which is most of the Rotmire fight), using Graveyard/Epidemic on 3+ targets yields more total damage than single-target Necrotic Coil/Death Coil. The player's higher Death Coil usage (8.2 vs 6.5 CPM) and lower Graveyard/Epidemic suggests defaulting to single-target Runic Power spending even when add targets are available.
Impact: Medium — With 42.7% of damage going to adds, optimizing AoE Runic Power spending during Forbidden Knowledge windows has compounding value.
5 Eliminate Bursting Shroom Damage Through Positioning
3 Bursting Shroom hits at 232K average damage each. Bursting Shroom is the proximity explosion when a Sporecap add dies. As a melee DPS, positioning near dying adds is sometimes unavoidable, but the reference data shows this damage can be minimized.
