Actionable Items

1. Stop Dying to Void Remnants (9 deaths, 33% of all deaths)
Void Remnants is a ground-targeted void pool that persists. It hits for 233K unmitigated per tick -- effectively a one-shot. Six of these deaths happen in P1 where healing demand is manageable. This is the highest-impact improvement available: staying alive through P1 means reaching the phases where the team needs healing. Use Travel Form or Wild Charge for faster repositioning. The fix is pure awareness -- these are avoidable pools that should never hit a ranged healer.
2. Position Away from Rift Simulacrums in P2 (5 Backlash deaths)
All 5 Simulacrum Backlash deaths occur between 4:42-5:13 in P2 when Rift Simulacrums are active. This is proximity AoE. As a ranged healer, Silencio should be maintaining maximum distance from these adds. When they die (Backlash), the burst can overlap with Voidstalker Sting or Void Remnants for a lethal combo. Pre-Barkskin when Simulacrums are near death.
3. Use Barkskin More Consistently (1 min CD)
F23 had only 1 Barkskin usage in 5:18 alive, while F28 had 3 uses in 5:13 alive. On a 1-minute cooldown, Barkskin should be used 5+ times per 5-minute pull. Key moments: before Void Expulsion, when targeted by Voidstalker Sting, during Intermission (Stellar Emission), and before Simulacrum Backlash windows. The F28 improvement is promising -- keep that discipline.
4. Delay Tranquility #1 to Intermission or P2
Both F23 and F28 show Tranquility cast at 0:13 and 0:25 respectively -- within the first 30 seconds of P1 when damage is minimal. On F23, Tranquility had 81% overheal overall. The Intermission (Stellar Emission at 2:22-2:52) is a sustained AoE healing check where Tranquility's channel + HoT extension would be far more impactful. Alternatively, saving it for early P2 at 2:53+ where new damage abilities begin would improve both HPS and mana efficiency.
5. Cast DPS Filler During Downtime (Currently 0 Wrath/Moonfire/Sunfire)
Zero damage filler spells were cast across the entire 27-pull session. All ~4K DPS comes from passive procs (Symbiotic Relationship, trinkets). During P1 downtime between damage events, Silencio should be casting Wrath and maintaining Sunfire/Moonfire DoTs on adds. Even at 3-4K DPS from filler, that is meaningful on prog where every bit of DPS helps meet DPS checks. This is especially valuable in P1 where healing demand is lowest.
6. Positive: Convoke Discipline is Excellent
4-5 Convoke uses per long pull is exactly right for a 2-minute cooldown. Silencio is keeping Convoke on CD consistently across all long pulls. Similarly, Innervate usage at 2 per pull is optimal. This CD discipline should be maintained and extended to Barkskin (which needs the same on-CD mentality).
