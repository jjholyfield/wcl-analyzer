Actionable Improvements

Ranked by expected impact. For progression, survival and mechanics take priority over rotation optimization.

1. Use Astral Shift aggressively on cooldown during damage events
Impact: Potentially prevent 5-8 deaths per session
Astral Shift (40% DR, 12s duration, 1.5min CD) was used only 1 time in a 6:44 pull. In a fight where Invincibale dies every pull, this defensive needs to be used on cooldown. Pre-plan usage:
  • P1 (0:25-0:30): Cover the first Void Expulsion / Silverstrike Arrow window
  • P2 (3:00-3:15): Cover early P2 damage overlap (Cosmic Barrier + Voidstalker Sting)
  • P2 (4:30-4:45): Cover late P2 damage spike
  • P3 (5:15+): Cover Cosmic Radiation overlap
4 uses minimum per pull. This single change has the highest survival impact of any recommendation.
2. Fix Cosmic Barrier positioning (4 deaths, top identified killer)
Impact: Prevent 4 deaths per session
Cosmic Barrier killed Invincibale in fights 6, 11, 17, and 25 (all in P2, avg time 4:21). This is a positional mechanic. 47 hits of Cosmic Barrier damage in fight 25 alone (1.1M total) indicates sustained contact with the barrier rather than brief accidental clips.
  • Use Gust of Wind (knockback/gap closer) to create distance from barrier when it spawns
  • Pre-position away from barrier spawn locations during Rift Slash cast
  • Use Spiritwalker's Grace before barrier appears to maintain DPS while repositioning
3. Avoid early P1 deaths (6 deaths before 1:42)
Impact: Recover 8 full-pull DPS contributions (currently 0 DPS on these pulls)
6 of 20 pulls had Invincibale die in P1 (before 2:14), producing 0 effective DPS. Death causes include Silverstrike Arrow (2), Void Remnants (2), Echoing Darkness (1), and Unknown (1).
  • Silverstrike Arrow: Dodge the projectile -- use Gust of Wind or sidestep. 2 deaths to this in early pulls.
  • Void Remnants: Avoid standing in void zones left by Sentinel deaths. Clear of corpse locations.
  • Echoing Darkness: Vorelus cast -- move out of the targeted area immediately.
  • Consider using Astral Shift pre-emptively at 0:25-0:30 when damage spikes occur in P1.
4. Simulacrum Backlash awareness in P2 (2 deaths + high damage)
Impact: Prevent 2 deaths + reduce 2.3M avoidable damage per pull
Simulacrum Backlash is the Rift Simulacrum's punishment mechanic. 82 hits for 2.3M damage in fight 25 suggests prolonged exposure. When the Rift Simulacrum begins casting Empowering Darkness or Rift Slash, create distance. Astral Shift should be saved for overlaps between Backlash and other mechanics (Cosmic Barrier, Voidstalker Sting).
5. Falling damage suggests knockback awareness gap (371K damage, 2 hits)
Impact: Prevent instant death from environmental damage
Two instances of falling damage (max hit: 185K) in fight 25 indicate getting knocked off a platform or into a pit. This is almost certainly from Gravity Collapse (3 hits, 467K) or a similar knockback mechanic. Position with back to safe ground, or use Gust of Wind to recover after a knockback.

Summary Verdict

The performance gap on this fight is entirely survival-based. When Invincibale is alive, the DPS output is competitive (rank 1 in two pulls, top-3 in four more). The rotation is solid -- Stormkeeper/Ascendance alignment is good, Flame Shock multi-dotting is active, and filler priority is correct.

The critical issue is dying in every single pull with an average survival of just 3:24 in an 8+ minute fight. Cosmic Barrier positioning (4 deaths), Simulacrum Backlash awareness (2 deaths + massive damage), and early P1 deaths (6 pulls with 0 DPS) are the three highest-impact areas.

The single highest-impact change is using Astral Shift on cooldown (1 use in 6:44 vs 4 possible). Forty percent damage reduction for 12 seconds every 90 seconds would directly prevent deaths to several of the top killing abilities.

Positive sign: DPS improved 12.2% across the session and the final pull was the best of the night (64K DPS, rank 1 in raid). The learning curve is trending upward.
