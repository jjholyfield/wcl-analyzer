Actionable Improvements

Priority-ordered improvements. Fixing items #1 and #2 alone would close the majority of the execution gap.

#1 — Use Chain Lightning as default filler whenever 2+ targets exist
Invincibale casts Chain Lightning 32 times (4.0 CPM) vs reference's 58 (8.5 CPM). This 53% deficit is the single largest source of lost damage: -7.3M Chain Lightning Overload damage and -3.3M direct Chain Lightning damage (10.6M combined, 44% of the total gap). Rotmire has adds up for significant portions of the fight via Awaken Fungi. Every time there are 2+ targets, Chain Lightning should replace Lightning Bolt. This also generates more Maelstrom for Earthquake and more Overload procs. Lightning Bolt should only be cast during pure single-target windows (boss alone, no adds).
#2 — Tighten Elemental Blast usage on cooldown
Invincibale casts Elemental Blast 32 times (4.0 CPM) vs reference's 37 (5.4 CPM) — a 26% deficit. EB is a core rotational ability that should be pressed on cooldown. Over the fight, this represents roughly 5 missed Elemental Blast casts (adjusting for fight length), which translates to ~1.6M lost damage (EB + EB Overload). EB also buffs secondary stats — delayed EBs mean lower sustained stats throughout the fight.
#3 — Reduce Ghost Wolf usage; use Spiritwalker's Grace more aggressively
Invincibale cast Ghost Wolf 6 times (reference: 0). Two of the worst casting gaps (6.7s and 4.5s) involved Ghost Wolf, representing ~11s of zero DPS from movement alone. Spiritwalker's Grace was cast 6 times but is clearly not covering all movement. On Rotmire, Festering Vines is the primary movement mechanic. Plan SWG usage around Vines, and for other movement use instant casts (Lava Burst w/ Nature's Swiftness, Flame Shock refreshes, Tempest) instead of dropping into Ghost Wolf.
#4 — Reduce Earthquake over-casting
Invincibale casts Earthquake 18 times (2.3 CPM) vs reference's 10 (1.5 CPM) — 55% more. While Earthquake is the correct Maelstrom spender during AoE, over-casting it during single-target windows wastes Maelstrom that could be saved for when adds spawn. The reference is more selective with EQ usage, concentrating it during actual add phases. Be disciplined about when EQ is worth casting vs holding Maelstrom.
#5 — Use a DPS potion, not just a health potion
No Tempered Potion or equivalent DPS potion was used. The Silvermoon Health Potion at 6:42 was a panic survival move, not a planned DPS cooldown. A pre-pot at 0:00 stacked with Time Warp + PI + Ascendance, plus a second potion during a later burst window, would add an estimated 2-4% total DPS.
