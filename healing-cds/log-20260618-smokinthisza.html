Actionable Improvements

1. Plan defensives for Voidstalker Sting (3 deaths)
The problem: Voidstalker Sting is a targeted DoT applied approximately every 25 seconds in P2. It killed Smokinthisza in fights 14 (3:27), 16 (4:58), and 23 (6:02). The DoT damage alone is survivable, but it becomes lethal when combined with other damage sources (Void Expulsion, Cosmic Barrier ticks).

The fix: When targeted by Voidstalker Sting, immediately use Survival of the Fittest (3-min CD, 40% DR) or Exhilaration (2-min CD, 30% heal) to counteract the DoT. If both are on cooldown, use Aspect of the Turtle. As a ranged class, also move away from other raid damage to reduce overlap. Establish a mental checklist: "Sting applied = press defensive."

Impact: Eliminating these 3 deaths would mean surviving to wipe in 4/13 pulls instead of 1/13, and more time spent doing DPS in P2/P3.
2. Pre-plan a defensive for P3 Cosmic Radiation transition (3 deaths at 5:06-5:13)
The problem: Fights 18, 19, and 20 all show death at 5:06-5:13 — exactly when P3 starts with Cosmic Radiation. This is the most dangerous transition in the fight: massive raid-wide AoE plus new enemies spawning simultaneously.

The fix: At ~5:00, ensure either Survival of the Fittest or Aspect of the Turtle is available and pre-press it. On fight 27 (the best pull), the defensive usage shows Survival at 4:50 and Turtle at 5:47 — good defensive pacing. The earlier pulls did not have this discipline. Consider holding Survival of the Fittest specifically for the 5:09 window if the previous usage was at ~3:00.

Impact: Later pulls (F23, F27, F28) show survival through this transition, so the learning is already happening. Lock it in as a planned CD.
3. Stabilize P2 DPS variance (80K-150K range)
The problem: P2 DPS ranges from 80.7K (F8) to 150.1K (F19) — an 86% spread. The rotational CPM is consistent (~31-32 CPM), so the variance is not from pressing fewer buttons. It is likely from target selection inefficiency and movement waste during Rift Slash and Call of the Void mechanics.

The fix: During P2, prioritize maintaining uptime on the Rift Simulacrum (kill target) while using Multi-Shot to cleave onto Undying Voidspawn. Use Aspect of the Cheetah for repositioning during Rift Slash rather than losing GCDs to running. Pre-position for Call of the Void (move early, not reactively).

Impact: Bringing the low-end P2 pulls up from 80-90K to 120K would add ~4M total damage across P2, which translates to roughly 2-3% more boss damage per pull.
4. Continue the P3 learning curve
The trajectory: P3 entry went from instant death (F18-F20) to surviving 30-56 seconds (F23, F27, F28) with 54-93K DPS. Fight 27 is the standout — 92.5K P3 DPS with proper defensive rotation (SotF at 5:38, Turtle at 5:47, Exhilaration at 5:47).

Next step: P3 is an overlap phase with P1 and P2 mechanics returning simultaneously. The key is spatial awareness — Vorelus returns with Echoing Darkness + Ravenous Abyss, and Rift Simulacrum spawns again. Maintain max range, use Disengage proactively, and save one defensive for unexpected overlaps.
5. Rotation execution is not the bottleneck
What is already good: 31-32 CPM across all phases is consistent and clean. Trueshot alignment with PI + Lust + Ebon Might is correct. Black Arrow / Aimed Shot / Arcane Shot weaving follows the Dark Ranger priority. Steady Shot usage is minimal (only as focus dump), indicating good focus management. The improvement path is entirely survival and positioning, not rotation changes.

Summary Verdict

Smokinthisza's performance gap on Mythic Crown is survival-based, not rotation-based. The 8% survival rate (1/13 pulls) is the primary issue. Rotational execution is clean — consistent CPM, correct CD alignment, proper Dark Ranger weaving.

The night shows clear learning: early pulls died at 3:27-3:35 in P2; late pulls survived to 5:39-6:02 in P3. Death causes shifted from varied P2 mechanics to concentrated P3 overlaps. This is the expected trajectory for a prog night — each death teaches something.

Priority for next session: Pre-planned defensive CDs for Voidstalker Sting and the P3 Cosmic Radiation transition. If those 6 deaths are eliminated, survival rate jumps from 8% to 54% and P3 practice time doubles. That is the fastest path to a kill.
