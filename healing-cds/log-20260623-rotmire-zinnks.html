Augmentation Evoker DPS Context

Understanding Aug Evoker DPS Numbers
Augmentation Evoker is a support DPS spec. Their primary contribution is buffing allies via Ebon Might (8% primary stat to all DPS) and Prescience (crit chance). The DPS number shown reflects attributed damage from these buffs plus personal damage. A 23.9% DPS gap to the #5 ranked Aug does not mean 23.9% less button-pressing -- it largely reflects the damage output of the raid team being buffed. A top-ranked Aug in a faster kill with higher-performing DPS players will naturally show higher attributed DPS. The CPM and mechanic execution comparison below is far more meaningful for evaluating Zinnks's individual play.

Actionable Improvements

1 Tighten first Ebon Might refresh (48.7s gap in opener)
First Ebon Might was cast at 0:03 but not refreshed until 0:52 -- a 48.7s gap. Ebon Might lasts 30s, meaning the raid had no Ebon Might buff from ~0:33 to 0:52. The reference refreshed at 0:32. Every subsequent refresh was properly timed (28-31s intervals). Fix: refresh Ebon Might at ~27-28s into the fight, before the first Festering Vines at 0:43.
Impact: ~20 seconds of additional Ebon Might uptime during the opener burst window where raid DPS is highest.
2 Recover the 8th Breath of Eons cast
7 Breath of Eons casts vs 8 from the reference. The 99s interval between casts 5 and 6 (4:56 to 6:35) is where the lost cast cascaded from. Breath of Eons CD is ~65s. If cast 6 had been used at ~6:01 instead of 6:35, there would have been room for cast 8 before the boss died. Fix: prioritize Breath of Eons as soon as it comes off cooldown -- don't delay for positioning.
Impact: One additional Breath of Eons burst window -- the highest single-event damage contribution for Aug Evoker.
3 Maintain CPS during Bloom #3 (6:27-6:42)
0.40 CPS during Bloom #3 vs 0.80 from reference -- the largest single mechanic window gap. Only 6 casts in this 15s window. The 3.7s gap from Hover to Ebon Might (6:28-6:32) accounts for much of the lost time. Defensive usage (Obsidian Scales + Zephyr) is necessary here but can overlap with casting. Fix: pre-cast Obsidian Scales and Zephyr 1-2 GCDs earlier to maintain rotational flow through the Bloom.
Impact: ~6 additional casts during a critical damage window where the raid needs maximum throughput.
4 Reduce post-Breath of Eons dead time
Three of the largest cast gaps (4.9s, 3.6s, 3.2s) follow Breath of Eons casts. Breath of Eons involves the Evoker flying to a target location, which creates natural travel time. However, the reference shows smaller post-Breath gaps (3.7s avg vs 3.9s). Fix: plan Breath of Eons flight path to end near the boss/adds, reducing re-engagement time. Start the next rotational cast (Eruption or Prescience) immediately upon landing.
Impact: ~3-4 seconds recovered across 7 Breath of Eons casts -- approximately 2-3 additional rotational casts.
5 Increase Hover usage for movement optimization
17 Hover casts vs 22 from reference. Hover allows casting while moving for Augmentation Evokers. The reference used Hover 29% more frequently, particularly during add spawn transitions and Festering Vines movement. More liberal Hover usage enables maintaining DPS during mechanics that would otherwise force movement gaps. Fix: use Hover proactively when movement is imminent, not reactively after being forced to move.
Impact: 5 additional Hovers would recover movement-related CPS drops in several mechanic windows.

Overall Assessment

The 23.9% DPS gap is primarily raid-team-driven, not execution-driven

Zinnks's cast profile is remarkably close to the top-5 reference. Rotational CPM is 43.1 vs 42.4 (+1.7%), total dead time is actually lower (97.5s vs 115.1s), and ability usage counts are nearly identical across every major spell. The DPS delta is fundamentally an Augmentation Evoker artifact: Aug DPS attribution depends on how much damage the buffed allies deal. A top-ranked Aug in a faster kill with higher-performing DPS players will show dramatically higher attributed DPS even with identical play.

What Zinnks does well:

  • Eruption and Living Flame CPM match or exceed reference
  • Ebon Might refresh cadence is tight after the opener (28-31s intervals)
  • Defensive timing is excellent -- Zephyr on every Bloom, Obsidian Scales properly aligned
  • Survived the kill cleanly with zero deaths across all 3 pulls
  • Movement handling during Festering Vines is strong (several windows exceed reference)
  • Kill fight was the best DPS performance of the session

Areas for improvement (execution):

  • First Ebon Might refresh 20s late in opener
  • One missing Breath of Eons cast due to delayed usage mid-fight
  • Bloom #3 CPS drop from defensive setup timing
  • Could use Hover more aggressively (17 vs 22 casts)
