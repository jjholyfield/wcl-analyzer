Hand of Gul'dan Gap (−19%)

Hand of Gul'dan is the primary Soul Shard spender and demon summoner — it defines the Demonology rotation. Sonìc averaged 13.1 casts/min vs Palelock's 16.2, a deficit of 3.1 casts per minute. Over 6 minutes, that is roughly 19 missed HoG casts, each spawning Wild Imps that contribute to Fel Firebolt damage and Implosion value. This cascading effect (fewer HoG → fewer imps → less Implosion damage → less pet damage overall) is the single largest rotational DPS loss.

Builder Throughput (SB/DB −12%)

Lower builder CPM directly causes lower HoG frequency. The combined gap suggests approximately 2-3 seconds of dead time per minute where no GCD is being pressed — likely movement-related downtime or delayed reactions after mechanic handling.

Actionable Improvements

1. Stay alive — use Dark Pact before every Vines and Bloom (estimated +15-20% effective DPS)
Sonìc died in all 20 pulls. The kill death at 6:07 removed 111 seconds of fight time — that is 111 seconds of Demonic Tyrant windows, imp generation, and execute-phase damage. Dark Pact was used only 3 times vs Palelock's 6. The fix: use Dark Pact proactively at 45s intervals, pre-shield before Festering Vines (every ~68s) and Fungal Bloom (every ~136s). Also use Demonic Circle for instant Vines escape. Survival alone would recover roughly 15-20% of the current effective DPS gap.
2. Increase Hand of Gul'dan frequency (+19% or ~19 casts over the fight)
HoG is at 13.1 CPM vs Palelock's 16.2 — a 19% deficit. Each missed HoG is a missed set of Wild Imps, which cascades into less Implosion damage, less pet melee, and fewer Fel Firebolt volleys. This is the largest rotational DPS loss. The fix: ensure Shadow Bolt and Demonbolt are being cast with minimal downtime to generate shards, and spend at 3 shards consistently rather than capping.
3. Increase GCD usage — reduce dead time between casts (estimated +10% CPM)
Total CPM is 45.8 vs Palelock's 53.5 (14.4% gap). The builder spells (Shadow Bolt at -12%, Demonbolt at -12%) are both below reference rates by the same margin, suggesting systematic GCD gaps of 2-3 seconds per minute rather than a single ability being wrong. The fix: ABC (Always Be Casting) discipline — pre-plan movement, use instant casts during forced movement, and minimize reaction time between GCDs. Consider Demonic Circle placement for instant repositioning during Vines rather than running.
4. Gear upgrades (272 → 290+ ilvl, estimated +10-12% raw DPS)
Sonìc's 290.5 ilvl is 18.4 points below Palelock (290.8). Note that Senshisama (#1 ranked) achieved 186K DPS at only 258.6 ilvl, so gear is not the whole story — but it is a meaningful contributor. Each ilvl point is roughly 0.5-0.7% DPS for a caster. Prioritize weapon (currently 298 — good), trinkets, and tier pieces.
5. Coordinate Power Infusion with a Priest teammate
Sonìc received zero PI casts. Palelock received 4 (at pull, 2:05, 4:17, 6:22). PI provides 25% haste for 15 seconds — 4 uses over a fight adds roughly 3-5% total DPS. This is a team coordination issue, not a personal play issue, but it is easy to fix by requesting PI from a Priest in the raid.

Gap Summary

The 26.4% DPS gap (132.9K vs 180.5K) breaks down approximately as: ~10-12% gear (18.4 ilvl deficit), ~8-10% death (111s lost uptime, execute phase missed), ~5-8% external buffs (0 PI vs 4 PI, fewer Ebon Might), ~5-7% rotational (HoG frequency, builder throughput, GCD gaps). These overlap — a dead player also stops generating pet damage, and lower ilvl means each GCD does less damage. The single highest-impact change is surviving: staying alive through the full fight would, by itself, likely bring DPS above 120K and make the rotational improvements compounding rather than truncated by death.
