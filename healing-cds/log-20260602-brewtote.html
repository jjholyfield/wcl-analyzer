How Restoration Shaman Works

Restoration Shaman heals through Chain Heal bounces, Riptide HoTs, and totem management. Your mastery makes heals more effective on lower-health targets, so you want to focus healing on the people who need it most.

Core loop: Keep Riptide on cooldown — it’s your foundation spell and enables Chain Heal’s Flow of the Tides bonus. Cast Chain Heal on targets that have Riptide for the bounce bonus. Use Unleash Life before your biggest heal for the buff. Deploy Healing Rain when the group is stacked.

Major CDs: Ascendance (burst throughput — Chain Heal hits extra targets), Spirit Link Totem (HP equalization + 15% DR), Nature’s Swiftness (instant free cast every 60s).

Between heals, cast Flame Shock and Lava Burst for DPS contribution — RSham is one of the better healer DPS specs.

How This Is Affecting Your Healing

Chain Heal at 67% of top (11.2/min vs 16.8/min). Chain Heal is your primary throughput spell. 5.6 fewer Chain Heals per minute is a massive HPS gap. This is the single biggest reason for the 72% HPS relative to #1. On raid-wide damage like LBV, Chain Heal bounces are how RSham keeps up.
Healing Wave at 242% of top (2.9/min vs 1.2/min). You’re casting Healing Wave nearly 3x as often as top players. Healing Wave is a single-target triage heal. Top Resto Shamans barely touch it because Chain Heal covers more targets per GCD. Every Healing Wave cast is a Chain Heal that didn’t happen.
21% dead time — highest among healers. One in five GCDs has no heal or DPS spell being cast. If nobody needs healing, that GCD should be Flame Shock or Lava Burst. If people need healing, it should be Chain Heal.

What You Need to Change

1
More Chain Heal, less Healing Wave — target 16+/min Chain Heal
Chain Heal is at 11.2/min and needs to be 16+. Healing Wave is at 2.9/min vs top’s 1.2/min. The fix is the same problem: replace Healing Wave casts with Chain Heal casts. Healing Wave is a single-target emergency heal. On raid-wide damage like LBV, Chain Heal is the primary throughput tool — it heals more people per GCD and benefits from Riptide bounces.
2
Fill GCDs — reduce 21% dead time
21% dead time means roughly 1 in 5 GCDs is empty. If nobody needs healing, cast Flame Shock and Lava Burst for DPS. If people need healing, Chain Heal. There should never be a GCD where nothing is being cast.
3
Use health potion — 0/26 pulls
Health potion is on a separate cooldown from your pre-pot. It’s a free survivability button that costs nothing. Use it when you take damage.
