Recurring Mechanic: Bursting Emptiness (2 Actionable Deaths)

Both actionable P1 deaths (fights 8 and 13) were caused by Bursting Emptiness at ~1:00 into the fight. This is the Void Droplet explosion mechanic in P1 -- when a Droplet is killed near players or reaches its target, it deals heavy AoE damage. Orichamaru was hit by this twice as one of the first 2 deaths in the raid. Recommendation: Use Dark Pact proactively when Droplets are about to expire, and maintain distance from Droplet kill locations during P1.

Actionable Improvements

1. Survive Bursting Emptiness in P1 (2 actionable deaths)

Issue: 2 of 3 actionable deaths are to Bursting Emptiness at ~1:00 into the fight (fights 8 and 13). This is the Void Droplet explosion in P1.
Impact: Both deaths ended any chance of a quality pull -- dying at 1:00 means zero contribution to the attempt.
Fix: Pre-position away from Droplet kill zones. Use Dark Pact proactively when Droplets are about to expire (~every 30s in P1). Dark Pact is a 1-minute CD so it should be available for every Droplet wave.

2. Use Defensives More Aggressively (1 Dark Pact in 7:12)

Issue: On the deepest pull (F14, 7:12), only 1 Dark Pact (at 4:44) and 1 Unending Resolve (at 5:38) were used. Dark Pact is a 1-minute CD that provides a ~35% max HP shield.
Impact: In a 7-minute fight, Dark Pact should be used 5-6 times. That is 4-5 missed defensive opportunities that could prevent deaths or reduce healer load in the deep progression windows where healing is stretched thin.
Fix: Map Dark Pact to predictable damage events: P1 Void Expulsion casts, Intermission Stellar Emission/Silverstrike Barrage, P2 Simulacrum Backlash waves, P3 Gravity Collapse overlaps. Use Unending Resolve for the biggest spikes (Silverstrike Barrage in Intermission, first P3 Devouring Cosmos overlap).

3. Survive Intermission Silverstrike Barrage (1 actionable death)

Issue: Fight 26 death to Silverstrike Barrage at 2:30 (during Intermission). This was the FIRST death in the raid.
Impact: Dying first in Intermission is highly disruptive -- the entire Intermission is a pure survival check with no DPS required. Being the first player to die here means something went wrong with positioning or defensive timing.
Fix: Commit Unending Resolve to every Intermission (it will be back for P3). Stack Dark Pact on top during Silverstrike Barrage timing. Stay in the center of the raid for maximum healer coverage during the channel.

4. Reduce DPS Variance (33% gap between best and worst pulls)

Issue: Best 3 pulls average 66.1K, worst 3 average 49.8K -- a 16.3K gap.
Impact: Inconsistent DPS means the raid cannot count on a stable damage contribution pull-over-pull.
Fix: Prioritize maintaining the Tyrant rotation cadence even during movement phases. Use Soulburn for instant Demonic Circle when forced to move during Tyrant setup windows. Pre-plan Hand of Gul'dan and Dreadstalker casts around known mechanic timers so the Tyrant setup completes before movement is required.

5. Consider Adding Versatility (Currently 0)

Issue: 0 Versatility provides zero passive damage reduction. The primary reference (Pymowl) runs 279 Vers.
Impact: Even small amounts of Vers (200+) provide meaningful passive damage reduction during P3 when raid damage ramps with Cosmic Radiation + overlapping mechanics. This is a pure survivability gain.
Fix: Gearing priority -- when upgrading ring/neck/cloak slots, consider pieces with some Versatility. This is not urgent but would compound with better defensive usage to improve deep pull survivability.
