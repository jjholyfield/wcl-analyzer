Actionable Items

1. Increase Divine Storm usage — 28 missing casts, 2.34M damage gap. Divine Storm was used 43 times (5.4/min) vs the reference's 71 (9.7/min). This is the single largest individual ability deficit and accounts for 25% of the total damage gap. Ret Paladin's AoE spender should be used more aggressively during add phases (Awaken Fungi every ~65s) and whenever 2+ targets are stacked. During Rotmire, adds are active for ~60% of the fight — Divine Storm should be the primary Holy Power spender during those windows, not Final Verdict.
2. Reduce dead time — 38.1s lost vs 14.3s reference (23.8s excess). Alitheria had 11 casting gaps exceeding 2.5 seconds, compared to Apr's 5. Eight of these gaps were not mechanic-related, representing pure uptime loss. At 1.89 casts/second, 23.8 seconds of excess dead time = ~45 lost GCDs. Focus on always having the next ability queued — if moving, use instant-cast abilities (Judgment, Hammer of Wrath, Divine Toll, Wake of Ashes) rather than stopping output entirely.
3. Use Divine Steed for uptime, not just escape. Apr used Divine Steed 5 times vs Alitheria's 2. Divine Steed is a movement tool that lets melee stay on the boss during repositioning phases. Use it proactively when you know movement is coming (pre-Bloom, pre-Vines) to maintain melee range and keep casting, rather than reactively after already losing uptime.
4. Execution Sentence alignment — 2.08M damage gap. Both players used Execution Sentence 8 vs 7 times (similar frequency), but Apr got 5.58M damage out of it vs Alitheria's 3.50M. This suggests Alitheria is not aligning Execution Sentence with burst windows (Avenging Wrath + Hammer of Light). The damage window where Execution Sentence detonates should overlap with the highest damage output period to maximize its stored damage conversion.
5. Maintain overall CPM through mechanics. Total CPM was 113.1 vs 129.3 (−12.5%). The gap is distributed across nearly every ability, suggesting a systemic issue rather than one missing button. Crusading Strikes (−12%), Empyrean Hammer (−11%), Blade of Justice (−15%), and Sacrosanct Crusade (−24%) are all below reference. The common thread: when a mechanic forces movement or attention, Alitheria stops pressing buttons. Ret Paladin has excellent instant-cast options during movement — use them.
