Prog Verdict — Fight 23 (Longest Pull, 4:52)

Invincibale dealt 157.7K DPS across 4:52 on the longest pull of the night, reaching 77.3% boss HP before the raid wiped. This is progression data — the reference Robotsevente killed the boss in 8:24 at 98.9K DPS. The higher raw DPS is expected on a shorter wipe (burst-weighted), so direct DPS comparison is misleading. The meaningful analysis is rotation execution.

Active-time rotational CPM is 28.7 vs 36.7 for the reference — a 21.8% gap that represents roughly 31 missed casts over 3:52 of active time. Invincibale died at 2:42 to Silverstrike Barrage, used Reincarnation, then died again at 3:56. Excluding the 61 seconds of total dead time, the rotation still has significant gaps: Lightning Bolt at 10.4 CPM vs 16.4 (-37%) and Elemental Blast at 2.8 CPM vs 4.4 (-35%). The biggest issues are casting gaps during mechanics (11.8s gap at 2:05, 12.5s gap at 3:30) and underusing the primary filler (Lightning Bolt). Flame Shock is being over-applied at 6.2 CPM vs 5.0 — GCDs spent refreshing the DoT too aggressively instead of casting Lightning Bolt. With only 5 talent differences and nearly identical ilvl (290.5 vs 290.7), the gap is execution-based.

Lightning Bolt Filler Gap (−37%)

Lightning Bolt is the primary filler for Stormbringer Elemental — it generates Maelstrom for Elemental Blast, builds Tempest stacks via overloads, and benefits from Stormkeeper empowerment. At 10.4 CPM vs 16.4 CPM, Invincibale is missing roughly 23 Lightning Bolt casts over 3:52 of active time. Each lost LB means less Maelstrom generation (fewer EB casts), fewer overload procs (fewer Tempest stacks), and less Lightning Rod damage. This is the single largest CPM deficit and the root cause of the downstream Elemental Blast and Tempest gaps.

Flame Shock Over-Refresh (+24%)

Flame Shock at 6.2 CPM (24 casts) vs 5.0 CPM (42 casts) is being refreshed too aggressively. Looking at the cast timeline, Flame Shock is being double-cast at many timestamps (paired casts at 0.5s, 8.6s, 21.7s, 45.1s, etc.) — this suggests Voltaic Blaze is applying FS to multiple targets, which is correct for add phases, but the manual re-applications are happening with significant pandemic time remaining. Each unnecessary Flame Shock is a Lightning Bolt that was not cast. Flame Shock should only be refreshed when 6 seconds or fewer remain on the DoT.

Tempest is the biggest distribution gap: 11.7% vs 20.7% of total damage. Actionable Items

1. Survive Silverstrike Barrage. Death at 2:42 to Silverstrike Barrage cost 61 seconds of total downtime (4.2s dead + 9.3s post-rez recovery + the preceding 17.7s of running/Ghost Wolf). That is ~21% of the fight spent dead or recovering. Silverstrike Barrage is a positional mechanic — the damage is avoidable. Use Astral Shift (available, was never cast in this pull) or position to avoid the arrow path. Surviving this single mechanic would add roughly 25–30 casts back into the rotation.
2. Fill with Lightning Bolt, not Ghost Wolf. At 10.4 CPM vs 16.4 CPM (−37%), Lightning Bolt usage is the largest ability gap. Ghost Wolf was cast 4 times during active combat (not during death), costing GCDs. Elemental Shaman has excellent movement tools: Spiritwalker's Grace (used only 2x), instant Lava Burst via Lava Surge procs, Gust of Wind, and Stormkeeper-empowered instant Lightning Bolt. During movement, the priority should be: (1) Spiritwalker's Grace for extended movement, (2) instant Lava Burst if Lava Surge is up, (3) Stormkeeper instant LB, (4) Flame Shock refresh if needed. Ghost Wolf should be a last resort, not a go-to movement tool. Estimated gain: 15–20 additional Lightning Bolt casts over the fight.
3. Cast Elemental Blast on cooldown with Master of the Elements. At 2.8 CPM vs 4.4 CPM (−35%), Elemental Blast is significantly underused. In the Stormbringer build, Elemental Blast is the primary Maelstrom spender that applies Lightning Rod for passive damage. The reference casts EB 4.4 times per minute vs Invincibale's 2.8. Each EB cast also grants a secondary stat buff (Haste, Crit, or Mastery). Prioritize casting EB immediately after Lava Burst (to consume Master of the Elements) for maximum damage. The Lightning Rod uptime gap (1.6% vs 5.0% damage share) is a direct consequence of fewer EB casts.
4. Reduce Flame Shock over-application. At 6.2 CPM vs 5.0 CPM (+24%), Flame Shock is consuming GCDs that should be Lightning Bolt. Looking at the timeline, Flame Shock is being double-cast at many timestamps (Voltaic Blaze applying to multiple targets), which is fine for multi-target. But manual refreshes with significant pandemic time remaining are pure waste. Flame Shock lasts 18 seconds — only refresh when 6 seconds or fewer remain. With Fire Elemental active, the duration doubles. Each unnecessary Flame Shock is a Lightning Bolt not cast.
5. Eliminate the 12.5-second gap at 3:30. Between 3:30 and 3:43, there are 12.5 seconds with only two Flame Shock casts and no damage abilities. This gap is not explained by death or Ghost Wolf — it represents a full rotation stoppage during an active phase. At 0.48 CPS, this gap alone costs ~6 casts. Whether this was a mechanics confusion, UI issue, or loss of focus, identifying and fixing this type of unexplained gap is critical for prog consistency.
