Actionable Improvements

CRITICAL — #1 PRIORITY

Survive the P1 Bursting Emptiness Window (~1:06)

4 of 7 Bursting Emptiness deaths occur at almost exactly 1:06–1:07 into the fight. This is the Null Corona + Void Expulsion overlap in Phase 1. Use Anti-Magic Shell (AMS) proactively at 1:04 to absorb magic damage and generate Runic Power. If AMS is down or insufficient, use Icebound Fortitude or Death Pact as a backup. This single fix would prevent ~20% of all deaths this session and likely push the intermission reach rate from 55% to 75%+.

CRITICAL — #2 PRIORITY

Avoid Void Remnant Pools Throughout P1

4 deaths to Void Remnants, 3 in P1. These are avoidable ground effects. As a melee DPS, positioning awareness around the boss is critical. Pre-position away from Void Remnant spawn locations and use Death's Advance for movement speed when repositioning. Do not chase the boss through pools. Being alive > one extra GCD of damage.

HIGH

Plan Defensives for Cosmic Barrier in P2/P3

3 deaths to Cosmic Barrier (fights 6, 11, 23). This is a shield mechanic that reflects damage or deals burst damage when not handled. Stop attacking during Cosmic Barrier if the shield is about to expire with high reflected damage stacking. Have AMS ready for the burst. The fight 23 death at 6:17 ended what was otherwise the best pull of the night.

MEDIUM

Voidstalker Sting Mitigation in P2/P3

2 deaths to Voidstalker Sting (F18 P2, F25 P3). This is a targeted debuff that deals sustained damage. When targeted by Voidstalker Sting, immediately use a defensive (AMS for magic, Icebound Fortitude if AMS is down) and call for external healing. Both deaths occurred on long pulls where defensives may have been on cooldown from earlier usage — plan a CD rotation: AMS for the first Sting, IBF for the second, Death Pact for the third.

MEDIUM

Maintain Cooldown Discipline on Long Pulls

On the best pulls (F23: 63.4K, F25: 58.0K), DPS is strong and competitive — ranked #1 and #3 respectively. The rotation profile looks correct: Putrefy as top damage, Necrotic Coil active during Forbidden Knowledge windows, Gargoyle and Army providing sustained pet damage. Continue aligning Army of the Dead + Dark Transformation every 90 seconds and ensure Putrefy charges are concentrated inside Dark Transformation windows. No major rotation issues detected.
