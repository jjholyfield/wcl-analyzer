Actionable Improvements

CRITICAL — #1 PRIORITY

Survive Phase 1 Consistently

Zinnks died during P1 in 6 of 20 pulls (30%), often to Silverstrike Arrow (2 deaths at ~1:03) and Void Expulsion. These are early-fight mechanics that should be survivable every pull. Fix: Pre-position for Silverstrike Arrow dodges. Use Obsidian Scales or Hover preemptively when Alleria casts. Glide/Hover immediately on knockbacks to avoid the 184K avg falling damage. Reaching Intermission alive every pull is the minimum bar — the raid loses Ebon Might when the Aug dies, which compounds the problem for everyone.

CRITICAL — #2 PRIORITY

Stop Dying to Cosmic Barrier and Avoidable P2/P3 Mechanics

Cosmic Barrier killed Zinnks 3 times across the session (fights 5, 23, 25). Silverstrike Ricochet killed twice (fights 6, 13). Simulacrum Backlash and Void Remnants each killed once. Fix: Cosmic Barrier is a positioning mechanic — stay at safe distance from the source. Silverstrike Ricochet is a dodge mechanic. In P2, the combination of Rift Slash (every 12s), Voidstalker Sting (every 25s), and Void Barrage creates high movement pressure. Pre-plan Hover usage for Rift Slash windows and use Obsidian Scales before Voidstalker Sting pairs.

HIGH

Increase Eruption and Living Flame CPM

Eruption is 12.9 CPM vs the reference 16.1 CPM (-20%). Living Flame is 6.1 vs 9.5 CPM (-36%). These are the primary filler abilities between empowered spells. The 14.4% total CPM gap (38.1 vs 44.5) translates directly to less Ebon Might extension and lower attributed damage. Fix: Between Fire Breath/Upheaval windows, prioritize pressing Eruption or Living Flame on every GCD. The reference player presses 5.4 more buttons per minute — that is roughly one extra GCD every 11 seconds that Zinnks is leaving empty. Much of this is likely caused by movement for mechanics (addressed in items 1-2), but also check for decision paralysis between abilities.

MEDIUM

Use Zephyr More Aggressively

Zephyr (group damage reduction) was used only once in fight 25 on a 2-minute CD. In a 6:04 active window, that is at least 2 missed uses. Fix: Assign Zephyr to specific mechanic windows — Void Expulsion, Stellar Emission (intermission), or Cosmic Radiation. This helps the entire raid survive, and Aug Evoker's value is in buffing and protecting the group, not just personal DPS.

MEDIUM

Use DPS Potion Instead of Health Potion

Zinnks used a Silvermoon Health Potion on fight 25. While understandable on prog, switching to an Intellect potion aligned with Breath of Eons + Lust windows would provide a meaningful DPS boost. This change should come after the survival issues are resolved — there is no point potting for DPS if you are dead 30 seconds later.

Summary Assessment

The gap is survival, not rotation knowledge

Zinnks' Ebon Might maintenance and empowered spell usage are at or above the reference level. The rotation fundamentals are correct. The problem is straightforward: Zinnks dies in every single pull, and a dead Aug Evoker provides zero value. The 38.8K avg DPS vs 103K reference is misleading — most of that gap comes from dying at 3:32 average instead of being alive for the full 8+ minute kill. If Zinnks survived the full fight with current P1-level execution, the DPS would be significantly higher. Focus entirely on survival mechanics for the next session. Once Zinnks can survive to the wipe (or the kill), the rotation and DPS will follow.
