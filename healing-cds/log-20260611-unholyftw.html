Progression Context

This is progression data — 26 wipe pulls on Mythic Crown of the Cosmos with no kill. The primary analysis uses Fight 23 (the longest pull at 4:52, reaching 77.31% boss). The reference comparison is against Lanadk, the #1 ranked Unholy DK who achieved a kill in 8:28 (508s). Prog pulls involve learning mechanics, deaths, and incomplete fight execution — DPS gaps vs a kill reference should be interpreted with that context. The raid reached 0.01% boss on fights 8 and 17 (very short pulls likely involving phase 1 burst), but fight 23 is the most data-rich pull for rotation analysis.

Bottom Line

Unholyftw dealt 157K DPS on the best long pull (Fight 23, 4:52) before dying at 4:14 to Simulacrum Backlash during the Phase 2 transition. Active DPS before death was 203K — actually 7.4% above the reference Lanadk's kill DPS of 189K, which is strong for a prog pull. The core rotation shows solid cooldown alignment — Dark Transformation was cast 5 times in 4:14 (1.2 CPM, matching reference), and Army of the Dead was correctly paired with every other DT window. However, rotational CPM is 35.2 vs 49.3 for the reference — a 28.6% gap. Approximately 50 seconds of that gap comes from the Phase 1→2 intermission (2:04–2:58) where the boss is untargetable and movement is required, plus additional downtime around deaths at 2:30+. Adjusting for the intermission, active-phase CPM is closer to ~43, still below the reference but much more reasonable for prog. The biggest improvement areas are Scourge Strike CPM (11.9 vs 21.1 — the largest single ability gap) and Death Coil + Necrotic Coil frequency (9.6 combined vs 17.7).

Scourge Strike Deficit: −35% CPM

Scourge Strike is the primary rune spender that consumes Festering Wounds to spawn Lesser Ghouls, which then deal significant pet damage through Sweeping Claws and Infected Claws. At 13.7 CPM vs 21.1 CPM, Unholyftw is casting ~35% fewer Scourge Strikes. Part of this gap comes from the 50-second intermission where no targets exist, but even adjusting for that (~40s of dead time), active-phase Scourge Strike CPM is still well below reference. Each missed Scourge Strike means fewer Lesser Ghouls spawned, fewer wound pops for Festermight stacks, and less Runic Power generated for Death Coil.

Death Coil + Necrotic Coil Combined Gap

Death Coil (6.1 CPM) + Necrotic Coil (5.0 CPM) = 11.1 combined CPM vs reference's 10.6 + 7.1 = 17.7 CPM. This is a −37% gap in Runic Power spending. Death Coil is the primary Runic Power dump and triggers Runic Corruption (rune regeneration), Sudden Doom procs, and maintains Icy Talons haste buff. Necrotic Coil replaces Death Coil during Army of the Dead windows. Fewer Death Coils means slower rune regeneration, which cascades into fewer Scourge Strikes and Festering Strikes.

Outbreak Over-Casting: 12 vs 3

Unholyftw cast Outbreak 12 times vs the reference's 3. Outbreak applies Virulent Plague, which only needs to be refreshed when it falls off (rare in single target) or applied to new targets. With Blightburst talented (which provides infinite plague duration on single target), Outbreak should almost never be needed after the opener. Those 12 Outbreak GCDs displaced ~9 Scourge Strikes or Death Coils. On a multi-target fight like Crown (with demibosses and adds), some additional Outbreaks are justified for spreading diseases to new targets, but 12 is excessive — the reference managed with 3 across a full 8:28 kill.

Actionable Items

1. Increase Scourge Strike frequency between cooldown windows. Scourge Strike at 13.7 CPM vs reference's 21.1 CPM is the single largest ability gap (−35%). Even after accounting for ~50 seconds of intermission downtime, active-phase Scourge Strike CPM is still below reference. Scourge Strike consumes Festering Wounds to spawn Lesser Ghouls (which generate substantial pet damage via Sweeping Claws and Infected Claws) and generates Runic Power for Death Coil. Every missed Scourge Strike cascades: fewer wounds popped → fewer Lesser Ghouls → less pet damage → less Runic Power → fewer Death Coils → slower Runic Corruption rune regen. Focus on pressing Scourge Strike whenever you have 1+ Festering Wounds and runes available. The rune-to-Scourge-Strike pipeline is the core of the spec's economy. Estimated impact: largest single improvement available.
2. Use Anti-Magic Shell on cooldown for Runic Power generation. AMS was used 1 time vs reference's 6 times in a fight nearly twice as long. On Crown of the Cosmos, Echoing Darkness deals constant magic damage (118 hits, 3.3M total on this pull). Each AMS absorbs multiple ticks and generates bonus Runic Power from the absorbed damage. With a 45-second cooldown, AMS should be used 5+ times in a 4:14 pull. This is both a survivability button (absorbed damage does not need healing) and a DPS button (extra Runic Power = extra Death Coils). The reference used it at 0:09, 4:06, 5:49, 6:29, 7:12, and 8:03 — essentially on cooldown whenever magic damage was incoming. Estimated impact: +3–5 extra Death Coils per fight from bonus RP.
3. Reduce Outbreak casts from 12 to 3–4. Outbreak was cast 12 times vs reference's 3. With Blightburst talented, Virulent Plague has infinite duration on single targets and should only need reapplication on new adds. Each unnecessary Outbreak GCD displaces a Scourge Strike or Death Coil. On Crown of the Cosmos, you need Outbreak for the opener, once for the Rift Simulacrum in Phase 2, and possibly 1–2 for demiboss target swaps in Phase 1. That is 3–4 total, not 12. Estimated impact: recovery of ~8 GCDs for higher-priority abilities.
4. Increase Festering Scythe uptime. Festering Scythe at 1.2 CPM vs reference's 2.6 CPM (−54%). Festering Scythe maintains a debuff that triggers Dread Plague and feeds the Festering Wound system. The reference casts it every ~23 seconds. Unholyftw cast it 5 times in 4:14, averaging every ~51 seconds. The debuff should be maintained continuously — when it falls off, prioritize recasting it before other fillers. This passively generates more Festering Wounds for Scourge Strike to consume.
5. Spend Runic Power more aggressively on Death Coil. Combined Death Coil + Necrotic Coil CPM is 11.1 vs reference's 17.7 (−37%). Death Coil is the primary Runic Power dump and triggers Runic Corruption (rune regeneration), which feeds back into more Scourge Strikes and Festering Strikes. Avoid sitting on high Runic Power — dump it with Death Coil whenever you are above ~80 RP and no higher-priority ability is available. During Army of the Dead windows, Necrotic Coil replaces Death Coil and should be spent even more aggressively to buff the summoned ghouls. Estimated impact: faster rune regeneration cycle, more total casts per minute.

Summary

This is a prog analysis — 26 wipes, no kill. Focus on the CPM gap during active combat phases, not the raw DPS number vs kill references.
