How Holy Paladin Works

Holy Paladin generates Holy Power through builders (Holy Shock, Judgment, Flash of Light, Holy Light) and spends it on Light of Dawn (AoE heal) and Word of Glory/Eternal Flame (single target). The spec has a very high cast rate — you’re constantly pressing buttons.

Holy Shock is your primary builder — cast it on cooldown for Holy Power and Infusion of Light procs. Flash of Light and Holy Light are your main healing spells — Flash is fast and procs instant with Infusion of Light, Holy Light is bigger but slower. Light of Dawn is your primary Holy Power spender for raid healing.

Major CDs: Avenging Wrath (2min throughput), Aura Mastery (raid-wide DR), Divine Toll (burst Holy Shocks), Lay on Hands (emergency), Blessing of Sacrifice (external).

Beacon of Light transfers healing to beacon targets — place on ranged DPS for mastery synergy.

How This Is Affecting Your Healing

CPM at 101% of top — uptime is NOT the issue. Dead time at 5.8% is excellent. You’re pressing buttons fast enough. The problem isn’t how many buttons you press — it’s which ones.
Flash of Light at 27% of top and Holy Light at 33%. This is the HPS gap. Top HPals cast Flash of Light 10.3/min and Holy Light 5.7/min. Dueche casts 2.8 and 1.9. These are the actual healing spells — they generate Holy Power AND heal the target. Missing ~12 healing casts per minute compared to top players.
Judgment at 294% of top. Casting 4.7/min vs top’s 1.6. Judgment generates Holy Power but doesn’t heal. You’re spending GCDs on a DPS/builder that doesn’t contribute to healing output.
Hammer of Wrath at 1.1/min — top players cast 0. Another DPS ability taking GCDs from healing. On a fight where raid healing is the bottleneck, every Hammer of Wrath GCD should be a Flash of Light instead.
The pattern: too many DPS-oriented GCDs, too few healing GCDs. The CPM is there but the spell priority favors damage over healing. Judgment and Hammer of Wrath are eating the GCDs that should be Flash of Light and Holy Light. Fix the spell priority and the HPS follows.
0/26 pre-pot, 19% healthstone, 19% health potion. Low consumable usage with a 23% death rate. Sacred Toll and Divine Toll killed you 4 times across the night.

What You Need to Change

1
More Flash of Light and Holy Light
Flash of Light at 2.8/min needs to be 10+. Holy Light at 1.9/min needs to be 5+. These are your core healing spells. Every FoL/HL cast generates Holy Power AND heals the target. This is where the entire HPS gap lives.
2
Less Judgment — drop from 4.7/min to ~2
Judgment generates Holy Power but doesn’t heal. On a fight where the raid is taking heavy damage, healing spells that ALSO generate HP are strictly better. Cut Judgment casts in half and replace those GCDs with Flash of Light.
3
Cut Hammer of Wrath entirely
1.1/min of a DPS ability on a fight where healing is the bottleneck. Top players cast zero. Those GCDs should be Flash of Light.
4
Use healthstone and health potion
19% usage on both with a 23% death rate. These are free survivability on separate cooldowns. Use them when you take damage.
