How Demonology Warlock Works

Demo Lock is a summoner spec built around managing an army of demons. Your job is to generate Soul Shards, spend them on Hand of Gul’dan to summon Wild Imps, then amplify all your demons’ damage with Summon Demonic Tyrant.

The core loop: Shadow Bolt generates Soul Shards when you’re low. Demonbolt (instant, from Demonic Core procs) generates shards quickly during burst. Hand of Gul’dan spends shards and summons Wild Imps. Call Dreadstalkers summons powerful demons on cooldown.

Summon Demonic Tyrant is your big 2-minute cooldown — the Tyrant’s damage scales with how many demons are active. The current design lets you spam Hand of Gul’dan DURING the Tyrant window since damage updates dynamically. Enter the window with Demonic Core stacks and shards ready, then dump everything.

Implosion detonates your Wild Imps for burst AoE damage. With proper talents it procs additional imps and returns resources.

Key rules: don’t overcap Demonic Core procs (max 4 stacks — use them before they’re wasted). Don’t starve yourself of shards before Tyrant comes off CD. Keep Call Dreadstalkers on CD for consistent demon presence.
