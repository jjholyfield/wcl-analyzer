Phase-Specific Insights

P1 Sentinels: Strong and Consistent

250.5K average DPS across all 14 pulls with only 12% variance (216K-278K). This is MM Hunter's strongest window on this fight -- multi-target cleave on sentinels with Volley, Trick Shots, and opener CDs. All 5 P1 deaths were cascade deaths (not among the first 2 to die in the pull). P1 execution is not a concern.

Intermission: Massive Variance

5.6K average with a range of 159 to 17,190. This is a 39-second survival phase (Stellar Emission channel + Silverstrike Barrage) where DPS uptime is heavily dependent on positioning and movement. Some pulls show near-zero damage (fights 1, 3) while later pulls show 11-17K. The learning trend here is positive -- Darthurg is finding opportunities to deal damage during intermission as the night progresses. 1 actionable death to Singularity Eruption in fight 1 (the first pull).

P2 Rift: High Variance, Trending Down

76.6K average but the spread is wide (48.6K to 107K). The best P2 pull was fight 6 (107K), and the latest deep pull (fight 25) was the worst P2 at 48.6K. Rift Simulacrum and Undying Voidspawn mechanics force target switching and movement. When Darthurg maintains uptime through Rift mechanics, P2 DPS is excellent. When movement is poorly managed, DPS drops by nearly half. This is the primary execution improvement area.

P3 Cosmos: The Death Zone

11 of Darthurg's 22 deaths occur in P3, though none are actionable (all cascade). 45.2K average DPS in P3 is expected for a phase where everything overlaps -- Cosmic Radiation, Gravity Collapse, Void Barrage, and Cosmic Barrier all demand constant movement. The 11/14 reach rate shows the raid is consistently making it to P3 but not surviving long within it. P3 is the team-wide wall, not a Darthurg-specific issue.

Actionable Improvements

1

Defensive Usage in P3

Only 1 Survival of the Fittest cast in the entire 7:56 deepest pull. P3 has Cosmic Radiation (constant ticking damage), Gravity Collapse (10 hits for 1.3M), and Void Expulsion overlaps. Survival of the Fittest should be used on every Gravity Collapse in P3 -- it is a 3-minute CD that should see 2+ uses in P3 alone. Additionally, Exhilaration (2 uses total) could be used more proactively during high-damage overlaps rather than saved for emergencies. While the 18 cascade P3 deaths are not Darthurg's fault individually, stronger personal defensive usage makes the player harder to kill in the cascade.

2

Intermission Positioning

2 of 4 actionable deaths came during intermission (Singularity Eruption in fight 1, Silverstrike Barrage in fight 24). The intermission is a 39-second pure survival phase. For a ranged DPS, the priority here is staying alive, not pushing damage. The intermission DPS improvement from 159 in early pulls to 17K in fight 25 shows the player is learning when it is safe to cast, but the fight 24 death shows intermission can still be punishing. Pre-position for Silverstrike Barrage and save Survival of the Fittest for intermission if Trueshot is not available.

3

P2 DPS Consistency

P2 DPS ranges from 48.6K (fight 25) to 107K (fight 6) -- a 2.2x spread. This is the widest variance of any phase. The Rift phase requires target switching between Rift Simulacrum, Undying Voidspawn, and Silver Simulacrum while handling movement mechanics. When Darthurg lines up Trueshot and Volley with add spawns for Trick Shots cleave, P2 DPS is exceptional. When these CDs are misaligned or movement disrupts the rotation, output drops sharply. Focus on aligning Trueshot with the P2 add spawns rather than using it on cooldown.

4

Falling Damage

383K falling damage (2 instances) in fight 25. This is fully avoidable and indicates knockback or displacement mechanics not being handled cleanly. Use Disengage (3 casts in fight 25) proactively to counteract knockbacks, or pre-position away from ledge/drop zones during Gravity Collapse. Falling damage in a prog environment can cascade into a death.

5

Session Trend is Positive

Late-session average (45.4K) is 9.7% higher than early-session (41.4K). The deepest pull of the night (fight 25, 7:56, 50.7K) came in the final pull. This is a strong progression trajectory -- Darthurg is learning the fight and performing better as the night goes on. The first 2 actionable deaths (fights 1, 2) were the opening pulls. After the early stumbles, execution stabilized significantly.
