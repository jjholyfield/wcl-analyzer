Actionable Improvements

1
Sustain DPS Through P2 Movement
Issue: P1 to P2 DPS drops 58% (219K to 92K). While some drop is expected due to P1 burst cooldowns expiring and P2 movement mechanics, 58% is steep. The best P2 pull (F24: 130.8K) shows the ceiling is higher.

Fix: Use Spiritwalker's Grace proactively at the start of P2 movement sequences (Rift Simulacrum spawns, Undying Voidspawn positioning). Pre-position for P2 mechanics during the tail end of intermission to minimize Ghost Wolf usage. When forced to move without Spiritwalker's Grace, prioritize instant casts: Lava Surge procs, Tempest (if available), and Flame Shock refreshes.

Impact: Closing even half the gap between average P2 DPS (92K) and best P2 DPS (131K) would add ~19K effective DPS during the P2 window (2:16 duration), representing roughly 2.6M additional damage per pull.
2
Astral Shift Usage Consistency
Issue: Astral Shift (1.5min CD, 40% DR) was only used in 5 of 11 pulls with just 7 total casts. On a 7-8 minute fight, this should be used 4-5 times per pull.

Fix: Plan Astral Shift for predictable high-damage moments: Silverstrike Barrage during intermission, Void Expulsion in P2, Cosmic Barrier overlaps in P3. Pre-plan usage at specific boss timers rather than reacting to low HP.

Impact: While this won't directly increase DPS, consistent defensive usage is critical in P3 where raid-wide damage ramps. Dying to cascade less often means more uptime in the phases that matter. The 0% actionable death rate is already excellent -- Astral Shift will help maintain that as the raid pushes deeper into P3 where survival pressure increases.
3
Reduce Ghost Wolf GCDs
Issue: 85 Ghost Wolf casts across 11 pulls (~7.7 per pull). Each Ghost Wolf is a GCD not spent on damage. While movement is necessary, the Gust of Wind talent (only 8 casts total) provides instant repositioning without consuming multiple GCDs for running in Wolf form.

Fix: Map out the movement patterns for each phase. In P2 specifically, where DPS drops the most, replace Ghost Wolf repositioning with Gust of Wind for quick jumps and Spiritwalker's Grace for sustained movement casting. Save Ghost Wolf for longer cross-arena repositioning (intermission) where casting is not possible anyway.

Impact: Reducing Ghost Wolf by 2-3 casts per pull during P2/P3 recovers 2-3 GCDs worth of damage (roughly 150-200K additional damage per pull).
4
Health Pot and Healthstone Usage in P3
Issue: Only 3 health potion uses and 2 Healthstone uses across 11 pulls. In P3 where raid damage ramps exponentially, these emergency tools should be used proactively.

Fix: Use Silvermoon Health Potion and Healthstone during the first Cosmic Radiation overlap in P3 (~5:30 mark) when healing pressure is highest. This reduces the burden on healers and keeps Chedrock alive longer during cascade-risk moments.

Impact: Indirect DPS gain through additional survival time. Even 5-10 extra seconds alive in late P3 translates to meaningful damage on the boss.
