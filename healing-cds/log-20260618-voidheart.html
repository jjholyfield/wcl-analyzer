Actionable Items

1. Use Defensive Cooldowns -- This Is the #1 Problem

Dispersion was never pressed in 27 pulls. It provides 90% damage reduction for 6 seconds on a 2-minute cooldown. On a 6-minute fight, that is 3 available uses. Desperate Prayer was used 3 times total (should be 1-2 per pull, ~40+ expected across 27 pulls). Pain Suppression on self was used once. Every single death to Bursting Emptiness (4 deaths), Void Remnants (4 deaths), and Silverstrike Barrage (3 deaths) is survivable with one of these buttons. These 11 deaths represent 41% of all deaths. This is the difference between the current 0% survival rate and a 50%+ survival rate.

Specific timing:
- Dispersion at 2:22-2:28 during Stellar Emission (Intermission) to survive Silverstrike Barrage overlap
- Desperate Prayer on any health drop below 50% (it is off the GCD)
- Dispersion on Bursting Emptiness detonation if below 60% health
- Fade or Dispersion during Void Remnants swirlies if caught

2. Fix Phase 1 Survival -- 14 of 27 Deaths Happen Here

Phase 1 is the lowest-damage phase of the fight. Dying here 52% of the time means mechanical/positioning failures, not healing throughput issues. Void Remnants and Bursting Emptiness are the primary P1 killers -- both are avoidable/mitigatable mechanics. Void Remnants: watch for swirl indicators on the ground and move early. Bursting Emptiness: track Void Droplet positions and maintain distance.

The 43% overheal in P1 also suggests attention is focused on healing instead of positioning. In P1, Smite spam through Atonement should handle most healing passively -- spend awareness on mechanics, not healing targets.

3. Reduce P1 Overheal to Conserve Mana

55%+ average overheal in P1 means nearly half of mana spent in P1 is wasted. P1 damage is low enough that Atonement from DPS filler (Smite, Penance, Mind Blast) should cover most healing needs. Save Flash Heal (61% overheal on this fight) for genuine triage situations. More Smite, less Flash Heal in P1 = more mana for P2/P3 ramps and better throughput through Atonement.

4. Pre-Plan Intermission Survival

3 deaths to Silverstrike Barrage in Intermission (2:14-2:53). The Intermission is a known, scripted survival check. Pre-plan: at 2:14 when Silversunder Catastrophe fires, immediately position for Stellar Emission. Have Dispersion or Desperate Prayer ready for the overlap window at 2:22-2:35. The team only has Silencio's Convoke covering this window -- a Disc personal defensive fills the gap.

5. Evangelism Ramp Timing Is Good -- Maintain This

The 3-ramp pattern (0:08, 3:20, 4:52) with 3x PWR per Evangelism is mechanically correct. The post-Evangelism damage sequence (SW:Pain, Mind Blast, Smite, SW:Death, Penance) is proper priority. P2 HPS of 99K with 30% overheal shows the rotation works when Voidheart is alive. Keep this pattern and extend it into P3 as survival improves.
