Actionable Items

1. Improve P3 Consistency (Biggest Growth Area)

P3 DPS ranges from 15K to 67K, a 4.5x spread. The best P3 pulls (F26: 67K, F28: 63K) show what Moistbear is capable of. The low pulls (F6: 21K, F27: 15K) correlate with Bear Form uptime for survival and early deaths after cascade begins. Action: On deep P3 pulls, prioritize maintaining Cat Form uptime. Use Survival Instincts (50% DR, 6s duration) proactively during Cosmic Radiation overlaps rather than shifting to Bear Form. This preserves DPS while providing similar survivability.

2. Use Survival Instincts More Aggressively

Only used in 6 of 11 long pulls (55%). Survival Instincts is a 50% damage reduction for 6 seconds on a 3-minute cooldown with 2 charges. In a 7-minute fight, that is 4 potential uses. Using SI instead of Bear Form during predictable damage windows (Cosmic Radiation, Voidstalker Sting ticks) maintains DPS uptime. Action: Pre-plan SI usage for P3 Cosmic Radiation windows. Two charges means one for the first wave, one for the second.

3. Tiger's Fury Usage Efficiency

On the best pull (F14, 7:12), Tiger's Fury was cast 10 times, which works out to roughly once every 43 seconds. The cooldown is 30 seconds. Losing ~13 seconds per cycle means ~3-4 missed casts per fight, each of which would have provided 15% damage amp for bleeds and an energy refill. Action: Track Tiger's Fury cooldown more tightly. It should be pressed almost on cooldown (slight delay to align with Rip/Rake refreshes for snapshotting is fine, but 43s gaps are too long).

4. P1 and P2 Execution Are Strong

P1 avg 247.8K with consistent cleave damage across all 19 pulls. P2 avg 95.0K with tight variance (79K-110K range). These phases are well-executed. Moistbear is cycling Rake on adds, using Berserk/Convoke windows effectively, and maintaining bleed uptime. The interrupt count (22 Skull Bash casts across 8 fights) and utility (15 Stampeding Roars, 5 Innervates) show strong raid awareness.

5. Death Profile is Clean

Only 4 actionable deaths in 19 pulls, and none are to the same mechanic twice. This means Moistbear is not repeatedly failing any specific mechanic. The two P1 actionable deaths (Void Expulsion, Grasp of Emptiness) are different abilities requiring different responses. There is no "death wall" pattern. The main survival work is in P3 where the raid as a whole is learning the phase.
