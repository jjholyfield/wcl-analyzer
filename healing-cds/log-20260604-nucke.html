How Vengeance Demon Hunter Works

Vengeance Demon Hunter is a melee tank built around generating and consuming Soul Fragments for self-healing and damage. The entire spec revolves around one loop: generate fragments, then spend them.

Fracture is your primary generator — it deals damage and produces 2 Soul Fragments every cast. Soul Cleave is your primary spender — it consumes Soul Fragments for both healing and damage. Spirit Bomb is your AoE spender — it consumes fragments for AoE damage and applies a debuff. The core loop is: Fracture → Fracture → Soul Cleave (or Spirit Bomb), repeat. Every second you’re not pressing these buttons, you’re losing both damage and self-healing.

Demon Spikes is your primary active mitigation. It provides a large armor buff on a short cooldown with 2 charges. Top VDH tanks maintain Demon Spikes with near-permanent uptime — you should almost always have at least one charge recharging. Think of it like Shield Block for warriors: it should be running at all times, not saved for emergencies.

Metamorphosis is both a DPS cooldown and a defensive cooldown — it provides Leech, which means every point of damage you deal heals you. More Meta uptime = more self-healing = less stress on healers. Fiery Brand is a targeted damage reduction you apply to the boss. Fel Devastation is a channeled frontal that deals damage and heals you.

The critical thing about VDH is that damage and survivability are the same thing. Soul Cleave heals you. Metamorphosis Leech heals you. More Fractures mean more Soul Fragments mean more Soul Cleaves mean more self-healing. A VDH who does low DPS is also a VDH who self-heals poorly — the two problems are inseparable.

How It’s Affecting Your Tanking

Soul Cleave at 45% of #1 is catastrophic. Soul Cleave is both Nucke’s primary self-heal AND his main Soul Fragment consumer. At 8.4/m vs 18.6/m, he’s missing roughly 10 Soul Cleaves per minute. Each Soul Cleave heals based on fragments consumed — that’s massive lost self-healing every single fight. Soul Fragments that aren’t consumed overcap and disappear, which means the Fractures he IS pressing are partially wasted.
Demon Spikes at 49% means half the fight has no armor buff. Demon Spikes is the VDH equivalent of Shield Block — it’s your primary physical damage reduction and it should be active at all times. At 3.6/m vs 7.4/m, Nucke is spending roughly half the fight taking full unmitigated physical damage. This directly increases the healing load on the raid’s healers.
Fracture at 67% is the root cause. Fewer Fractures means fewer Soul Fragments, which means fewer Soul Cleaves. Fracture is the generator that feeds everything — when it’s low, the entire spec falls apart. 36.3/m vs 54.1/m means roughly 18 missing Fractures per minute, which translates to ~36 missing Soul Fragments per minute that never get generated.
DPS at 58% of #1 is a survivability problem, not just a damage problem. VDH self-heals through damage — Leech from Metamorphosis, healing from Soul Cleave, Spirit Bomb. A tank doing 81K instead of 139K is also self-healing proportionally less. The healers have to make up the difference. Low DPS on VDH is low survivability.

What You Need to Change

1
More Soul Cleave — target 18+ per minute
Currently at 8.4/m, the #1 does 18.6/m. Soul Cleave is both your biggest heal and your main damage spender. Every time you have Soul Fragments available, you should be pressing Soul Cleave. This is the single highest-impact change — it fixes both your self-healing and your DPS simultaneously.
2
More Demon Spikes — target 7+ per minute
Currently at 3.6/m, the #1 does 7.4/m. Demon Spikes has 2 charges and a short recharge. You should be pressing it on cooldown to maintain near-permanent uptime. Don’t save both charges for emergencies — always have one recharging. Think of it as a rotational button, not a cooldown.
3
More Fracture — target 50+ per minute
Currently at 36.3/m, the #1 does 54.1/m. Fracture is your primary generator — every Fracture creates 2 Soul Fragments that feed Soul Cleave and Spirit Bomb. More Fractures = more fragments = more Soul Cleaves = more self-healing. This is the root cause of issue #1.
4
More Metamorphosis — target 2.5+ per minute
Currently at 1.3/m, the #1 does 2.7/m. Metamorphosis gives you Leech, which means all your damage heals you. Use it aggressively and frequently — it’s not just a defensive cooldown, it’s a DPS cooldown that also keeps you alive. Holding it too long wastes potential uses over the fight.
5
Health Pot every pull
Currently at 12/30 (40%). As a tank, you take the most consistent damage in the raid. Health Pot is on a separate cooldown from your DPS pre-pot. There’s no reason not to use it on every pull that lasts more than a minute. Healthstone at 57% should also come up — target both every pull.
