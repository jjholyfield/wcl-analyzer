How Survival Hunter Works

Survival Hunter is a melee spec that revolves around weaving Kill Command, Raptor Strike, and Wildfire Bomb in a fast-paced priority rotation. You generate Focus through auto-attacks and spend it on your core abilities.

Kill Command is your highest-priority ability — press it on cooldown every time. It generates Focus and triggers procs for your other abilities. Raptor Strike (single target) and Raptor Swipe (cleave) are your main Focus spenders — they deal damage and interact with Kill Command’s reset mechanics.

Wildfire Bomb is a charge-based ability that applies a DoT and deals AoE damage. Keep charges rolling — don’t sit on 2 charges.

Your burst CDs are Aspect of the Eagle (enhances your attacks) and Coordinated Assault / Spearhead depending on talents. Use these on cooldown for burst windows.

Takedown and Boomstick are additional damage abilities on short cooldowns — press them when available.

The core loop: Kill Command on CD → Raptor Strike/Swipe to spend Focus → Wildfire Bomb to keep charges rolling → weave in Takedown/Boomstick → repeat. Always be pressing buttons — Survival has very little natural downtime compared to other melee specs.
