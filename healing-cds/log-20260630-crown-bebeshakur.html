Actionable Improvements

1. P1 Survival -- Avoid Bursting Emptiness and Void Remnants

Impact: 4 actionable deaths, all in P1 before 1:05. Three of these caused or contributed to full-raid wipes.
Problem: Bursting Emptiness (Void Droplet explosion) killed him in fight 3 at 1:05 as the first raid death. Void Remnants caught him in fights 16 and 24. Corrupting Essence hit him in fight 17.
Fix: Pre-position farther from Void Droplet spawn locations. Use Anti-Magic Shell proactively when Droplets are about to expire. Death Strike is available for emergency healing. Given fight 17 where the raid continued to 7:49 after Bebe died at 0:52, eliminating even one of these early deaths adds significant pull value.
Priority: Highest. Early P1 deaths waste the entire pull for the player.

2. Reduce Falling Damage -- Positioning on Knockbacks

Impact: 5.1M total falling damage across 26 hits in 13 fights. 194K average per hit.
Problem: Consistent falling damage across nearly every qualifying pull suggests a repeating positioning issue with knockback mechanics or platform transitions.
Fix: Pre-position against walls or corners before knockback abilities. Use Death's Advance (if talented) or Death Grip on a target to arrest momentum. Wraith Walk can also help. Since this is consistent across 13 fights, it is a systematic issue rather than a one-off mistake.
Priority: High. 5.1M in avoidable damage puts pressure on healers and increases death risk.

3. Dark Transformation Usage -- 73% Uptime

Impact: 82 casts at 0.97 CPM vs ideal 1.33 CPM (45s CD). Approximately 27% of potential Dark Transformation windows are missed or delayed.
Problem: Dark Transformation is the core DPS cooldown for Unholy. Every missed cast delays the next Army of the Dead alignment (DT aligns with Army every 90s). Delayed DT also means fewer Putrefy windows and reduced pet damage.
Fix: Press Dark Transformation on cooldown as the highest priority. Even during movement, DT is instant-cast. The only acceptable delay is if holding 2-3 seconds to align with Army of the Dead.
Priority: Medium-High. Improving to 90%+ usage would increase sustained DPS by an estimated 3-5%.

4. Use Lichborne and Healing Consumables in P3

Impact: Lichborne used 1 time in 85 minutes of combat. Healthstone 3 times. Healing potion 1 time.
Problem: P3 (Devouring Cosmos) is the current progression wall. Cosmic Radiation, Voidstalker Sting, and Gravity Collapse create sustained damage pressure. With 4 healers and stacking mechanics, self-healing is critical for survival.
Fix: Use Lichborne (2min CD, provides Leech) during heavy AoE in P3, especially on Cosmic Radiation pulses. Use Healthstone and healing potions as P3 intensifies. Death Strike is also available for emergency self-healing even as Unholy.
Priority: Medium. Better self-sustain in P3 extends pull duration and gives healers more bandwidth.
