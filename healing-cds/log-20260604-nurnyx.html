How Shadow Priest Works

Shadow Priest is a ranged caster built around generating Insanity (resource) and spending it on powerful abilities while maintaining damage-over-time effects. The spec has a fast, proc-heavy rotation that rewards constant button-pressing.

The core loop is: maintain Shadow Word: Pain on targets → cast Mind Blast on cooldown (generates Insanity, high priority) → cast Void Blast on cooldown (big damage, similar priority to Mind Blast) → fill with Mind Flay (channeled filler that generates Insanity and procs Shadow Word: Madness) → spend procs of Shadow Word: Madness instantly (instant cast, high damage, use immediately when it procs).

Shadowy Apparitions fire automatically based on your casts and crits — they’re the single biggest DPS contributor. More casts = more apparitions. The gap in Shadowy Apparitions directly reflects the gap in overall casting speed.

Tentacle Slam comes from your Void mindbender pet cooldown. Power Infusion is a burst cooldown you can use on yourself or a teammate.

The key metric is CPM. Shadow Priest has one of the highest CPM requirements in the game. At 77.5 vs #1’s 107.9, Nurnyx is pressing buttons 28% slower — that cascades into fewer procs, fewer Apparitions, and significantly less damage.

What You Need to Change

1
More Mind Flay — Target 9+ Per Minute
You’re at 4.3/m, the #1 is at 9.6/m. Mind Flay is your filler. Any time Mind Blast, Void Blast, and Shadow Word: Death are all on cooldown and you don’t have a Madness proc, you should be channeling Mind Flay. This also generates more Shadow Word: Madness procs, which is free bonus damage.
2
Press Buttons Faster — Target 100+ CPM
You’re at 77.5 CPM, the #1 is at 107.9. Shadow Priest demands the highest APM in the game. Every empty GCD is lost Insanity, lost Apparitions, lost procs. The rotation is fast — you should always be pressing something. Never stand idle. If nothing is available, Mind Flay.
3
More Void Blast — Target 6+ Per Minute
You’re at 4.4/m, the #1 is at 6.8/m. Void Blast is a major damage ability that should be used on cooldown, same priority tier as Mind Blast. Don’t sit on charges — cast it the moment it’s available.
4
Use Healthstones and Health Pots
4/16 Healthstone (25%) and 3/16 Health Pot (19%) is very low, especially with 3 deaths. These are free survival buttons that cost zero DPS. Make a habit of pressing Healthstone when you drop below 50%, and Health Pot when Healthstone is on cooldown. Dead DPS is zero DPS.
