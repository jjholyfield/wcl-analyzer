Actionable Improvements

1
Defensive Cooldown Discipline — Use Dark Pact and Unending Resolve Proactively
Sonic died in all 27 pulls to 14 different abilities. This is not a "learn one mechanic" problem — it is a health management problem. Demo Warlock has two strong defensives: Dark Pact (absorb shield, ~45s CD) and Unending Resolve (40% DR, 3min CD). These should be mapped to specific boss ability timings:

• Unending Resolve for: Intermission Stellar Emission, P2 Call of the Void, P3 Cosmic Radiation
• Dark Pact for: Bursting Emptiness windows (the #1 killer with 6 deaths), Void Expulsion soak
• Healthstone: Keep one ready at all times. Use at 40% HP, not 10%.
Priority: Critical — dying in 27/27 pulls is the #1 issue
2
Pre-place Demonic Circle Before Intermission (2:14)
4 deaths occurred during or near the Intermission (Orbiting Matter, Silverstrike Barrage, 2 Unknown). The platform change at 2:14 + Stellar Emission channel creates a positioning + survival checkpoint. Place Demonic Circle at the safe position before the transition starts. This gives an instant teleport to safety if caught in a bad position during the fall or the Silverstrike Barrage volley. 30% of pulls ended before or during this phase.
Impact: Could save 4+ deaths per session
3
P2 Survival Plan — Map Cooldowns to the Rift Phase
10 deaths occurred in P2 (2:53–5:09), the highest death phase tied with P1. Unlike P1 (where deaths are from individual mechanic failures), P2 deaths are scattered across 8 different abilities. This indicates being overwhelmed by overlapping mechanics (Rift Slash + Call of the Void + Grasp of Emptiness + continuing Void Expulsion).

Create a personal cooldown map for P2:
• Rift Slash at ~3:05, ~3:17: sidestep, no CD needed
• Void Expulsion at ~3:20: Dark Pact
• Call of the Void at ~3:45: Unending Resolve
• Void Expulsion at ~4:20: Dark Pact (back off CD)
• Dark Rush at ~4:50: Demonic Circle teleport
Impact: P2 is where 37% of all deaths occur
4
Maintain P2 DPS During Movement — Use Instant Casts
Raw event DPS drops 61% from P1 (302K) to P2 (118K). While some drop is natural (less cleave, more movement), Demo Lock has strong tools to maintain DPS during movement: Demonbolt (instant with Demonic Core procs), Hand of Gul'dan (instant), and pet damage which continues automatically. Bank 2+ Demonic Core stacks before heavy movement windows (Rift Slash, Call of the Void) so you always have instant-cast options. Never let Demonic Core cap at 4 stacks.
Impact: Even 10% improvement in P2 DPS adds ~4-5K overall
5
Tyrant Windows — Optimize Summoning Sequence
Demonic Tyrant (Burning Cleave) was the #2 damage source on the best pull at 14% of total damage. Tyrant DPS scales with how many demons are active when summoned. On longer pulls where Sonic survived to 5:00+, ensure the sequence before Tyrant: Grimoire: Imp Lord → Hand of Gul'dan x2 (summon 6 imps) → Call Dreadstalkers → Summon Demonic Tyrant. Missing one Hand of Gul'dan before Tyrant loses significant scaling.
Impact: Correct Tyrant sequencing is worth ~5-8% total DPS
