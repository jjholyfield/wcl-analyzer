Actionable Items

1. Echoing Darkness Positioning (P1) — 7 deaths

Echoing Darkness from Vorelus fires orbs every ~3 seconds starting at pull. The damage stacks and becomes lethal within 6-8 seconds of sustained exposure. 7 of 27 deaths (26%) are to this single mechanic, all in the first 70 seconds of the fight. Action: Identify where Vorelus orbs spawn and pre-position away. Use Roll or Tiger's Lust immediately when orbs target your area. Fortifying Brew should be used proactively if caught in an orb path rather than trying to heal through the stacking damage.

2. Bursting Emptiness Awareness (P1/P2) — 4 deaths

Bursting Emptiness creates void pools on the ground that deal massive damage (302K+ on hit). 4 deaths to this mechanic across P1 and P2. Action: These pools are visible on the ground and fully avoidable. During movement-heavy mechanics, keep awareness of pool placements. Do not roll into pools — check destination before using Roll.

3. Revival Usage — Unused in 9/27 pulls (including best pull)

Revival is a 3-minute instant raid-wide heal + dispel that was not cast in 9 pulls and completely absent from fight 23 (the 6:05 best pull). On a 6-minute fight, Revival should be cast at least once (ideally twice — around 1:30 in P1 and again around 4:30 in P2). Action: Plan Revival for a specific damage event. The Intermission Stellar Emission (2:22–2:52) is a strong candidate for the first Revival. The P2 Call of the Void (~3:45) or P3 entry overlap is ideal for the second.

4. Fortifying Brew / Defensive Usage

Only 2 Fortifying Brew casts in the 5:22 best pull. As a personal 20% DR on a relatively short cooldown, this should be used proactively before known high-damage windows — especially the P2-to-P3 transition where Cosmic Radiation, Voidstalker Sting, and Simulacrum Backlash all overlap (the exact window that killed Senssay at 5:22). Dampen Harm (if talented) or Diffuse Magic should also be mapped to specific mechanics.

5. Throughput and CD Usage Are Strong

When alive, Senssay ranks #2 in healer HPS (147.5K avg), hits 190K in P2, and maintains consistent Yu'lon/Celestial Conduit usage (26/27 and 22/27 pulls respectively). Thunder Focus Tea and Mana Tea usage show good rotational discipline. Cast profile is active with 13.4 CPM Vivify and 10.6 CPM Renewing Mist. The healing fundamentals are solid — the entire improvement path is survival.
