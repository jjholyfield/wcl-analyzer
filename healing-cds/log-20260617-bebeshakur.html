Actionable Improvements

Specific, concrete improvement items ranked by estimated DPS impact. Fix these in order for maximum improvement.

#1 — Eliminate Cast Gaps: 70 Seconds of Dead Time
Bebeshakur had 12 cast gaps > 2.5 seconds totaling 70 seconds of zero activity. Tranqx had zero. This alone accounts for an estimated ~54 lost casts and ~8.7M lost damage. 7 of 12 gaps followed Death Coil, suggesting a pattern of dumping RP then stalling. Fix: After every Death Coil, immediately queue the next GCD — Scourge Strike if wounds are up, Festering Strike if wounds are low, or Outbreak/DnD refresh if needed. Practice on a target dummy until ABC (Always Be Casting) is muscle memory.
#2 — Use Death and Decay on Cooldown: 1 Cast vs 17
Death and Decay was cast once the entire 7:58 fight. Tranqx cast it 17 times. DnD transforms Scourge Strike to cleave, provides AoE damage to adds standing in it, and is a core rotational ability for Unholy. Fix: Cast DnD on cooldown (45s CD). It should be up for every add wave and maintained during boss-only phases for the Scourge Strike enhancement. This is likely worth 10-15K DPS on its own given the fight's add density.
#3 — Increase Scourge Strike CPM: 16.7 vs 21.3 Reference
37 fewer Scourge Strikes than reference across the fight. This is partially caused by the cast gaps (#1) and partially by lower overall cast speed. Scourge Strike should be the default button whenever runes are available and the target has Festering Wounds. With DnD active (#2), every SS also cleaves. Fix: Prioritize SS over Death Coil when runes are available. SS generates RP while consuming wounds — it feeds the rotation forward.
#4 — Shift RP Spending to Epidemic During Add Phases
Death Coil CPM matches reference (7.0 both), but Epidemic is at 2.6 CPM vs 5.1 CPM. On Rotmire with frequent Shroomling/Fungling spawns, Epidemic should replace Death Coil as the primary RP spender whenever 2+ targets are active. Fix: During add phases (after Awaken Fungi), use Epidemic instead of Death Coil. Switch back to Death Coil only during single-target boss phases with no adds alive.
#5 — Reduce Excess Death Grips: 15 vs 4 Reference
15 Death Grips is 3.75x more than the reference's 4. Each Death Grip costs a GCD that could be a damage ability. While some grips are necessary for add management, this many suggests using it reactively as filler or for positioning when it is not needed. Fix: Reserve Death Grip for specific add positioning calls from the raid leader. If an add is already in melee range, hit it with SS instead of gripping.
