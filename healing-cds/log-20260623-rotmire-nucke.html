Boss Mechanics — Rotmire (Single Phase)

Rotmire is a single-phase boss with a repeating ~136s mechanic cycle. Key tank mechanic is Putrid Fist (tank buster every ~12s on current tank).

Putrid Fist ×29 (every ~12s) Awaken Fungi ×8 (every ~65s) Festering Vines ×7 (every ~49s) Fungal Bloom ×3 (every ~136s) Bursting Pustules ×12 (every ~21s) Bursting Shroom ×3 (after each Bloom)
Mechanic cycle: Bursting Pustules (0:10) → Awaken Fungi (0:15) → Putrid Fists begin (0:26) → Bursting Pustules (0:31) → Festering Vines (0:43) → ... → Fungal Bloom (2:00) → Bursting Shroom (2:16) → Cycle repeats at ~2:16

Actionable Items

1. Felblade Usage — 71% Below Reference
Nucke cast Felblade 18 times (2.2 CPM) vs Kaali's 64 casts (7.7 CPM). Felblade is a Fury generator and deals meaningful damage — it should be used on cooldown between Fracture casts. The reduced Felblade usage likely cascades into fewer Soul Cleaves (less Fury generation = less Fury to spend). Neliele (closest build, rank 3) also only used 14 Felblades (1.6 CPM), so this may be a talent choice difference — verify whether Nucke talents into Felblade's enhanced CDR.
2. Total CPM Gap — 141.4 vs 169.7 (-17%)
Nucke pressed 28.3 fewer abilities per minute than the rank 1 reference. Over an 8:09 fight, that is ~231 missing button presses. This is the core driver of the DPS gap. The difference compounds: fewer Fractures = fewer Soul Fragments = fewer Soul Cleaves = fewer Demonsurge procs = fewer Voidfall Meteors. Focus on reducing deadtime between casts — ABC (Always Be Casting). Even Neliele at rank 3 was at 153.7 CPM.
3. Soul Cleave at 14.4 CPM vs 18.9 CPM (-24%)
Soul Cleave is both a damage ability and the primary Fury dump. 41 fewer Soul Cleaves than Kaali means 41 fewer damage events plus reduced self-healing. This is likely downstream from the Felblade deficit (less Fury generation) and the lower total CPM. Priority: fix Felblade usage and overall CPM first, and Soul Cleave volume should naturally follow.
4. Metamorphosis Cycling — 13 vs 21 Casts (-38%)
Metamorphosis is the engine of the Fel-Scarred DPS kit. Each Meta cast triggers Demonsurge, which generates Voidfall Meteors (Nucke's #1 damage source at 19%). Kaali cast Meta 8 more times over a similar fight duration, resulting in 2.3x more Voidfall Meteor damage. The Annihilator/Fel-Scarred build has significant Meta cooldown reduction through talents — are all CDR-related talents fully invested? More aggressive Meta cycling would be the single highest-impact DPS change.
5. Talent Choices — Missing Soul Carver and Fel Devastation
Kaali talents into both Soul Carver (7 casts, 674K damage) and Fel Devastation (4 casts, 633K damage + 326K self-healing). Nucke's build skips both. These two abilities contributed 1.31M damage (2% of Kaali's total) plus meaningful self-healing. Review whether the alternative talent picks provide equivalent or greater value. Fel Devastation in particular doubles as a burst self-heal during high-damage windows.
6. Defensive Execution is Strong — Maintain This
95.4% Demon Spikes uptime and 14/14 Putrid Fist hits covered by at least one defensive. DTPS of 34.1K was lower than the rank 1 reference (35.4K). Zero deaths across all 3 pulls. The survival execution is already at a high level — the focus should be purely on increasing offensive output while maintaining this mitigation discipline.
