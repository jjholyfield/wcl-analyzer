What Needs to Change
[P1] Clean up P1 deaths to get more P3 practice
12/20 pulls have a death before 2:14 (P1). When someone dies in P1, the pull averages 4:10. When P1 is clean, it averages 6:24. The top actionable killers in P1 are Bursting Emptiness (13 total deaths) and Void Remnants (9). Dueche (5 actionable P1 deaths) and Bebeshakur (4 actionable P1 deaths) are the most frequent early casualties. Reducing P1 deaths from 1.1 per pull to 0.5 would dramatically increase P3 practice time.
[P3] Stabilize P3 execution -- Voidstalker Sting + Bursting Emptiness
P3 averages 19.5 deaths per pull (most are cascade). The top individual killers are Bursting Emptiness (42 deaths), Voidstalker Sting (39), and Gravity Collapse (24). The raid needs a cleaner response to Voidstalker Sting and better spread positioning to prevent Bursting Emptiness chains. Cosmic Barrier (20 deaths) also indicates soak coordination issues.
[Intermission] Silverstrike Barrage kills in Intermission
Silverstrike Barrage caused 7 deaths in Intermission across 15 pulls. Baodabao died to it twice (both actionable). Darthurg and Senssay each have 2 actionable Intermission deaths. Cleaning up Intermission survival would preserve more resources entering P2.
[P2] P2 is reasonably clean -- maintain discipline
P2 has 67 total deaths across 14 pulls (4.8/pull), but only 5 actionable deaths (0.36/pull). Most P2 deaths are cascade from earlier problems. The main P2 killers are Bursting Emptiness (15) and Voidstalker Sting (7). Keep the current approach working.
[Overall] Night 5 is trending the right direction
The longest pull improved from 7:12 (Night 4) to 7:56 (Night 5) -- 44 seconds deeper into P3. The raid reached P3 in 55% of pulls. DPS output is strong on long pulls (888K raid DPS on the best pull). The comp is producing enough throughput. The limiting factor is survival, particularly early deaths compounding through the fight. Orichamaru, Blowe, Moistbear, and Smokinthisza all had zero actionable deaths -- they are executing cleanly.
