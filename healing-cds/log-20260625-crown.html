What Needs to Change

1. Clean Up Bursting Emptiness in P1 (Phase Wall Breaker)

Bursting Emptiness is responsible for 9 actionable deaths total (7 in P1, 2 in P2). Players hit: Baodabao, Orichamaru (x2), Dueche (x2), Senssay, Invincibale (x2), Alitheria. This ability consistently kills people in the 53-65 second window of the fight. It caused 6 of the 8 P1 wipes. Fix: ensure the raid is spreading for Bursting Emptiness, and healers have CDs planned for this timing.

2. Darthurg Must Stop Dying to Orbiting Matter in Intermission

Darthurg died to Orbiting Matter 3 times in the Intermission — pulls 16, 17, and 19 (Fights 24, 26, 28). This is the single most repeated individual death pattern in the entire log. Orbiting Matter is a positional mechanic — the fix is movement, not healing. Darthurg needs to prioritize dodging orbs over DPS during the 39-second Intermission.

3. Intermission Survival Needs Work

10 actionable deaths in a 39-second window = 0.91 deaths per pull. Silverstrike Barrage killed 4 (Smokinthisza x2, Snackznchill, Orichamaru). Orbiting Matter killed 4 (Darthurg x3, Snackznchill). These are positioning and awareness checks. The Intermission has a higher death RATE per second than any other phase. Consider adding a healing CD rotation for this window (Darkness, Rally, or Spirit Link at the Barrage cast).

4. Alitheria: First Night Back Needs Ramp-Up

Alitheria leads with 5 actionable deaths (more than anyone else) and the lowest DPS among pure DPS at 37.6K avg. Dying twice to Silverstrike Arrow (pulls 3 and 19) and once each to Void Expulsion, Corrupting Essence, and Bursting Emptiness shows broad mechanic awareness gaps. This is their first night back in the roster — expect improvement, but P1 survival needs to be the focus.

5. What's Working: P2 is Clean and the Late Session Was Excellent

P2 is essentially solved — only 0.45 actionable deaths per pull, and the raid passes through it consistently when they enter with 20 alive. The last 5 pulls of the night all reached P3, averaging 6:08 duration. Nucke and Silencio had zero actionable deaths all night. DPS output is consistent (730K+ raid DPS on P3 pulls). The fundamental execution is there — the kill will come from cleaning up the 30-60 seconds around P1 Bursting Emptiness and the Intermission transition.
