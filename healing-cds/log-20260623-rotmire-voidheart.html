Boss Mechanic Timeline

Rotmire is a single-phase fight with repeating ~136s mechanic cycles. Fungal Bloom (100 energy) is the primary healing check — it revives add corpses as Bursting Shrooms that must be killed within 16s. Festering Vines roots players in expanding damage circles. Bursting Pustules is the soft enrage, adding Rotting Pustules stacks each cycle.

Actionable Items

Power Word: Shield severely undercast: 7 vs reference 65
PW:Shield is a critical Atonement applicator between ramps and provides absorb healing. 여우신선 cast it 65 times (7.6 CPM) while Voidhéart cast it only 7 times (0.9 CPM). Every unused PW:Shield is a missed Atonement and missed absorb value. This is the single largest CPM gap in the profile.
Penance CPM 7.9 vs reference 10.4 (-24%)
Penance is the highest-priority rotational ability for Disc Priest. It should be pressed on cooldown. 여우신선 averaged 10.4 CPM. At 7.86 CPM, Voidhéart is leaving Penance idle for extended periods. Each missed Penance is ~5 Atonement healing events lost.
Shadow Word: Death underused: 2.6 vs reference 4.1 CPM
Shadow Word: Death is the highest single-hit damage spell for Disc and drives the most Atonement healing per GCD during ramp windows. 여우신선 used it 35 times. Voidhéart used it 21 times. Casting SW:Death during ramp windows significantly amplifies burst Atonement healing.
Bloom #2 at 4:16: No ramp before damage
Zero PWR casts and no Evangelism before Bloom #2. The cast log shows Voidhéart was casting Penance and reactive Flash Heals instead of setting up Atonements beforehand. PWR casts appear at +10s, +11s — after the damage already hit. This turns a proactive spec into a reactive one, wasting Disc Priest's core strength.
Bloom #3 at 6:32: Incomplete ramp (1 PWR before damage)
Only 1 PWR cast(s) before Bloom #3. A full ramp needs 2-3 PWR before Evangelism to have 10-15 Atonements active when damage hits. The late ramp reduces throughput on the final Bloom cycle where damage is highest due to Bursting Pustules stacks.
HPS gap: 141,039 vs reference 235,806 (60%)
Voidhéart's HPS is 60% of the top reference. Key contributors to the gap: PW:Shield usage (7 vs 65 casts), Penance CPM (7.86 vs 10.35), and incomplete ramps on Blooms #2 and #3. Fights 1-2 show 20-25K HPS (non-Disc spec), confirming this was the first pull as Disc Priest. Context matters: the kill was achieved and overheal is healthy at 25.2%.
