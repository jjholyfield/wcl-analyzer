Actionable Improvements

1. Survive Echoing Darkness in Phase 1 (5 deaths)
Echoing Darkness from Vorelus hits every 2 seconds for 25–35K per tick. All 5 deaths occur between 1:03 and 1:55. Sonic needs to use Dark Pact (1 min CD, absorb shield) at the start of Vorelus's active window (~0:40), and Unending Resolve (3 min CD, 40% DR) if HP drops below 50% during the damage. Healthstone should be available for emergency top-offs. Burning Rush (self-damage) should be toggled OFF during heavy Echoing Darkness overlap. Sonic took 2,978K damage from Burning Rush across these pulls — that is self-inflicted damage during a survival crisis.
2. Survive the Intermission (3 deaths)
Silverstrike Barrage during Intermission (2:22–2:52) killed Sonic 3 times at 2:31–2:42. This is sustained unavoidable AoE. Plan: arrive at Intermission above 80% HP. Use Dark Pact at 2:20 (just before Stellar Emission channel begins at 2:22). If UR is available (second charge or first use was pre-1:00), overlay it. Demonic Circle pre-placed for positioning. There are no targets to hit during Intermission, so survival is the ONLY job.
3. Handle Cosmic Barrier (5 deaths)
Cosmic Barrier killed Sonic 5 times across P2 and P3 (3:41–6:22). This is 12.3M total damage absorbed from 417 hits. This mechanic creates a barrier that must be broken — standing inside it or being caught in the AoE pulse is lethal. Positioning matters: stay at range during Cosmic Barrier casts and focus damage on breaking the barrier. Use Demonic Gateway to reposition quickly if caught.
4. Stop falling off the platform (4.8M falling damage)
22 instances of falling damage totaling 4.8M is substantial and completely avoidable. This is likely related to knockback mechanics or poor positioning near platform edges. Demonic Circle should be placed in a safe spot and used to recover from knockbacks. Demonic Gateway (used 2 times in fight 25) can also serve as a safety net. Awareness of platform edges during movement-heavy phases (P2 Rift Slash, P3 Dimensional Slash) needs to be higher.
5. Increase defensive cooldown usage throughout the fight
On the longest pull (6:22 alive), Sonic used Dark Pact only 2 times and Unending Resolve only 2 times. Dark Pact is a 1-minute CD — it should be used 5–6 times in a 6-minute fight. Unending Resolve is 3 minutes — use it on pull for Echoing Darkness and again during the P2 transition. Plan defensives around known damage windows: Echoing Darkness (P1), Silverstrike Barrage (Intermission), Void Expulsion (P1/P2), and Cosmic Barrier (P2/P3). Proactive use before damage events is far more effective than reactive use after HP drops.

The path to improvement is straightforward: use Dark Pact and Unending Resolve proactively around known damage events, stop taking falling damage, turn off Burning Rush during heavy damage windows, and respect Cosmic Barrier positioning. Fixing survival will naturally improve DPS by extending uptime into later phases where the fight is actually decided.
