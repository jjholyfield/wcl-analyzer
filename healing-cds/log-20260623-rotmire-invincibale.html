Actionable Improvements

1
Talent Build: Switch to Stormbringer
The single largest DPS improvement available. Every top-5 Elemental Shaman on Mythic Rotmire runs Stormbringer (46-50 talent differences from current build). The Stormbringer build with Voltaic Blaze, heavy Lava Burst, and Chain Lightning cleave does 90-107% more DPS on this boss. Rotmire's frequent add spawns (every ~65 seconds) make Chain Lightning cleave and Earthquake spam extremely valuable. The current build lacks Voltaic Blaze entirely and under-uses Chain Lightning (4.7 CPM vs 8.9 CPM reference). Switching builds is the single change that would bring performance from the ~111K range into the ~180K+ range.
Impact: Estimated +80-100K DPS based on the build's inherent strength on this encounter.
2
Movement Uptime: Cast Through Festering Vines
Average CPS during Vines windows is 0.48 vs reference's 0.81 — a 41% deficit. This is ~116 seconds of fight time (7 Vines events x 12s each = 84 seconds) where significant casts are being lost. Specific fix: Pre-plan Spiritwalker's Grace for every other Vines (alternate with Astral Shift for defensive ones), and prioritize instant casts during Vines — Lava Burst procs, Tempest, Earth Shock/Elemental Blast as Maelstrom dumps, Flame Shock refresh only if needed. The worst window (Vines #2 at 1:32, only 3 casts in 12 seconds) should have been 8-10 casts.
Impact: ~30 additional casts during Vines windows, equivalent to roughly +3-5K DPS.
3
Reduce Flame Shock Over-Application
64 Flame Shocks in 8:09 (7.9 CPM) is far too many. With an 18-second duration, you should only need a Flame Shock refresh roughly every 14 seconds on a single target (accounting for pandemic), which would be ~35 applications over 8 minutes. The 30% of casting gaps that follow a Flame Shock cast confirm this is a habitual pattern — Flame Shock is being used as a "something to press" during transitions rather than being reserved for when it is actually expiring. Specific fix: Only refresh Flame Shock when the debuff has 6 seconds or less remaining. On target switches to adds, apply it once then switch to damage abilities. Never use Flame Shock as a gap-filler.
Impact: ~29 GCDs reclaimed (64 casts - 35 needed = 29 excess), each one a potential Lava Burst, Lightning Bolt, or spender.
4
Reduce Dead Time Between Casts
145 seconds of total dead time (29.7% of the fight) across 40 gaps longer than 2.5 seconds. This is substantial — nearly 2.5 minutes of the 8:09 fight where no rotational ability was pressed. The gap pattern is evenly distributed across abilities (12 after Lightning Bolt, 12 after Flame Shock, 7 after Lava Burst) rather than concentrated after one spell, suggesting general "thinking time" between casts rather than a specific rotation confusion point. Specific fix: Practice the ABC rule — Always Be Casting. If uncertain what to press next, Lightning Bolt is always correct as a filler. Pre-plan the next GCD during the current cast. The goal is to keep gaps under 1.5 seconds (one GCD delay at most).
Impact: Recovering even half of the 116 estimated lost casts would add ~58 casts, equivalent to +5-8K DPS.
5
Use Combat Potions
No pre-pot and no second potion were used. A DPS potion on pull (aligned with Time Warp + Ascendance) and a second potion during a late Ascendance window would provide a small but free DPS increase. This is the lowest-effort improvement available.
Impact: ~2-3K DPS depending on potion type.

Summary Verdict

The DPS gap is primarily build-based, with execution improvements available

Invincibale's 111K DPS vs the 210-229K range of top Elemental Shamans is a 48-52% gap. The dominant factor is talent build selection — every top player runs Stormbringer with a fundamentally different ability priority that generates dramatically more damage from Rotmire's constant add spawns. Within the current build, execution is reasonable: cooldowns are used on CD, add handling is solid through most of the fight, defensive usage is clean, damage intake is minimal, and DPS variance across pulls is very low (4.2%).

The execution improvements (movement uptime during Vines, Flame Shock reduction, dead time reduction, potions) would add an estimated 10-15K DPS within the current build. The build switch to Stormbringer is the transformative change that would close the remaining ~90-100K gap.

  • Consistency: 107K-112K across 3 pulls, only 4.2% variance. Very repeatable.
  • Survivability: Zero deaths across the kill, clean damage intake, no avoidable mechanic failures.
  • Cooldown discipline: Ascendance and Stormkeeper used on cooldown throughout. No wasted charges.
  • Add handling (early-mid fight): Matches or beats reference on add phases #1-6.
  • Defensive usage: 6 Spiritwalker's Grace and 4 Astral Shifts well-spaced throughout the fight.
  • Pull opener: Strong first 35 seconds — 1.07-1.38 CPS in opening quiet windows.
