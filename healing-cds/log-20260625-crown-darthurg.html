Actionable Improvements

1
Reserve a Defensive for Intermission Orbiting Matter
3 of 4 actionable deaths came from Orbiting Matter during Intermission.
The Intermission starts at ~2:14 with Stellar Emission + Silverstrike Barrage. Orbiting Matter projectiles overlap this survival window. Darthurg has Exhilaration (self-heal), Survival of the Fittest (20% DR), and Aspect of the Turtle (immune) available. Plan to use at least one of these specifically for the Intermission overlap -- do not spend Turtle in late P1. The deaths occurred at 2:32, 2:38, and 2:43, all tightly clustered around the same mechanic window.
2
Late-Session Focus: Last 5 Pulls Had All 4 Actionable Deaths
0 actionable deaths in pulls 1-14, then 4 in pulls 24-28.
The first 14 pulls showed strong survival -- Darthurg only died to cascade in every case. In the final 5 pulls, he became one of the first 2 to die 4 times. This pattern suggests fatigue or reduced focus late in the 3+ hour session. Consider taking a short break or doing a mental reset for Intermission mechanics after long prog sessions. The mechanical demands of the Intermission do not change -- only the player's attention to them.
3
P2 Void Barrage Awareness
1 actionable death to Void Barrage at 3:10 in F27.
Void Barrage is cast by Undying Voidspawn in P2 and is a frontal that must be dodged. As a ranged DPS, Darthurg should maintain distance from the Voidspawn while DPSing adds. Disengage is available for quick repositioning if caught in the telegraph. This was a single occurrence, so it may have been a positioning lapse rather than a recurring issue, but it is worth noting since it was the only non-Intermission actionable death.
4
DPS is Not the Bottleneck -- Survival Is
Best pull DPS (106.9K) matches mid-range reference kills (103K-117K) at 3 ilvl lower.
The Marksmanship rotation is being executed well. P1 DPS of 225K average is strong, and the 4 deep pulls that reached P3 all had consistent alive-DPS in the 102-107K range. There is no rotation optimization that would meaningfully change this fight's outcome. The progression bottleneck is surviving the Intermission cleanly and getting more reps in P2/P3 to build familiarity. Every additional pull that makes it through Intermission alive is more P2/P3 practice time.
