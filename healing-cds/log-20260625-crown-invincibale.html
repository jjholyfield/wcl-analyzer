Actionable Improvements

Prog priorities: SURVIVAL > MECHANICS > DPS optimization. Invincibale's core story is not individual failure (only 2 actionable deaths out of 25) — it is surviving longer through cascade raid damage in P2/P3 and tightening P2 DPS consistency.

#1 — SURVIVAL
Bursting Emptiness Positioning
Both actionable deaths were to Bursting Emptiness — expanding void zones that must be dodged. Fight 18 was the most concerning: Invincibale was the first player to die in the entire raid at 1:55 into a clean P1 pull. Zero chaos, zero pressure — a pure positioning failure on void zone mechanics.

Fix: Prioritize Void Droplet awareness during P1 and P2. Use Spiritwalker's Grace during Bursting Emptiness movement windows to maintain casting while repositioning. Pre-position away from void zones before they expand rather than reacting after the damage begins.
Eliminating these 2 actionable deaths directly improves raid consistency entering P2/P3.
#2 — SURVIVAL
Personal Defensives in P3
10 deaths in P3 across 8 attempts — all cascade, but surviving even 15–20 seconds longer extends learning time. Cosmic Barrier (3 deaths) is the top P3 killer. Very low Versatility (16 rating) means Invincibale takes significantly more damage than peers during raid-wide P3 pressure.

Fix: Use Astral Shift proactively during Cosmic Barrier casts in P3. Save Healthstone for P3 overlap windows. Earth Elemental can provide emergency damage reduction. Consider Nature's Guardian talent for passive survivability if not already taken.
Surviving 15–20s longer in P3 adds meaningful DPS and learning reps for the newest phase.
#3 — DPS
P2 DPS Consistency (30.9K avg vs 47.5K ceiling)
P2 DPS ranges from 16.2K to 47.5K — a 3x spread indicating highly variable execution. Best P2 pulls (F6: 47.5K, F28: 43.1K) show the potential when mechanics are handled cleanly. Worst P2 pulls (F18: 16.2K, F14: 22.2K) coincide with early deaths or heavy movement.

Fix: Maintain Flame Shock uptime during P2 movement (Rift Simulacrum transitions, Void Barrage dodging). Use Spiritwalker's Grace for Lava Burst casts during forced movement. Pre-position for Undying Voidspawn spawns. Save Stormkeeper for static P2 windows where full casts are possible.
Consistently reaching the P2 ceiling (47.5K) would raise average fight DPS by 5–8K.
#4 — DPS
P3 CD Alignment
P3 DPS improved from 155 (F2) to 34,097 (F28) through the session — natural learning curve. The jump from F26 (18K) to F28 (34K) shows rapid improvement.

Fix: Ascendance and Stormkeeper should be available for a second burst window at ~5:30 (entering P3). Aligning these CDs with a safe casting window in early P3 would front-load damage when the raid is still healthy. This will develop naturally with more P3 reps.
Natural improvement expected — P3 DPS should stabilize around 30–40K with more experience.
#5 — GEAR
Versatility Is Critically Low (16 Rating)
At 16 Vers rating, Invincibale has effectively zero passive damage reduction. Reference players range 52–103 Vers. While Mastery is the primary Elemental DPS stat, even modest Vers provides meaningful survivability — especially during P3 Cosmic Radiation/Cosmic Barrier overlaps where the spec's already low HP pool gets tested.

Fix: Look for gear upgrades that include some Vers without losing total stat budget. This is a long-term gearing priority, not an immediate fix. Even reaching 50–70 Vers rating would noticeably improve P3 survival.
Marginal per-instance but compounding across 7+ minute fights with constant damage intake.

Summary Verdict

What Is Going Well
  • P1 execution is strong. 116.8K average DPS with a 147.8K peak shows excellent opener and cooldown alignment with Ascendance + Stormkeeper.
  • Improving through the session. +6.6% DPS trend, best pull on the final attempt. No signs of tilt or fatigue.
  • Clean intermission survival. 11/11 intermissions survived with zero deaths. Handles Stellar Emission + Silverstrike Barrage cleanly.
  • Rapid P3 learning. P3 DPS tripled from early to late session (11.4K → 34.1K).
  • Low individual death responsibility. Only 2 of 25 deaths were actionable — 92% of deaths were cascade.
The Overall DPS Gap Is Phase-Weighted, Not Rotational
The 56K avg vs 98.5K reference gap is misleading. Reference players completed 7–8 minute kills with full P3 DPS and second CD cycles. The gap comes from:
  1. P2 inconsistency — 16K to 47K range vs likely steady 50K+ from references
  2. P3 unfamiliarity — only 5 meaningful P3 attempts so far
  3. Cascade death rate — dying in every pull reduces effective fight time and overall DPS

This is an experience and execution gap that will naturally close with more P2/P3 reps. It is not a fundamental rotation or talent problem. P1 numbers are already competitive.
