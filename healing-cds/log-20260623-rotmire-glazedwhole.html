Glazedwhole survived all three pulls including the kill, which is the most important metric for a tank on prog. However, DPS output is a significant concern at 69.6K vs the reference's 131.4K — a 47% deficit that cannot be fully explained by the 5-ilvl gap. The core issue is Death Strike CPM at 14.0 vs 18.4 (-24%) and Heart Strike CPM at 10.8 vs 15.7 (-31%), meaning fewer rune-spending abilities are being pressed per minute. Death Strike is the #1 damage ability for Blood DK (33% of player damage vs 50.5% for reference), so fewer casts directly impact both DPS and self-healing. Blood Boil is being overcasted at 12.0 CPM vs 7.4 (+62%), suggesting GCDs are going to Blood Boil instead of Heart Strike or Death Strike. Defensive CD usage was actually excellent — 13 Vampiric Blood casts across the 8:09 fight and Bone Shield was maintained throughout. Anti-Magic Shell was never used (0 casts vs reference's 3), which is a missed opportunity for free Runic Power generation on magic damage.

Actionable Items

1. Death Strike CPM: 14.0 vs 18.4 reference (-24%)
Death Strike is the #1 damage ability for Blood DK and the core self-heal. At 114 casts in 8:09 vs the reference's 114 casts in 6:12, the per-minute rate is significantly lower. Each missed Death Strike is lost damage, lost healing, and a lost Blood Shield absorb. Focus on converting Runic Power into Death Strikes faster — avoid sitting at high RP.
2. Heart Strike CPM: 10.8 vs 15.7 reference (-31%)
Heart Strike is the primary rune spender that generates Runic Power for Death Strike. 31% fewer Heart Strikes means less RP generated, which cascades into fewer Death Strikes. Reduce Blood Boil usage (12.0 CPM vs 7.4 reference) and replace those GCDs with Heart Strike when adds are not actively spawning.
3. Anti-Magic Shell: 0 casts (reference used 3)
AMS was never pressed in the entire 8:09 fight. On a ~45-second cooldown, it could have been used 10+ times. Rotmire has significant magic damage (Rotting Pustules, Fungal Bloom, Bursting Pustules). Each AMS use absorbs damage, prevents debuffs, and generates bonus Runic Power — which means more Death Strikes. The reference absorbed 1.2M through AMS alone. This is the highest-impact single-button fix available.
4. Blood Boil Overcasting: 12.0 CPM vs 7.4 reference (+62%)
Blood Boil is being used nearly twice as often as the reference. While it is needed for AoE threat on Awaken Fungi adds and to maintain Blood Plague, 98 casts in 8:09 suggests it is being used as a filler instead of Heart Strike. Blood Boil should be used for charge management (2 charges) and AoE windows, not as a default GCD.
5. Icebound Fortitude: 1 cast in 8:09 (could have used 2-3)
IBF is a 3-minute CD that reduces all damage by 30% for 8 seconds. With only 1 use at 6:46, there was room for at least 1-2 more uses earlier in the fight. Pair IBF with Putrid Fist hits that land during Fungal Bloom or Bursting Pustules windows for maximum value.
6. Strengths: Survival, Bone Shield, Vampiric Blood discipline
Glazedwhole survived all 3 pulls — including both wipes where 22-23 other players died. Bone Shield was maintained for the entire fight without dropping. Vampiric Blood was used 13 times (approximately on cooldown for the entire 8:09). Tank swap pattern with Nucke was clean and well-coordinated. These fundamentals are solid.
