Actionable Improvements

1 Survive Simulacrum Backlash — 3 deaths (highest single cause)
Rift Simulacrum's Cosmic Barrier must be broken before the Backlash detonation kills anyone in range. Baodabao died to this in F19 (4:57), F21 (4:36), and F24 (4:38) — all mid-to-late P2. Fix: Pre-position further from the Simulacrum spawn. Use Shimmer to instantly gap-close if caught in the detonation zone. If the barrier is not going to break in time, Ice Cold (available every pull) or Ice Barrier should be up before the Backlash fires. This single mechanic accounts for 21% of all deaths.
2 P2 DPS uptime — 64% drop from P1
P2 average of 26.6K vs P1 average of 74.6K is a massive falloff. While some drop is expected (fewer multi-targets, more movement), the best P2 pulls (F27: 42.8K, F23: 40.1K) prove 40K+ is achievable. The worst P2 pulls (F11: 7.8K, F3: 10.6K) are 5x lower than the best. Fix: Frost Mage should leverage Shimmer to maintain Ray of Frost channels during movement. Pre-position for Void Expulsion and Rift Slash to minimize displacement. Use Frozen Orb on cooldown as passive damage during forced movement. The DPS improvement from P2 consistency alone (getting worst pulls from 8K to 25K+) would raise overall averages by ~15%.
3 Voidstalker Sting handling — 2 deaths
Died to Voidstalker Sting in both F15 (4:32) and F16 (4:46). This is a targeted debuff from Alleria that deals ramping damage. Fix: When targeted by Voidstalker Sting, immediately Ice Barrier and consider Ice Cold if stacks are high. Coordinate with healers for external cooldowns. This debuff is predictable — watch for the cast and pre-shield.
4 P3 positioning — 2 melee deaths
Two deaths in P3 were from Melee (F18 at 5:53, F28 at 5:26), meaning Baodabao was in melee range of an enemy. P3 has Vorelus returning with Ravenous Abyss and Rift Simulacrum adds — getting caught between multiple enemies is lethal. Fix: Maintain maximum range in P3. As a ranged DPS with Shimmer, there is no reason to be in melee range. Watch for add spawns and preemptively reposition. Frost Mage has the tools (Shimmer, Slow Fall) to stay mobile and safe.
5 Defensive cooldown discipline — only 5 Ice Barriers in 6:05
Ice Barrier is a 25-second cooldown absorb shield. In a 6-minute fight, it could theoretically be used 14+ times. 5 uses suggests it is only being pressed reactively when health drops, not proactively before known damage events. Fix: Pre-cast Ice Barrier before every Grasp of Emptiness, Void Expulsion, and Stellar Emission. During P2 and P3, keep it rolling on cooldown — the absorb will smooth incoming damage and prevent the sudden deaths that account for all 14 pull deaths. With 0 Versatility, proactive shielding is even more critical.
