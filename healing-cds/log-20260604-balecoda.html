How Augmentation Evoker Works

Augmentation Evoker is a support DPS spec — your job is to buff your allies’ damage through Ebon Might while dealing your own damage through Eruption and Living Flame. The spec’s damage shows up partly on your meter and partly on the people you’re buffing, so your personal DPS is a direct reflection of how well you’re maintaining buffs AND pressing your own damage buttons.

Ebon Might is the core buff — it empowers your 4 nearest DPS allies. You cast it to apply it, and it extends automatically when you cast Eruption, Living Flame, Fire Breath, or Upheaval. The key is to NEVER let Ebon Might fall off. When you’re recasting Ebon Might at 1.9/m vs the #1’s 1.1/m, that means it’s falling off and you’re spending globals re-applying it instead of dealing damage.

Eruption is your #1 damage button — it hits hard, extends Ebon Might, and should be pressed as often as possible. Living Flame is your filler that also extends Ebon Might. Fire Breath and Upheaval are empowered abilities with longer cooldowns.

Prescience is your secondary buff — a crit chance increase on allies. Cast it pre-pull and on CD during the fight. The gap between good and great Aug Evokers is almost entirely Eruption frequency and Ebon Might uptime. More Eruptions = more damage + longer Ebon Might = more ally damage.

How It’s Affecting Your Damage

Eruption at 71% of #1 is the single biggest DPS loss. 16.1/m vs 22.8/m means you’re missing ~7 Eruptions per minute. Each Eruption deals direct damage AND extends Ebon Might — fewer Eruptions means less personal damage, shorter Ebon Might uptime, and less damage from the 4 DPS you’re buffing. This one gap cascades into everything else.
Living Flame at 58% of #1 compounds the problem. 7.2/m vs 12.5/m is a massive filler gap. Where are those globals going? Some are going to Verdant Embrace (off-healing) and excess Ebon Might recasts. Every Living Flame you’re not casting is damage lost AND an Ebon Might extension you’re not getting.
Ebon Might at 173% of #1 confirms the buff is dropping. You’re recasting Ebon Might 1.9 times per minute vs the #1’s 1.1. That means your Eruption and Living Flame frequency is too low to keep the buff extended, so it falls off and you spend a global re-applying it — a global that should have been an Eruption or Living Flame that would have kept it up in the first place. It’s a vicious cycle.
Verdant Embrace at 0.6/m is stealing globals from damage. The top Aug Evokers don’t off-heal. Every Verdant Embrace is a global that could have been an Eruption or Living Flame. You have 4-6 healers in the raid — trust them to do their job.

What You Need to Change

1
More Eruptions — Target 22+ Per Minute
You’re at 16.1/m, the #1 is at 22.8/m. This is the #1 priority by a wide margin. Eruption is both your main damage ability and your primary Ebon Might extension — it’s the single most important button you press. Every Eruption you’re missing costs damage for you AND the 4 allies you’re buffing. Cast it any time you have essence.
2
More Living Flame — Target 12+ Per Minute
You’re at 7.2/m, the #1 is at 12.5/m. Living Flame is your best filler between Eruptions — it deals solid damage and extends Ebon Might. Stop spending globals on Verdant Embrace and Ebon Might recasts. If there is nothing else to press, press Living Flame. Never stand still.
3
Stop Off-Healing
Verdant Embrace at 0.6/m is stealing damage globals. The top Aug Evokers cast zero off-healing abilities. Trust your healers. Every Verdant Embrace you remove becomes an Eruption or Living Flame that extends Ebon Might and deals real damage.
4
Health Pot Every Pull
8/30 (27%) is one of the worst on the team. Health pots are on a completely separate cooldown from your pre-pot DPS potion. There is no reason to not use one. Free survivability that costs you nothing.
5
Let Ebon Might Fix Itself
If you execute on priorities 1-3, your Ebon Might recast rate will drop on its own. More Eruptions + more Living Flame = more Ebon Might extension = fewer recasts needed. The 1.9/m recast rate is a symptom, not the root cause. Fix your damage rotation and the buff uptime follows.
