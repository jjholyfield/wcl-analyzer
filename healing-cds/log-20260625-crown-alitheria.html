Plan P3 Defensive Rotation for Bursting Emptiness
Impact: P3 survival is the ceiling for the kill.
Bursting Emptiness killed Alitheria in P3 on F6, F14, and F17 (cascade deaths, but still indicate vulnerability). Impact: P2 DPS ranges from 59.8K to 112.6K (88% spread).

4
Optimize Avenging Wrath Timing Across Phases
Impact: Maximize 2-min cooldown windows.
On a 7+ minute fight, Avenging Wrath (2-min CD) should fire 3-4 times: on pull (P1 burst), ~2:30 (late P1/early Inter -- may be wasted if used in Inter), ~4:30 (mid P2), and ~6:30 (P3). If Alitheria dies at 5:30-5:41 (F14, F24), the 3rd window is truncated or lost entirely. On the best pull (F2: 53K, death at 6:40), all three windows likely completed. Getting three full AW windows consistently is the single largest DPS gain available and directly ties to surviving deeper into P3.
5
Get More Reps
Impact: 8 missed pulls = reduced fight familiarity.
Alitheria was absent from 8 of 19 pulls. Players who saw all 19 had nearly twice the practice time for mechanic recognition. Phase reach rates (53% to Intermission, 42% to P3) will improve with more reps.
