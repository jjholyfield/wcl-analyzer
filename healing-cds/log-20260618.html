What Needs to Change

1. P2 Wall: Simulacrum Backlash is the #1 P2 Killer (38 deaths)

Rift Simulacrum's Backlash kills more people in P2 than any other single ability. The raid needs a clear interrupt/focus-target rotation for the Simulacrum or better positioning to avoid the backlash. This single mechanic accounts for 20% of all P2 deaths.

2. P1 Bleed: 8.8 Deaths Per Pull Before 2:14

The raid averages 8.8 deaths in P1 — before mechanics even ramp up. Bursting Emptiness (38) and Void Remnants (35) are avoidable ground effects. The first death occurs in P1 in 23/27 pulls. When the first death is in P1, the pull averages 3:03. When it is not, the pull averages 5:33. Cleaning up P1 execution would add 2+ minutes to every pull.

3. Intermission Deaths: Silverstrike Barrage (18 deaths)

The intermission is losing 3.1 players per pull that reaches it. Silverstrike Barrage is a repeating volley that requires spreading and movement. Better CD rotation during Stellar Emission channel and awareness of Orbiting Matter positioning would cut intermission deaths significantly.

4. First Death Offenders: Alitheria (5x), Invincibale (4x), Dueche (3x), Unholyftw (3x)

These four players die first in 15/27 pulls combined. The first death triggers a cascade that shortens the pull by an average of 2:30. Alitheria dying first 5 times (mostly in P1 around 0:40) is the highest individual impact on pull longevity.

5. What's Working: Clear Session-Over-Session Improvement

The raid is learning. Pull duration nearly doubled from early to late session. P3 was first reached on pull 14 and hit consistently in the final pulls. The two best boss % attempts (0.01% on fights 5 and 10) came in the first half, suggesting the raid has the DPS to reach near-zero boss HP when execution lines up. Night 3 should focus on making P1 and intermission cleaner to reach P2 with a full 20-person roster.
