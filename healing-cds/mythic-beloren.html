Mythic Belo’ren, Child of Al’ar — Full Raid Guide

Four Dads One Pizza — MW / RSham / HPal — 3 healers

Single boss, two-phase fight with Flames intermissions. Voidlight Convergence (~10M raid hit) fires every ~50s during normal phases — swaps player colors (Light/Void). Light Flames + Void Flames intermissions deal ~13M/10s sustained for ~30s — the primary healing check. Each Rebirth applies Ashen Benediction (permanent 10% healing reduction), making each intermission harder than the last. Radiant Echoes orbs touching the boss is an instant wipe on Mythic. ~4:20 fight.

Color Assignment (Light / Void)

Voidlight Convergence assigns every player either Light Feather or Void Feather. Colors swap every VLC. You soak mechanics matching your color — you take reduced damage from same-color hits.

Rule: Soak your color. Dodge the other. On Mythic, even same-color mechanics hit hard if multiple people stack — one person per soak.

Key Mechanics

Voidlight Convergence

~10M raid-wide hit that swaps all player colors. Fires at 0:00, 0:50, ~2:10, ~3:00. Creates orb circles around the boss. Stop soaking orbs a few seconds before VLC — the color swap will one-shot soakers who are mid-soak when it fires.

Radiant Echoes

Wall of Light and Void orbs traveling across the room in horizontal or vertical lines. Matching-color players create gaps by standing in the path (50% DR). On Mythic, any orb touching Belo’ren is an instant raid wipe. Immune players (bubble, netherwalk) clear orbs near the boss first.

Horizontal pattern: Tank creates gap toward Yellow marker, soakers plant there.
Vertical pattern: Tank creates gap toward Red marker, soakers plant there.
The pattern that appears first in Phase 1 repeats for all of Phase 2.

Infused Quills (Mythic)

Targeted quills that splash on Mythic. Only one person should get hit per cast — everyone else dodges the circle, even if they match the color.

Light Dive / Void Dive

Must be soaked by matching-color players. Spawns pools and adds with Light Blast / Void Blast (interruptible, but only by matching-color players). Send 6 players per soak (2 targets, 2 healers, 2 ranged) and use personals.

Guardian’s Edict (Tanks)

Tank must soak the correct color or boss gains a permanent stacking 20% damage buff. Face boss toward pillar at Green marker. During Radiant Echoes, the matching-color tank creates gaps in orb lines.

Death Drop

Knock-up at ~1:20 and ~3:30 triggering each intermission. ~3M damage.

Embers of Belo’ren / Rebirth

Boss crashes and enters egg phase during intermissions. Clear surrounding orbs before they touch the boss. Egg phase is the main DPS burn window (~30s). Goal: push to 0% as boss finishes 3rd VLC to skip a 3rd Embers wave.

Ashen Benediction

Each Rebirth applies a permanent 10% healing reduction to the entire raid. After two intermissions, all healing is reduced by 20%. This is why Intermission 2 is the wall — same Flames damage, 20% less healing.

Burning Heart

Constant raid-wide ticking damage (~2M per 10s). Always happening, makes everything else harder.

Wipe Conditions

  • Radiant Echoes orb touches boss — instant raid wipe
  • Guardian’s Edict missed — boss gets permanent 20% damage buff per miss
  • Phoenix egg respawns — failed DPS check, typically wipes
  • Soaking wrong color during VLC swap — one-shots the soaker
  • Missing add interrupts — one of the biggest wipe reasons

Damage Profile & Sources
Fight Structure (~4:20)

Phase 1 (0:00-1:20): Two Voidlight Convergences (~10M each at 0:00 and 0:50). Burning Heart ticks constant ~2M/10s. Light/Void Dive, Echo, Edict as moderate fillers.

Intermission 1 (1:20-2:10): Death Drop at ~1:20 triggers intermission. Light Flames + Void Flames deal ~13M/10s sustained for ~30 seconds. Ashen Benediction hits at ~2:00 as transition back.

Phase 2 (2:10-3:30): Two more Voidlight Convergences (~2:10 and ~3:00). Same Burning Heart + filler pattern as Phase 1.

Intermission 2 (3:30-4:10): Same as Intermission 1 — Death Drop, then 30s of Flames at same intensity.

Kill Push (4:10+): Kill happens during or shortly after Intermission 2.

Damage spikes (from 5 ranked kills):
VLC: ~10M raid-wide at 0:00, 0:50, 2:10, 3:00
Flames: ~13M/10s sustained at 1:30-2:00 and 3:35-4:05
Burning Heart: ~2M/10s constant throughout
Death Drop: ~3M at 1:20 and 3:30
Ashen Benediction: ~3M at 2:00
