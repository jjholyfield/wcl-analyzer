P2 Performance: Solid

109.5K avg DPS across 12 pulls in P2 is where Nurnyx does the most consistent damage. The range (73K-136K) is wide, but the low-end pulls (fight 7 at 73K, fight 15 at 87K) both had Nurnyx dying during P2 itself, cutting active time short. On pulls where Nurnyx survived the full phase, DPS was consistently above 100K. Rift Simulacrum and Undying Voidspawn targets provide good multi-DoT opportunities for Shadow.

Intermission: 2 of 4 Actionable Deaths

Fights 24 and 25 saw Nurnyx die during or shortly after the Intermission as one of the first 2 raid deaths. Fight 24: killed by Silverstrike Barrage at 2:37. Fight 25: killed by Orbiting Matter at 2:38. These are the deepest pulls of the night, meaning Nurnyx's early death cost the raid DPS for the remaining 5+ minutes. Use Dispersion or Fade (with defensive talent) to survive through the Intermission burst.

P3 Performance: Volatile, Raid-Dependent

P3 avg of 49.6K has a massive spread (9K-93K). The best pull (fight 11, 93K) had Nurnyx alive for 2:20 into P3 with the raid mostly intact. The worst (fight 1, 9K) had Nurnyx die just 30 seconds into P3 while the raid was already collapsing. All 7 P3 deaths were cascade. P3 performance is entirely a function of how long the raid stays alive around Nurnyx.

P3 Reach Rate Is The Key Metric

Only reaching P3 alive in 35% of pulls (7/20) is below average for this roster. This is largely because Nurnyx dies during P2 in 5 pulls and during the Intermission in 3 pulls. Improving Intermission survival alone would add 2-3 more P3 appearances and significantly increase Nurnyx's overall DPS contribution.

Actionable Improvements

1

Survive the Intermission (Highest Impact)

Nurnyx died during or just after the Intermission in 3 of 15 pulls that reached it (fights 22, 24, 25). The fight 24 and 25 deaths were both actionable (first 2 to die) and occurred on the two longest pulls of the night. On fight 25 (7:56 total), Nurnyx died at 2:38, missing 5:18 of potential DPS. Use Dispersion (90% DR, 6s duration) during the Silverstrike Barrage cast in the Intermission. Desperate Prayer gives an on-demand health buffer. Fade with defensive talent provides additional damage reduction. These three tools together should make the Intermission survivable even without external healing.

Impact: Surviving Intermission on those 2 pulls would add ~5 minutes of DPS time across 2 pulls. At 80K active DPS, that is roughly 24M additional damage contribution to the raid.
2

Survive Through P2 — Defensive Cooldown Planning

Nurnyx died during P2 in 5 of 12 pulls that reached it. While most were cascade deaths, fight 7 was actionable (Grasp of Emptiness at 4:05). The P2 phase features Void Remnants, Grasp of Emptiness, and Bursting Emptiness as primary threats. Plan Dispersion for the most dangerous P2 overlaps. Save Desperate Prayer for emergencies. Use Angelic Feather proactively for repositioning during Grasp of Emptiness.

Impact: Each additional P2 survival adds ~2 minutes of P3 DPS time. Surviving P2 in 2 more pulls would add ~4M damage.
3

Increase Psychic Link Damage Contribution

Psychic Link was 2.0% of Nurnyx's damage on the best pull vs 6.3% for the reference. This is Shadow's primary passive cleave mechanic. In P3 where multiple targets are active, Psychic Link damage should naturally increase. Since most of the gap is explained by limited P3 time (only 7 pulls reached P3, vs the reference completing a full kill), this should improve naturally as survival improves. However, make sure to keep DoTs spread on multiple targets when available to maximize Psychic Link procs.

Impact: Increasing Psychic Link to reference levels would add ~4% total damage on pulls where P3 is reached.
4

Avoid Falling Damage

Fight 11 showed 2 "Falling" events for 366K total damage. This comes from being knocked off the platform. Position closer to the center during mechanics that can displace, and use Angelic Feather to recover position quickly if displaced. This damage is entirely preventable.

Impact: 366K damage is a small amount, but the positioning displacement that caused it likely also caused DPS downtime.
5

Positive Trend — Keep Progressing

DPS improved by 21% from the first half to the second half of the session. The best two pulls (fight 11 at 71K, fight 17 at 62.5K) came in the second half. This positive learning curve is exactly what you want to see during progression. The rotation and ability usage are correct (ability mix closely matches the reference). The primary path to higher DPS is surviving longer, which will come with more P2/P3 experience.

Trend: Learning the fight. Active DPS when alive (~80K) is already at 73% of reference kill DPS.
