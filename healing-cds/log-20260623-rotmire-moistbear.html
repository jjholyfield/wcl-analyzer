Actionable Improvements

1. Replace Single-Target Rip with Primal Wrath During Add Windows

Impact: +30-40 Primal Wrath casts, estimated +15-20% add damage, +5-8% overall DPS

Moistbear used Primal Wrath only 7 times vs the reference's 39 (0.9 vs 4.8 CPM, -82%). During every Awaken Fungi window, the reference spends combo points on Primal Wrath to apply Rip to all nearby adds simultaneously rather than using single-target Rip (19 casts). This is the single largest execution gap. Every add window with 2+ targets should prioritize Primal Wrath as the finisher — it applies Rip to all targets in range. Only use single-target Rip on the boss when no adds are present.

2. Increase Rake Usage Across All Targets

Impact: +26 Rake casts, estimated +5-8% overall DPS

Rake CPM is 5.2 vs reference's 8.4 (-39%, 42 vs 68 casts). Rake should be maintained on the boss at all times AND applied to every add target during Fungi windows. Rake is a builder that snapshots Tiger's Fury damage, meaning Rakes applied during Tiger's Fury deal increased damage for their entire duration. The reference is multi-dotting Rake on every add as they spawn, which also generates combo points for Primal Wrath finishers. During each add window: Rake each add target, then spend combo points on Primal Wrath.

3. Reduce Bear Form Usage

Impact: ~8-10 recovered GCDs, estimated +2-3% DPS

4 Bear Form swaps (at 0:48, 4:42, 5:01, 7:24) vs the reference's 2. Each swap costs 2-3 GCDs (Bear Form + potential Frenzied Regen + Cat Form back). Barkskin (1min CD, already used well at 5x) and Survival Instincts (used only 1x) should cover most damage intake without needing Bear Form. Save Bear Form for true emergencies where you would otherwise die. The reference manages survival with fewer defensive form swaps.

4. Maintain Uptime During Festering Vines

Impact: ~15-20 recovered casts, estimated +3-4% DPS

5 of 7 Vines windows show CPS below reference, with the worst at -38% (Vines #7, 7:31). Pre-position before Vines goes out so you don't have to move far from the boss. If targeted by Vines, use Wild Charge to quickly return to boss range after placing the circle. The reference maintains near-full uptime during most Vines windows, suggesting they minimize displacement rather than running far.

5. Tighten Post-Convoke Transitions

Impact: ~4 x 4.5s = 18s recovered, estimated +2% DPS

All 4 Convoke casts are followed by a 4-5 second gap before the next ability. After Convoke channels, immediately resume the rotation — the next priority is typically a Tiger's Fury-buffed Rip refresh or Ferocious Bite at 5 combo points. The reference transitions out of Convoke within 2-3 seconds.

Context & Verdict

Moistbear's 100.2K DPS on the Mythic Rotmire kill represents 53.4% of the rank #1 reference (187.8K). After accounting for external buff differences (no PI received vs reference's 4x PI, offset by Aug Evoker buffs Moistbear did receive), roughly 40% of the gap is execution-based with only 2 talent differences and identical item level.

The two dominant issues are Primal Wrath underuse (-82% CPM) and Rake underuse (-39% CPM). Together these represent the core of the DPS gap: the reference multi-dots Rake across all add targets and uses Primal Wrath as their primary AoE finisher, generating far more damage during the 8 Awaken Fungi windows that define this fight. Moistbear is playing a single-target rotation on a fight that demands heavy multi-dotting.

What's already good: Cooldown alignment is textbook — Berserk and Convoke are paired correctly every use and Tiger's Fury is used on cooldown. Add switch timing is competitive (within 1-2s of reference). Interrupt contribution is outstanding (8 Skull Bashes vs reference's 2). Barkskin timing is proactive and well-placed before Fungal Blooms. The kill itself was clean with zero deaths after dying in both wipe pulls, showing clear learning and adaptation.

Priority fix: Adopt the Primal Wrath + multi-Rake AoE pattern during add windows. This single change would address the largest gap in the profile.
