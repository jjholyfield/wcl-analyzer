Data & methodology (click to expand)
Source: Pulled rankings from WCL API on Mythic Vorasius. Found teams running our exact comp (HPal / RSham / MW / PEvo). Analyzed top 5 by kill time (293s–339s). Per-player sourceID queries for every healer's complete cast timeline. CDs discovered from data (any ability cast ≤8 times = potential CD). All timers verified against actual cooldown durations.

Key findings:
• Blisterburst 1 is THE healing check — all major CDs stack here (Asc 5/5, SLT 4/5, Rewind 4/5, AM 3/5, Revival 3/5)
• Boss Phase 2 covered by 2min throughput CDs (AW, DF, Yu'lon, CC)
• Blisterburst 2 is lighter — 3min CDs still on CD. CC + DF fill the gap
• Kill push: all 3min CDs come back
• AM is 3min — goes Blisterburst 1 with 2nd use at kill push
• No RSham runs HTT (0/5). Ascendance universal.
• 3/5 MW run Yu'lon, 2/5 run Restoral. 2/5 PEvo run Zephyr.

Healer CDs (from data):
HPal: AW (2min, 5/5), AM (3min, 4/5), BoSac (reactive), LoH (reactive)
RSham: Ascendance (3min, 5/5), SLT (3min, 5/5) — 0/5 HTT
MW: Revival (3min, 3/5), Yu'lon (2min, 3/5), CC (90s, 5/5), Life Cocoon (reactive)
PEvo: Rewind (3min, 5/5), DF (2min, 5/5), TtS (~90s, 5/5) — 2/5 Zephyr

Top damage sources: Primordial Power 68M (constant DoT), Dark Energy 28M (beam ticking), Shadowclaw Slam 23M (raid splash), Blisterburst 22M (burst — #1 CD target), Parasite Expulsion 20M, Primordial Roar 11M.
