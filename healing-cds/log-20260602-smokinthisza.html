How Marksmanship Hunter Works

Marksmanship Hunter is a ranged spec built around the Aimed Shot → Precise Shots → Arcane Shot cycle. You cast Aimed Shot or Rapid Fire to generate Precise Shots procs, then immediately spend those procs with Arcane Shot (single target) or Multi-Shot (AoE). This weaving pattern is the core of the spec.

Aimed Shot is your hardest-hitting ability — keep it on cooldown. When it fires, it generates Precise Shots, which makes your next Arcane Shots deal more damage and cost less Focus. Always spend Precise Shots before casting another Aimed Shot or you waste the proc.

Rapid Fire is your secondary cooldown — it channels rapid hits, generates Precise Shots, and builds Bulletstorm stacks. Press it on cooldown.

Trueshot is your major CD (~90s) — increases crit, reduces ability CDs, and guarantees procs. Use it on cooldown. Volley works with Double Tap for burst — sequence them properly (don’t overlap Volley with Trueshot cast).

Steady Shot is your low-priority filler when nothing else is available.
