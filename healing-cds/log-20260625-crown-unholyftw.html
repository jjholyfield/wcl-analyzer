Actionable Items

1 Investigate the F14 P1 DPS Drop
Fight 14 (the deepest pull at 7:12) had P1 DPS of 247.6K — 33% below the 370.2K full-P1 average. Every other full P1 was 335K+. Possible explanations: holding Army of the Dead for P2, an early disruption to the opener sequence, or being displaced by a mechanic before building Festering Wound stacks. The pull still reached deep P3, so this may have been an intentional cooldown management adjustment. Review the opener on that specific pull to determine if cooldowns were delayed or disrupted.
2 P3 Target Uptime and AMS Usage
P3 DPS ranges from 2.2K to 88.7K with high variance across 6 pulls. The 88.7K (F17) shows what is achievable; the average of 54.5K has room to climb. Focus areas:
• Maintain Virulent Plague on all targets during Cosmic Radiation overlaps.
• Use Epidemic for Runic Power spending during multi-target windows instead of Death Coil.
• Use Anti-Magic Shell proactively on Cosmic Radiation ticks — this absorbs damage while generating Runic Power, turning a defensive into a DPS cooldown.
• More P3 reps will naturally improve familiarity. The team has only reached P3 in 42% of pulls (8/19).
3 Cooldown Alignment Through Intermission
The 39-second intermission (no targets) presents a cooldown alignment opportunity. The ideal pattern:
• Army of the Dead (90s) early in P1 → available again for P2 start (~2:53).
• Dark Transformation (45s) on pull, then ~45s, then ~90s → naturally realigns for P2.
• If cooldowns are being held past their natural timers to "save for later phases," the P1 DPS variance (335K-444K range) could partially explain itself. Prioritize using cooldowns on time — Unholy DK burst windows (Army + DT + Putrefy) lose significant value when delayed.
4 Survival Is Already a Strength — Maintain It
With only 1 actionable death in 19 pulls, Unholyftw is one of the most mechanically reliable players on the roster. The single early death was Grasp of Emptiness on pull 2 at 17 seconds — it did not recur. Death Knight defensives (AMS, Icebound Fortitude, Lichborne) are being used effectively. Do not sacrifice survival for DPS — the priority at this stage of progression is clean P3 execution, and staying alive is the prerequisite.

Progression context: Top-ranked Unholy DK kills average ~101K DPS over a full 7-8 minute fight. At ilvl 292 (highest of all compared references), there is a gear advantage that should help.
