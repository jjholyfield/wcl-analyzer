Actionable Improvements

Prioritized by impact. These are the changes that would close the gap to the reference players.

1. Cast more Arcane Shot as Focus dump
Smokinthisza averaged 3.4 Arcane Shot/min vs Dunkël's 6.1/min — a 44% deficit and the single largest DPS gap driver. The 17 missing Arcane Shots represent ~3.0M lost damage (~4.3% of total output). When Aimed Shot, Black Arrow, Rapid Fire, and Explosive Shot are all on cooldown or you have excess Focus, Arcane Shot is the correct filler. It appears Multi-Shot was used in some of these spots instead (9 casts vs 0 from reference) — on single-target, Arcane Shot always wins.
2. Eliminate Multi-Shot on single-target
9 Multi-Shot casts on a fight where the reference cast 0. Multi-Shot is only worthwhile when multiple targets are stacked (add phases for Trick Shots). Using Multi-Shot as a Focus dump instead of Arcane Shot is a direct DPS loss per cast. This is likely a habit from dungeons where Multi-Shot is the default. Action: Arcane Shot as default filler, Multi-Shot only during Awaken Fungi add phases.
3. Get one more Wailing Arrow cast
3 casts vs reference 4. Wailing Arrow deals significant damage (~800K per cast) and has a ~45s cooldown. Over a 7:58 fight, 4 casts is achievable. The missing cast is worth approximately 800K damage (1.1% of total). Check if Wailing Arrow was delayed by Trueshot alignment — it should be used on cooldown unless a Trueshot window is within 5 seconds.
4. Reduce post-Black Arrow gaps
31 gaps >2.0s after Black Arrow (vs reference 24). Black Arrow is instant-cast, so there should be no natural GCD delay. The 7 extra gaps suggest occasional Focus starvation or decision-making hesitation after pressing BA. Action: Queue the next ability (Aimed Shot or Arcane Shot) immediately after Black Arrow. If Focus is low, use Steady Shot to regenerate.
5. Continue improving survivability
Dying in 17/20 pulls is the dominant progression issue. On the kill fight, defensive usage was good (4x Exhilaration, 4x SotF, Turtle at the end). The progression from "dying every pull" to "surviving the kill" shows learning. Key: pre-use Survival of the Fittest before Fungal Bloom rather than reactively, and maintain range from the boss to avoid melee hits (12 melee hits taken = positioning too close at times).
