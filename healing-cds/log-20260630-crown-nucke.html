Actionable Items

1. Exceptional Survivability -- Zero Deaths
Zero deaths across 20 pulls including 11 P3 attempts. This is the most important tank metric on prog. Nucke never caused a cascade wipe, never consumed a battle rez, and maintained consistent threat throughout every attempt. The DTPS trend also shows improving mitigation -- dropping from 50K+ early to 38.3K on the deepest pull.
2. Strong Defensive CD Discipline
100% Demon Spikes usage across all phases. 100% Fiery Brand and Metamorphosis usage in P1 and P2. The defensive rotation is clearly internalized. Darkness is deployed strategically as a raid CD in P1 (45% of pulls) and P3 (64% of pulls), showing awareness of when the raid needs it most.
3. Immolation Aura CPM (-38% vs Reference)
2.6 CPM vs Melvabeam's 4.2 CPM. Immolation Aura generates Fury and deals passive AoE damage. Pressing it closer to on-cooldown is the single largest DPS gain available with no defensive tradeoff. Target: 3.5+ CPM.
4. Soul Cleave Frequency (-20% vs Reference)
10.8 CPM vs 13.5 CPM. Soul Cleave is both a primary damage spender and self-heal. More Fury (from #3) enables more Soul Cleave casts, improving both DPS and survivability. The reference gets approximately 3 extra Soul Cleaves per minute.
5. Consider Talenting Fel Devastation and Vengeful Retreat
The reference uses Fel Devastation (10 casts, 3.4% of total damage) and Vengeful Retreat (7 casts, mobility). If the talent trade-off is acceptable defensively, adding Fel Devastation alone would close approximately 1,500-2,000 DPS of the gap to the reference. This is a build decision, not a rotation error -- 4 total talent differences between Nucke and the primary reference.
6. Sigil of Flame CPM (-47% vs Reference)
0.9 CPM vs 1.7 CPM. Sigil of Flame is a free AoE damage ability on a short cooldown. Pressing it closer to on-cooldown adds consistent damage with no defensive cost. Estimated gain: 500-700 DPS.
7. Clear DPS Improvement Trend
DPS rose from 28.8K on pull 1 to 43.8K on pull 25 -- a 52% increase through the session as the fight became more familiar. The reference averages 47.9K on a kill. With the CPM improvements above (Immolation Aura, Soul Cleave, Sigil of Flame), Nucke should comfortably reach 45K+ DPS when the kill attempt arrives.
