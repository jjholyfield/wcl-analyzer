Actionable Improvements

1. Replace hardcast Rip with Primal Wrath on AoE. Primal Wrath applies Rip to all nearby enemies and triggers Rampant Ferocity AoE from subsequent Ferocious Bites. Currently 12 casts vs reference's 36. Increasing Primal Wrath usage and reducing Rip hardcasts from 20 to ~16 would unlock Rampant Ferocity damage (estimated 6,400K+ over the fight, approximately 13.4K DPS recovery). This is the single largest improvement available and accounts for approximately 20% of the total DPS gap.
2. Remove Moonfire from the rotation entirely. 25 Moonfire casts = 25 wasted GCDs. Every Moonfire should be a Shred instead (generates combo points, higher per-GCD damage). At Moistbear's average Shred damage of ~40.9K per cast, replacing 25 Moonfires with Shreds would gain approximately 1,023K damage directly, plus additional combo points for ~5 more finishers. Estimated impact: 4–5K DPS gain.
3. Increase overall CPM from 50.0 to 54+. The 8.6% CPM gap means approximately 37 missed casts over the fight. At Moistbear's average damage per cast of ~131K, that is roughly 4.9M lost damage (10.2K DPS). Focus areas: less downtime between ability presses, faster target switching after adds die, maintaining melee uptime during Vines movement. The CPM gap is distributed across Shred (-8%), Ferocious Bite (-13%), Rake (-23%), and Swipe (-26%).
4. Improve Rake uptime / application on adds. Rake is 23% behind reference (36 casts vs 49). Rake is Feral's strongest per-combo-point ability and should be applied to every target in range. On add spawns, tab-Rake all adds before using Shred as combo point filler. Each additional Rake application adds bleed damage and generates a combo point.
5. Gear toward Mastery over Haste. Moistbear has 879 haste vs 474 (reference) and 1,760 mastery vs 2,318 (reference). Mastery (Razor Claws) directly scales all bleed damage, which is 40%+ of Feral's total output. Swapping haste-heavy pieces for mastery-heavy pieces would increase the per-tick value of Rip, Rake, and Feral Frenzy. This is a longer-term gear optimization — the rotation fixes above will have larger immediate impact.
Context & Positives

The 37.5% DPS gap is primarily execution-based. Gear differences (mastery distribution) account for perhaps 5–8% of the gap; the remaining 30%+ is rotation. On the positive side: Moistbear survived the full fight while 8 other raiders died. Tiger's Fury and Feral Frenzy usage match reference perfectly (within 4%). Berserk and Convoke timings are correct (4 of each, matching reference). Interrupt contribution is outstanding (12 Skull Bashes, triple the reference). The core rotation structure is there — the issues are specific and fixable: Primal Wrath priority, dropping Moonfire, and general button pressing speed.

The raid lost 8 players over the course of the fight (first death at 3:08, three cluster deaths at 6:07 and 7:38). Late-fight performance (post-6:00) should be contextualized: fewer players alive means more mechanics to handle and less Aug Evoker uptime (Zinnks died at 6:40). Moistbear's ability to survive the full fight while maintaining rotation is a meaningful contribution to the kill.
