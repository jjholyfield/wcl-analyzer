Actionable Improvements
1. Address 35-43% DPS Gap to Raid Peers

Cybrosaen is producing 34K-41K DPS on full-duration pulls while the other two Unholy DKs in the raid are doing 50K-67K. This is the single biggest improvement area. At 289 ilvl, the gap is too large to be gear alone. Review:

  • Dark Transformation alignment with Army of the Dead (should be every other DT window)
  • Putrefy usage during Dark Transformation windows (only 2.3 CPM aggregate)
  • Death Coil vs Epidemic decision-making in single-target vs cleave situations
  • Lesser Ghoul stack management before burst windows
2. P2 DPS Needs Significant Improvement

P1 burst (avg 227K) is competitive, but P2 sustained damage (avg 87K) drops 62%. While some drop is expected from the phase transition, the gap to peers suggests lost uptime during Rift mechanics. Focus on maintaining GCD usage during Rift Simulacrum and Undying Voidspawn add phases -- target switching should be faster, and Death and Decay should be placed to cleave both boss and adds.

3. Cooldown Alignment Through Phase Transitions

With Dark Transformation on a 45s CD, it should be available for every major damage window. Army of the Dead (90s CD) should align with every other DT window. Check that cooldowns are being used on CD rather than held -- during prog, cooldown usage on CD is more valuable than saving for specific timings you haven't practiced yet. 35 total DT casts across 34.7 minutes = 1.0 CPM (theoretical max ~1.33 CPM).

4. Survival is Already Strong

Only 1 actionable death across 8 pulls is excellent for progression. Cybrosaen is consistently among the last to die in wipes, which means mechanical awareness and defensive usage (Anti-Magic Shell, Icebound Fortitude) are solid. The focus should be entirely on DPS output improvement rather than survival.

5. Scourge Strike Usage is High -- Verify Festering Wound Management

11.6 CPM on Scourge Strike is the highest ability by cast count. Verify that Festering Strike is being used enough to maintain Lesser Ghoul stacks (2.7 CPM may be low). The rotation priority should ensure you have 4-6 stacks before entering Dark Transformation windows, and Putrefy should be prioritized during DT.

Context

The 35-43% DPS gap to the other Unholy DKs is primarily execution-based. At 289 ilvl (comparable to raid peers), the gap is too consistent across pulls to be attributed to gear or RNG. The stable DPS numbers suggest a systematic rotation issue rather than occasional mistakes.

What is going well: Survival is excellent (1 actionable death in 8 pulls). P1 burst windows are competitive. Anti-Magic Shell usage shows awareness of incoming magic damage. The player is reaching the same phases as the rest of the raid consistently.
