Actionable Improvements

1 Reduce Casting Gaps After Drain Soul
Impact: 30 of 67 casting gaps (45%) follow Drain Soul, totaling ~90s of dead time. The reference has half as many gaps from Drain Soul (16/31).
Fix: Queue your next ability before Drain Soul finishes channeling. If you need to move for Festering Vines or add repositioning, cancel the channel and immediately cast an instant (Agony refresh, Haunt, or Seed of Corruption) rather than waiting for the channel to complete then moving in silence. On Rotmire, movement is frequent enough that finishing every Drain Soul channel is not realistic.
2 Replace Manual Corruption Casts with Seed of Corruption
Impact: Sonic cast Corruption 11 times manually. The reference cast it 0 times, relying entirely on Seed of Corruption to apply and spread Corruption. This is 11 GCDs spent on single-target Corruption application instead of AoE Seed detonations.
Fix: On Rotmire, adds are present for over half the fight. Use Seed of Corruption to both apply and spread Corruption to all targets simultaneously. Manual Corruption should only be used on single-target windows where no adds are alive and Seed would have no secondary targets. Seed of Corruption was 25% of total damage -- increasing its cast rate from 12.8 to 16+ CPM would significantly close the gap.
3 Maximize Dark Harvest Value with DoT Spread Timing
Impact: Both players cast Dark Harvest 11 times, but the reference dealt 7.3x more damage with it (15.4M vs 2.1M). Dark Harvest damage scales with active DoT effects on targets in its area.
Fix: Delay Dark Harvest by 2-3 GCDs after add spawns until Seed of Corruption has detonated and spread Corruption to all adds, and Agony has been applied to at least 2-3 priority targets. Casting Dark Harvest into a single target with only 1-2 DoTs active wastes its potential. Aligning Dark Harvest with full DoT spread windows would recover the majority of the 13.3M damage gap on this ability alone.
4 Improve Uptime During Fungal Bloom Windows
Impact: CPS during all three Fungal Bloom windows averaged 0.71 vs reference's 0.91 -- a consistent 22% gap. These are the highest-damage moments in the fight and also when Bursting Shrooms spawn (requiring immediate AoE).
Fix: Pre-position near the center of the group before Fungal Bloom to minimize post-knockback movement. Have Seed of Corruption pre-cast or immediately ready to cast on the Bursting Shrooms that spawn. The reference maintains near-full CPS through Blooms because they pre-position and immediately Seed the shroom pack.
5 Maintain Agony on Priority Adds
Impact: Agony CPM was 6.8 vs the reference's 9.3 -- a 27% gap. Agony is the primary Soul Shard generator, and on Rotmire with constant adds, multi-dotting Agony on 3-5 targets feeds more Shards for more Seed of Corruption and Unstable Affliction casts.
Fix: During Awaken Fungi windows, apply Agony to the Sporecap and 2-3 Shroomlings/Funglings after getting Seed rolling. Each Agony tick on a new target has an independent chance to generate a Soul Shard. More Shards = more Seeds = more AoE damage. The reference prioritizes multi-dotting Agony during every add phase.

Context

The raw 39.1% DPS gap between Sonic (136.7K) and the rank 1 reference Inphected (224.6K) is misleading without context:

  • Power Infusion: Sonic received 0 PI casts, the reference received 4. This alone accounts for an estimated 5-8% of the gap.
  • Fight duration: Sonic's kill was 8:08 vs the reference's 7:20. Longer fights accumulate more Bursting Pustules stacks (soft enrage), increasing raid damage taken and reducing effective DPS uptime in the final minutes.
  • Raid composition: The reference raid killed the boss 48 seconds faster, meaning less time spent at high Pustule stacks and fewer mechanic cycles to handle.

After accounting for these factors, the execution gap is closer to 25-30%, primarily driven by:

  • 199.6s of casting dead time vs 97.3s for the reference (102.3s excess, ~70 lost GCDs)
  • Dark Harvest alignment with DoT spread (13.3M damage gap on one ability)
  • Manual Corruption casts replacing Seed of Corruption (11 wasted GCDs)
  • Lower Agony multi-dotting on adds (6.8 vs 9.3 CPM)
