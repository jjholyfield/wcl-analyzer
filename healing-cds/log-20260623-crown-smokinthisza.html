Actionable Improvements

CRITICAL Survive Cosmic Barrier in early P2

7 deaths to Cosmic Barrier, 6 of them in the first 70 seconds of Phase 2 (3:00-3:43). This is a positional mechanic — the barrier creates a damage field that ticks for 24-25K per second. Use Survival of the Fittest at the P2 transition (~2:53) and pre-position away from barrier spawn locations. If a barrier catches you, use Disengage or Aspect of the Cheetah immediately to escape. The reference player uses Aspect of the Turtle for one barrier overlap — Smokinthisza never used Turtle in any observed pull.

Impact: Eliminating these 6 early-P2 deaths would increase phase reach rate from 20% to potentially 50%+ for P3.

CRITICAL Use Aspect of the Turtle

Aspect of the Turtle (full immunity) was not cast in any recorded pull. Morganalter used it once in their kill. For a player dying in every single pull, Turtle is a free extra life that can completely negate Cosmic Barrier ticks, Voidstalker Sting damage, or any other lethal overlap. With a 3-minute cooldown, it should be available at least twice per pull. Map it to a specific danger window: the first Cosmic Barrier in P2 (~3:00) and late P2/early P3 Voidstalker Sting overlap.

Impact: One Turtle per pull could prevent 50%+ of P2 deaths. This is the single highest-value change available.

HIGH Echoing Darkness positioning in P1 (resolved mid-session)

4 deaths to Echoing Darkness, all between 1:13-1:17 when Vorelus is active alongside other sentinels. This mechanic hits every 3 seconds — the damage is periodic, not a one-shot. After fight 14, no more Echoing Darkness deaths occurred, suggesting Smokinthisza learned the positioning. Continue to maintain range from Vorelus during the multi-sentinel overlap and pre-use Survival of the Fittest if caught in the AoE.

Impact: Already improving. 0 Echoing Darkness deaths in the final 6 pulls.

HIGH Press Steady Shot between ability procs

Steady Shot CPM is 3.1 vs the reference's 7.6 — a 59% gap. Steady Shot is the Focus generator that should be pressed whenever Aimed Shot, Black Arrow, and Rapid Fire are all on cooldown and no Precise Shots procs are available. The gap suggests significant dead time between ability casts. During movement, use Steady Shot as it can be cast while moving with the Steady Focus talent. This is the biggest rotational issue.

Impact: Increasing Steady Shot to 5+ CPM would provide ~30% more Focus generation, enabling more Aimed Shots and Black Arrows.

MEDIUM Voidstalker Sting management in P2/P3

4 deaths to Voidstalker Sting across fights 18, 19, 23, and 25. This debuff ticks for ~27K per second and becomes lethal when overlapping with Cosmic Radiation or other P3 damage. Use a personal defensive (Survival of the Fittest or Turtle) when targeted by Voidstalker Sting during high-damage overlap windows. The fact that these deaths occur on the longest, best pulls means this becomes the next progression wall after Cosmic Barrier is solved.

Impact: Addressing this will be critical for P3 progression and eventual kill attempts.
