Actionable Improvements

P1 Use Dispersion Proactively for Known Damage Events

Dispersion was used once in the longest pull (6:44). It has a 2-minute cooldown and provides 90% damage reduction for 6 seconds. This is the most impactful change Voidhéart can make. Plan Dispersion for: (1) Every Intermission Silverstrike Barrage window (~2:14-2:53), (2) Cosmic Barrier in P2 when stacks get high, (3) Any overlap moment where multiple mechanics fire simultaneously. At 2 min CD, it should be available 3 times in a 7-minute pull. Currently using it less than once. Estimated impact: Would directly prevent 6+ of the 19 deaths (Silverstrike Barrage x2, Cosmic Barrier x3, Bursting Emptiness x1).

P1 Use Desperate Prayer on Cooldown During Damage

Desperate Prayer was used once in 6:44. It is a 1.5 min CD instant heal that increases max HP. Shadow Priests have limited self-healing outside of Vampiric Embrace — Desperate Prayer is the primary reactive heal. Use it any time health drops below 50%, especially during Echoing Darkness ticks in P1, Stellar Emission in Intermission, and Cosmic Barrier in P2. Expected uses in a 7-minute fight: 4.

P2 Dodge Corrupting Essence in Phase 1 (2 Deaths at ~1:08)

Corrupting Essence killed Voidhéart in fights 22 and 25 at nearly the same time (1:07 and 1:08). This is a consistent positional failure — the mechanic fires at the same time every pull. Fix: Pre-position before the ~1:05 mark to avoid the Corrupting Essence source. This is a free death to remove because the timing is 100% predictable.

P2 Plan Intermission Survival Rotation

4 of 19 deaths occurred during the Intermission (2:14-2:53). The Intermission has no DPS targets — it is pure survival. Build a personal survival plan: Dispersion for Silverstrike Barrage burst, Desperate Prayer when health is low, Fade for movement speed (Phantasm), and pre-shield with Power Word: Shield if available. Surviving the Intermission is a binary gate — failing it means zero P2 contribution.

P3 DPS Execution is Not the Problem — Survival Is

Voidhéart's P1 DPS (228.6K avg) is strong, and the best full-fight DPS (112.2K in F25) is competitive with the top 5 Shadow Priests worldwide despite a 4-ilvl disadvantage. The ability rotation looks correct: SW:Madness and Mind Blast are the top two damage sources (33% combined), DoTs are maintained, Voidform is used on CD, Halo is pressed regularly. There are no major rotation fixes needed. The entire DPS gain from here comes from staying alive. Each additional 30 seconds of survival adds ~7M damage to the raid's total.

Summary Assessment

Voidhéart's performance gap is 100% survival-based, not execution-based. P1 DPS is competitive. Ability priority is correct. CDs are used on schedule (Voidform every 2 min, PI synced). The problem is dying in 19 of 20 pulls due to a combination of: (1) severely underused personal defensives (Dispersion, Desperate Prayer), (2) repeated positional failures with Corrupting Essence at ~1:08, and (3) no survival plan for the Intermission Barrage window.

What is already good: P1 DPS execution is strong and improving (+13.7% over the session).
