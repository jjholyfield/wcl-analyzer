Actionable Items

Zero Early Deaths -- Tank Survivability Is Not the Problem
Glazedwhole was never one of the first 2 deaths in any pull across 20 attempts. On the 5 deepest pulls, he was consistently among the last 2-3 players alive. This is the #1 priority for a prog tank and he's executing it cleanly.
Death Strike CPM: 11.1 vs Reference 15.1 (-26%)
Death Strike is Blood DK's primary self-heal and the core of the spec's survivability. 88 casts in 7:56 vs Nolandk's 128 in 8:28 means fewer self-heals per minute. This is partly offset by using more Blood Boil (10.6 vs 6.7 CPM) which is runic-power-neutral -- shifting some Blood Boil GCDs to Heart Strike would generate more Runic Power for additional Death Strikes.
Heart Strike CPM: 7.2 vs Reference 11.2 (-36%)
Heart Strike is the primary Runic Power generator. At 7.2 CPM vs 11.2, Glazedwhole is getting ~36% fewer Heart Strikes, which directly reduces available Runic Power for Death Strikes. Meanwhile, Blood Boil is at 10.6 CPM vs reference 6.7 -- some of those Blood Boil GCDs should be Heart Strikes instead when only 1-2 targets are in range.
Dancing Rune Weapon: 3 Uses vs Reference 6 in Similar Duration
DRW grants 20% bonus parry and doubles Bone Shield stack generation. On a 2-minute cooldown, 3 uses in 7:56 means significant uptime was left on the table. The reference used it 6 times in 8:28 -- essentially on cooldown. Notably, Glazedwhole used 0 DRW in P3 despite it being the hardest phase.
Anti-Magic Shell: 5 Uses vs Reference 8 -- More Needed in P3
AMS is roughly a 1-minute cooldown and provides massive magic damage reduction. Only 1 AMS was used in P3 despite heavy magic damage from Cosmic Barrier (26% absorption), Cosmic Radiation (3.4% absorption), and Voidstalker Sting. These low absorption rates on magic damage suggest AMS should be prioritized for those hits.
P3 CPM Drops 31% vs P1 -- Movement Costing Uptime
Total CPM drops from 69.9 in P1 to 47.9 in P3. While some drop is expected due to overlapping mechanics requiring movement, a 31% reduction is significant. Death and Decay usage drops from 3.6 to 2.2 CPM and Vampiric Strike from 7.6 to 4.3 CPM. Maintaining GCD uptime during P3 chaos will improve both DPS and self-healing.
Melee Absorption at 56.1% -- Strong Active Mitigation
Bone Shield and Blood Shield are absorbing over half of all melee damage (12.5M absorbed out of 22.3M raw melee). This indicates good Bone Shield uptime and effective Death Strike shielding on the highest-volume damage source.
