Silencio casts 130 Regrowths across the fight vs Tigrane's 212. This is the single biggest throughput difference. In the current Restoration Druid patch (12.0.7), the Abundance cycle rewards chaining Rejuvenation into Regrowth — after Tranquility or Convoke extend HoTs, Regrowth becomes free-mana and 100% crit. Tigrane achieves nearly double the Regrowth spam, translating directly to the 67K HPS gap.

Strong Wild Growth Usage (4.17 vs 2.12 CPM)

Silencio casts Wild Growth nearly twice as often as the reference. This could indicate over-reliance on Wild Growth for sustained healing instead of the more efficient Regrowth-chain approach. However, Wild Growth is a strong spell — check if the extra casts are during actual damage or blanket-casting during downtime.

Actionable Items

1. Regrowth Spam is the Core Throughput Driver

130 Regrowths (15.96 CPM) vs Tigrane's 212 (26.40 CPM) — 40% fewer casts of the spec's primary throughput spell. After Tranquility or Convoke extends HoTs, Abundance stacks make Regrowth free and always crit. This is the biggest single change that would close the HPS gap. During Bloom windows and their aftermath, prioritize Regrowth spam over additional Rejuvenation applications.

2. Swiftmend More Frequently (3.68 vs 4.61 CPM)

Swiftmend feeds the entire ramp cycle: it triggers Power of the Archdruid (3 Rejuv targets), extends HoTs by 8 seconds, and provides instant healing. 30 casts vs 37 — roughly one missed Swiftmend per minute. Using it on cooldown keeps more HoTs rolling and enables more Regrowth chain opportunities.

3. Re-evaluate Tranquility Timing

Tranquility at 0:10 (10 seconds into the fight, before any significant damage) resulted in 68.1% overheal on Tranquility overall. The reference player (Tigrane) uses Tranquility at 2:05 (responding to Bloom #1) and 6:36 (Bloom #3). Save Tranquility for Bloom windows where it extends a full set of pre-applied HoTs and raid health is actually low.

4. Consider Keeper of the Grove Hero Talents

Silencio runs Wildstalker (Symbiotic Blooms), while the top-ranked Resto Druids (including Tigrane) run Keeper of the Grove. Keeper of the Grove significantly enhances AoE healing and cooldown power in raid environments. This is a meaningful throughput difference for fights like Rotmire with heavy raid-wide damage.

5. Address 44.7% Overall Overheal

44.7% overheal vs 35.8% reference. Key contributors: Tranquility (68.1% OH — pre-fight cast wasted), Lifebloom (64.6% OH — partially normal), Swiftmend (55.5% OH — using on full-health targets). Reducing overheal means more mana and more effective throughput per GCD. Focus Regrowth and Swiftmend on targets that are actually injured.
