Scourge Strike Deficit

Scourge Strike is the primary rune spender and core rotational ability for Unholy DK. Unholyftw averaged 16.7 CPM vs Pickltoez at 24.4 CPM — a 32% deficit, which translates to roughly 35 missing Scourge Strike casts over the fight length. Part of this gap is explained by the 21 Outbreak casts consuming GCDs that should have gone to Scourge Strike. The remaining gap (~14 casts) suggests periods of downtime or inefficient resource management between rune regeneration cycles.

Outbreak Over-casting

Outbreak applies Virulent Plague to all nearby enemies. It should be cast once per target (the DoT does not stack), then refreshed by Scourge Strike / Festering Strike via Pestilence. Unholyftw cast Outbreak 21 times (2.6 CPM) vs Pickltoez's 1 cast (0.1 CPM). That is 20 wasted GCDs — time that should have been spent on Scourge Strike or Festering Strike. Every unnecessary Outbreak is a rune spender that does not generate meaningful damage.

Graveyard Usage

Graveyard is a significant damage source contributing 9.1% of Pickltoez's total damage (7,144K). Unholyftw cast it only 8 times (1.0 CPM) vs Pickltoez's 30 casts (4.4 CPM) — a 77% deficit. Graveyard contributed just 3.6% of Unholyftw's damage (2,209K). This is a major missed opportunity and likely the single biggest contributor to the DPS gap after Scourge Strike.

Festering Scythe + Epidemic

Festering Scythe at 1.8 CPM vs 3.2 CPM (−45%) and Epidemic at 0.6 CPM vs 1.3 CPM (−52%) suggest Unholyftw is not maximizing AoE during add phases. Pickltoez squeezes nearly twice as many Festering Scythe procs and Epidemic casts, which adds up especially during Rotmire's frequent Shroomling spawns where cleave damage matters.

Actionable Improvements

Ranked by estimated DPS impact. Fixing the top 3 items would recover an estimated 40-50K DPS, closing the majority of the gap to the reference.

1. Stop re-casting Outbreak — save 20 GCDs per fight (~10K DPS recovery)
Outbreak was cast 21 times vs the reference's 1 cast. Virulent Plague is maintained automatically by Scourge Strike and Festering Strike via Pestilence. Apply Outbreak once on pull and on new add spawns only. Every unnecessary Outbreak costs a Scourge Strike, which is ~30K raw damage per cast. Converting 20 Outbreak casts to Scourge Strikes recovers ~600K damage and frees runes for more productive spenders.
2. Increase Scourge Strike CPM from 16.7 to 24+ — core rotational uptime (~15K DPS recovery)
Scourge Strike at 16.7 CPM is 32% below the reference's 24.4. This is partly caused by Outbreak waste (item 1) but also indicates periods of GCD downtime. As the primary rune spender, Scourge Strike should be pressed on every available rune when other priorities (Festering Strike for wounds, cooldowns) are met. Target: 24+ CPM. An additional 35 Scourge Strikes across the fight would add approximately 35 × 30.5K = ~1,070K direct damage, plus Pestilence and Wound burst procs.
3. Dramatically increase Graveyard usage from 8 to 30 casts (~10K DPS recovery)
Graveyard contributed 9.1% of the reference's total damage but only 3.6% of Unholyftw's. At 1.0 CPM vs 4.4 CPM, this single ability's deficit accounts for ~4,935K lost damage (10.3K DPS). Graveyard should be used on cooldown, especially during add phases and Dark Transformation windows. This is the highest ROI fix — one button pressed 22 more times would close ~17% of the total DPS gap.
4. More Festering Scythe + Epidemic during add phases (~3-5K DPS recovery)
Festering Scythe at 1.8 CPM (vs 3.2) and Epidemic at 0.6 CPM (vs 1.3) indicate underutilized AoE. During Rotmire's frequent Shroomling spawns, prioritize Festering Scythe procs and use Epidemic to spend Runic Power when 3+ targets are stacked instead of single-target Death Coil. This would improve add phase contribution and Epidemic's total damage (currently 2,032K vs reference 3,089K).
5. Positioning for Festering Vines — survival priority
Died at 7:39 to Festering Vines (19 hits of damage taken). Multiple raid members died to Vines in this pull (3 at 6:07, 1 at 7:38). While late-fight deaths are less impactful since the boss was at <1%, improving Vines handling ensures more uptime on the boss in progression pulls where every second counts. Pre-position for root breaks and use Death's Advance or Death Charge to create distance before the root lands.
