Holy Word: Serenity Gap

Holy Word: Serenity is the single most impactful ability for a Holy Priest — instant-cast (with Apotheosis), massive single-target heal, and each cast reduces the cooldown of Apotheosis. Voidhéart casts it 40 times (5.0/min) vs Nax's 58 times (8.0/min). That is 18 fewer Serenity casts across the fight — at ~133K effective healing per cast, that is roughly 2.4M missed healing. During Apotheosis windows, HW:Serenity should be cast every GCD.

Benediction Under-Use

Benediction (Renew extension/application talent proc) is cast 11 times vs Nax's 31 times (−67%). Benediction feeds the Echo of Light mastery chain and sustains Renew on multiple targets. With fewer Benediction procs, Voidhéart's Renew uptime across the raid is lower (5,801K Renew healing vs 8,203K for Nax). Renew ticks heal passively, freeing GCDs for other spells.

Actionable Items

1. Cast Holy Word: Serenity more aggressively. This is the #1 throughput improvement available. Voidhéart cast HW:Serenity 40 times (5.0/min) vs the reference's 58 times (8.0/min) — a 38% gap representing roughly 2.4M missing effective healing. During Apotheosis windows, HW:Serenity should be cast every other GCD (alternating with Prayer of Healing). Outside Apotheosis, it should be used on cooldown on any target below full health. Each Serenity cast also reduces the cooldown of Apotheosis, creating a virtuous cycle.
2. Pre-activate Apotheosis before Bloom, not after. Across all 3 Blooms, Apotheosis was activated 4.1–4.8 seconds after damage hit. The reference activates it 4.3 seconds before. Blooms fire on a fixed timer (2:00, 4:16, 6:32). Start Apotheosis at 1:56, 4:12, and 6:28 so instant-cast Holy Words are already landing as damage hits. This window shift alone would improve burst throughput during the 3 most critical healing moments of the fight.
3. Pre-activate Power Infusion before Bloom. PI was consistently activated 2–8 seconds after Bloom damage. The reference activates PI 1–2 seconds before or at the moment of Bloom. Queue PI alongside Apotheosis at 1:56, 4:12, and 6:28. Combined with earlier Apotheosis, this stacks 20% haste on top of instant-cast Holy Words during peak damage.
4. Increase Benediction usage. Only 11 Benediction casts vs 31 from the reference (−67%). Benediction extends and applies Renew, which feeds Echo of Light mastery healing passively. Renew healing was 5,801K vs 8,203K (−29%). Using Benediction on cooldown during sustained damage windows adds passive throughput without costing GCDs on direct healing.
5. Add DPS fillers during low-damage windows. 12.2K DPS vs reference's 19.1K (−36%). Smite count is actually slightly higher (67 vs 53), so the gap comes from Halo damage (2,734K vs 4,504K) and missing Holy Nova/SW:Pain. If talents allow, adding Holy Nova for melee-range AoE damage during quiet windows and maintaining SW:Pain uptime on the boss would close the DPS gap and increase Searing Light passive healing.
