How Feral Druid Works

Feral Druid is a melee DoT/bleed spec played entirely in Cat Form. Your damage comes from maintaining bleeds on targets (Rake, Rip) while spending combo points on Ferocious Bite and Primal Wrath, and using Swipe as your primary builder.

The core loop: Rake to apply the bleed → Swipe to generate combo points → Primal Wrath (AoE bleed finisher, replaces Rip on multi-target) or Ferocious Bite (single target finisher) to spend combo points. On a council fight like LBV with 3 bosses, Primal Wrath is the primary finisher because it applies Rip to all targets.

Tiger’s Fury is your main CD — press it on cooldown for the damage buff and energy. Berserk/Frantic Frenzy is your major burst window. Stay in Cat Form the entire fight — shifting to Bear Form or Moonkin Form costs you GCDs and drops your DPS.

Swipe is your highest-volume ability — top players cast it 12–24 times per minute. It generates combo points on all nearby targets. Keep Rake up on all targets for the bleed damage and combo point generation from crits.

What You Need to Change

1
Evaluate whether hybrid Cat/Balance playstyle is outperforming pure Cat Form
Top-ranked Ferals on this fight use Cat Form exclusively and average 59 CPM vs 40.2. The 4.5 Balance ability casts per minute (Starsurge, Starfire, Starfall) are not used by any top Feral on this encounter. If this is an intentional hybrid build, check whether it’s yielding better results than going full Cat Form. If the Balance casts are a habit rather than a build choice, replacing them with Swipe, Rake, and finishers would significantly increase output.
2
Use healthstone every pull
0 out of 26 healthstones with a 31% early death rate. This is the worst HS usage on the team. A healthstone is an extra 500K+ HP on demand. With 8 deaths in 26 pulls, having a healthstone available would have likely saved multiple of those pulls.
3
More Swipe — target 15+/min
Swipe is at 6.7/min vs 17.8/min from top players. This is your primary combo point builder. Fewer Swipes means fewer combo points means fewer finishers. If you’re in Cat Form and nothing else is higher priority, press Swipe. Once the Balance abilities are gone, this number should climb naturally.
4
Maintain Rake on all targets
Rake is at 3.2/min vs 6.9/min from top players. On a 3-boss fight, Rake should be active on all three targets at all times. The bleed damage is a major portion of Feral output, and Rake crits generate combo points. Tab-target and refresh Rake before it falls off.
