Actionable Items

1. Fix P1 Bursting Emptiness Deaths

3 of 5 actionable deaths are to Bursting Emptiness (Void Droplet explosions) at 0:40 and 1:07. Two deaths at the exact same timestamp (0:40) across fights 12 and 18 suggest a repeatable positioning mistake during the first Droplet spawn. Use Divine Protection proactively when Droplets are about to detonate, and ensure positioning is away from the explosion radius. Eliminating this single mechanic failure removes 60% of actionable deaths.

2. Plan Aura Mastery Usage

Aura Mastery was used in only 13/20 pulls (65%). On a 3-minute CD, it should be available at least twice on any pull reaching P3. The timing varies wildly between pulls (sometimes 0:51, sometimes 1:06, sometimes 4:36, sometimes 6:56), indicating reactive usage rather than a plan. Assign AM to specific mechanics -- e.g., first use during Intermission Stellar Emission, second use in P3 Cosmic Radiation -- so it becomes muscle memory.

3. Increase Holy Shock CPM

11.1 Holy Shock CPM vs the reference 15.4 CPM. While Lightsmith and Herald rotations differ, Holy Shock is the #1 priority for both builds -- it generates Holy Power, triggers Infusion of Light, and is the core healing tool. Aiming for 13+ CPM would meaningfully increase both healing output and Holy Power generation for more Light of Dawn / Word of Glory casts.

4. Strong Foundation to Build On

Dueche is already the #1 healer on the team (159K avg HPS), has excellent CD discipline with Avenging Crusader (100% usage, 0:04 avg opener), healthy overheal (25.2%), and active DPS contribution (~10K avg). The Lightsmith build with Holy Bulwark absorbs is providing clean, efficient healing. The improvements needed are mechanical (dodging Bursting Emptiness) and procedural (planning AM timing), not rotational.

5. Budget Resources for P3

P3 HPS (143.7K) drops below P1 (182.5K) and P2 (161.8K). Lay on Hands is used 20 times in P1 but only 2 times in P3. Consider banking the second LoH charge for P3 on pulls that reach deep. Aura Mastery should be planned for P3 damage windows (only 5 of 17 total AM casts landed in P3). As the raid gets deeper into P3, having CDs available there will matter more than front-loading them in P1.
