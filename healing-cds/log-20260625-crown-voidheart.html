PW: Shield -- The Missing Piece

Voidheart averaged 0.39 PW:Shield casts per minute vs Sadboyhorse's 6.30 CPM. On fight 14: 4 casts vs 47 from the reference. PW:Shield is core to Disc in Midnight -- it applies Atonement, generates absorb healing, and feeds Divine Aegis. Sadboyhorse got 5.9M healing from PW:Shield alone (5.7% of total). This single gap likely accounts for the majority of the HPS difference. Coming from Shadow, PW:Shield may not be ingrained in the muscle memory yet.

Penance & SW:Death Under-use

Penance at 6.63 CPM (-34%) and SW:Death at 1.33 CPM (-60%). These are the primary Atonement damage sources -- fewer casts directly means less Atonement healing. The reference gained 11.2M from Penance healing vs Voidheart's 6.9M on fight 14. Part of the gap is explainable by Haste difference (918 vs 1342), which lets the reference fit more casts per minute.

SW:Pain Over-casting

2.78 CPM vs reference 1.61 CPM (+73%). SW:Pain only needs to be applied per target and refreshed on expiry. 26 casts in 7:12 is roughly every 16 seconds -- the DoT duration is much longer. Excess SW:Pain refreshes waste GCDs that should go to Smite, Penance, or PW:Shield. Cutting SW:Pain casts in half would free ~7 GCDs per fight minute for higher-value abilities.

Evangelism & PI Discipline

Actionable Items

1. PW: Shield Must Enter the Rotation

0.39 CPM vs 6.30 reference is the single largest rotation gap. PW:Shield applies Atonement, generates absorb healing, and feeds Divine Aegis. The reference gets 5.9M healing from PW:Shield -- Voidheart gets 0.5M. In the current Midnight Disc kit, PW:Shield should be woven between Smite and Penance casts as a primary Atonement applicator. This alone would add an estimated 15-20% to total HPS. Coming from Shadow, this button may not be muscle memory yet -- practice casting it on cooldown between ramps.

2. Use Ultimate Penitence Twice on Long Pulls

4-minute CD should be available twice on any 7+ minute pull. On fight 14 (7:12), it was used at 0:53 and never again -- 6+ minutes idle. Fight 28 showed better discipline holding it for P3 at 5:22. Only 2/8 P3-reaching pulls had two uses (F6, F17). The 2nd use should target P2 or P3 entry (~5:00-5:30) for maximum impact during the heaviest healing phases.

3. Increase Penance and SW:Death Usage

Penance at 6.63 CPM (-34% vs reference) and SW:Death at 1.33 CPM (-60%). These are the primary Atonement damage sources -- fewer casts = less Atonement healing. Part of the gap is Haste (918 vs 1342), but reducing wasted SW:Pain refreshes (2.78 vs 1.61 CPM) would free GCDs for more Penance and SW:Death. On fight 14, cutting SW:Pain from 26 to ~12 casts frees ~14 GCDs for higher-value abilities.

4. Save 4th Evangelism for P3

On fights 14 and 17, the 4th Evangelism fired at 4:54 (still P2) with nothing left for P3 -- the hardest healing check. Fights 2, 6, and 28 showed the correct pattern with the 4th Evangelism at 6:17, 6:00, and 5:50. Holding the 4th Evangelism until ~5:15-5:50 (P3 entry) ensures Disc contributes CD coverage when the team needs it most.

5. Solid Foundations for a First Night

Evangelism ramp timing is already consistent (0:08 avg opener, 1:44 avg 2nd, 3:20 avg 3rd). Power Infusion paired correctly. Survival is excellent (95% under 2-death rule). Smite filler is active in every pull. These fundamentals are strong for someone who just switched from Shadow DPS to Disc healer. The path from 131K to competitive HPS is clear: add PW:Shield to the rotation, use Ultimate Penitence twice, and convert excess SW:Pain casts into Penance and SW:Death.
