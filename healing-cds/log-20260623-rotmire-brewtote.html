Key Rotation Findings

Chain Heal + Riptide Deficit
Both core rotational abilities are 31% below reference. Chain Heal is the #1 throughput tool for Resto Shaman — 14.5 CPM vs Puddle's 21.1 means roughly 86 fewer Chain Heal casts over a full fight. Riptide at 7.2 CPM vs 10.6 means fewer Tidal Waves procs (which buff Chain Heal and Healing Wave). These two gaps compound: fewer Riptides = fewer empowered Chain Heals.
Lava Burst Over-Use
19 Lava Bursts vs Puddle's 10 — nearly 3x the DPS filler on a per-minute basis. While DPS contribution during low-damage windows is good, the extra Lava Bursts appear to be displacing healing casts. DPS filler should give way to Chain Heal and Riptide during damage windows. The 9 extra Lava Bursts could have been 9 Chain Heals (each hitting 4+ targets).
Unleash Life Under-Used
8 casts vs Puddle's 24 — 52% below reference on a per-minute basis. Unleash Life amplifies the next heal and spawns Ancestors (Farseer hero talent), making it a core part of the healing rotation. Missing these casts means fewer Ancestor mirrors and less amplified healing throughput.
Maintenance Abilities On Track
Flame Shock (+2%), Healing Stream Totem (+3%), and Nature's Swiftness (+21%) all match or exceed the reference. Downpour (22 casts, 4.09 CPM) is a talent Puddle doesn't run — it contributed 4.51M effective healing (5.5% of total), so the talent difference accounts for some of the Chain Heal gap.

Actionable Items

#1 Survive Festering Vines
Death at 5:23 cost 28.4% of the fight and left the team with 2 healers for the hardest phase. Festering Vines ticks hit 70-75K each, and combined with Rotting Pustules (36-38K) the damage is unsurvivable without a defensive. Hold Astral Shift specifically for Vines windows after the 2nd cycle when Pustule stacks make the overlap lethal. Pre-position to move out of Vines circles before they expand.
#2 Increase Chain Heal + Riptide CPM
Chain Heal at 14.5 CPM is 31% below Puddle's 21.1, and Riptide at 7.2 is 31% below 10.6. These are the two most important buttons for Resto Shaman throughput. Each missing Riptide also means a missing Tidal Waves proc that would empower the next Chain Heal. Aim for 18+ Chain Heal CPM and 9+ Riptide CPM. Reduce Lava Burst casts during damage windows to free up these globals.
#3 Deploy CDs With Blooms, Not After
Spirit Link Totem fired 13 seconds after Bloom #1 (2:13 vs 2:00). Ascendance fired 25 seconds after (2:25). Both should deploy at the moment Bloom hits (or 1-2 seconds before) to catch the burst damage and Shroom explosions. Spirit Link Totem at 1:58-2:00, Ascendance at 1:55-2:00. Reference player Puddle deploys Ascendance at 2:00 exactly.
#4 Cast Unleash Life on Cooldown
8 casts in 5:23 alive = 1.49 CPM vs Puddle's 3.09. Unleash Life has a ~9s cooldown and amplifies the next heal while spawning Ancestor copies (Farseer hero talent). This is effectively a free healing amplifier every 9 seconds. Missing it halves the Ancestor generation and reduces Chain Heal throughput by the amplification amount.
#5 DPS Filler During Downtime (Positive)
7,465 DPS with Lava Burst as primary filler is solid. Flame Shock uptime and Lava Burst usage during true downtime windows are correct. The issue is only when DPS filler displaces healing casts during active damage — reduce Lava Burst during Bloom windows and Festering Vines, maximize it during the quiet gaps between mechanics.
