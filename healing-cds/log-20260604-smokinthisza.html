How Marksmanship Hunter Works

Marksmanship Hunter is a ranged DPS spec built around a rhythm of building Focus with Steady Shot and spending it on hard-hitting abilities — particularly Aimed Shot, the spec’s signature move.

The core loop is: use Aimed Shot on cooldown (highest priority, hardest hitting ability), fill with Arcane Shot (instant Focus spender that procs Precise Shots), and weave Steady Shot when you need Focus or have nothing else to press. Rapid Fire is a channeled burst ability that generates Focus rapidly and should be used on CD.

Aimed Shot is the priority — it hits the hardest and has a cast time, so you need to be standing still. The rhythm of the spec is: cast Aimed Shot → instant Arcane Shots during Precise Shots procs → Steady Shot to regen Focus → repeat. Trueshot is your major burst cooldown that makes Aimed Shot cast faster and resets its CD.

The key metric for MM Hunter is how many Aimed Shots you land per minute. Aimed Shot is the biggest damage button, and every missed Aimed Shot is your highest-value GCD wasted. Arcane Shot fills the gaps between Aimed Shots and procs free Precise Shots — low Arcane Shot means you’re not spending Focus fast enough.

Dead time is particularly punishing for MM because the spec has a strict priority system — any second not casting is a second that Aimed Shot could have been firing.

What You Need to Change

1
Reduce Dead Time — Target Under 15%
Currently at 22.9%. This is the #1 priority. Every second of dead time is a missed Aimed Shot or Arcane Shot. Always be casting something — if Aimed Shot is on CD and you have Focus, press Arcane Shot. If you’re low on Focus, press Steady Shot. There should never be a moment where nothing is happening.
2
More Arcane Shot — Target 17+ Per Minute
Currently at 12.2/m, the #1 is at 17.8/m. Arcane Shot is your primary Focus spender between Aimed Shots. Don’t let Focus cap — if you have Focus and Aimed Shot is on cooldown, press Arcane Shot. It’s instant, costs Focus, and procs Precise Shots for more damage.
3
More Aimed Shot — Target 14+ Per Minute
Currently at 11.2/m, the #1 is at 15.1/m. Aimed Shot is your hardest-hitting ability and should never be delayed. Cast it on cooldown. If Trueshot is up, it resets the cooldown and makes it cast faster — spam it during those windows.
4
Health Pot Every Pull
Currently at 13/30 (43%). Health Potion is a separate cooldown from your pre-pot — it should be used every single pull. Free survivability that costs nothing and is off the GCD. Target 30/30.
