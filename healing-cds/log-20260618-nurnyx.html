Actionable Improvements

#1 CRITICAL Survive Echoing Darkness in P1 (5 deaths)

Echoing Darkness (Vorelus orbs) killed Nurnyx in 5 pulls at an average of 1:32 into the fight. This is a repeating ability with ~3s intervals that stacks damage. In fights 2, 5, 7, and 25, multiple simultaneous Echoing Darkness hits dealt 20-42K each, overwhelming available healing.

Fix: Use Dispersion proactively when Vorelus spawns Echoing Darkness clusters. Fade can drop threat/aggro but won't prevent the orb damage. Track Vorelus's cast timer and pre-position away from orb paths. On short pulls where Vorelus is alive (0:00-2:05), this ability is the #1 threat.

#2 CRITICAL Survive Simulacrum Backlash in P2 (4 deaths)

Simulacrum Backlash killed Nurnyx in fights 15, 16, 21, and 24 at an average of 4:38. This is raid-wide pulsing damage from the Rift Simulacrum that hits every ~0.7 seconds. Combined with Void Expulsion and other P2 mechanics, this creates lethal damage windows.

Fix: Use Vampiric Embrace proactively during Simulacrum Backlash windows. Coordinate Dispersion for the most dangerous overlap (Backlash + Void Expulsion). Desperate Prayer is a 2-minute CD -- it should be available for every Backlash window in P2.

#3 HIGH Survive the Intermission (4 deaths in Inter + P1/Inter transition)

Stellar Emission channel (2:22-2:52) plus Silverstrike Barrage every 6s creates a sustained damage check during intermission. Deaths at 2:30 (F9) and 2:35 (F20) indicate insufficient defensive usage during this window. Orbiting Matter also killed Nurnyx at 2:37 (F11).

Fix: Pre-cast Desperate Prayer before Stellar Emission begins at 2:22. Save Dispersion for the middle of the channel (~2:35-2:41) when damage peaks. Healthstone usage during intermission is critical. Note: Nurnyx used a Healthstone and a Silvermoon Health Potion in fight 28 -- make sure both are available every pull.

#4 MODERATE P2 DPS Optimization

P2 DPS averages 113.2K, which is 64% lower than P1's 314.8K. Some drop is expected (movement from Rift Slash, target switching to adds), but the best P2 pull (F14, 175.5K) shows significantly higher potential.

Fix: Align the second Voidform + PI window with P2 start (Heroism timing at 2:54 is already correct in F28). Maintain DoTs on Rift Simulacrum and Undying Voidspawn via Tentacle Slam while keeping SW:Madness rolling on the priority target. Movement during Rift Slash should use Shadow Word: Death as a movement GCD.

#5 POSITIVE Strong P1 Execution and Improving Trend

P1 DPS is consistent at 314.8K average across 27 pulls, with the best P1 at 361.8K. Overall DPS trend appears lower in the second half of the session, but this is expected -- later pulls lasted longer into P2/P3 where DPS naturally drops due to mechanics. The deepest boss percentage pulls came late in the session (F27: 31.27%, F28: 36.59%), demonstrating active learning and improved survival.

Cast profile on the best pull shows good ability usage: 7.9 Mind Blast CPM, 6.6 SW:Madness CPM, proper Voidform/PI synchronization, and correct Archon hero talent usage (Halo on cooldown). Power Infusion was correctly self-cast 6 times with Twins of the Sun Priestess.
