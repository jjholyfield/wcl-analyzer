Actionable Improvements

1. Increase Shadow Bolt Filler Usage
Impact: High -- accounts for roughly half the CPM gap.
Shadow Bolt CPM is 5.5 vs reference 8.5 (-3.0 CPM). Shadow Bolt is the shard generator filler that should be cast whenever no higher-priority ability is available. 9 Glide casts in fight 25 suggest movement is eating into filler GCDs. Focus on minimizing movement (pre-position for mechanics) and ABC (Always Be Casting) -- every GCD without a cast during safe windows should be a Shadow Bolt.
2. Maintain Uptime in P3
Impact: High -- P3 is where the biggest DPS gap lives (63K vs 105K reference, -40%).
P3 CPS drops to 0.61 from 0.79 in P2 (a 23% activity drop). This is partly raid state (cascade deaths, fewer healers means more defensive play), but the reference maintains 0.74 CPS in P3. P3 DPS will naturally improve as the raid gets cleaner reps and Orichamaru gains familiarity with Devouring Cosmos positioning. Use Demonic Circle more aggressively for instant repositioning during Cosmic Radiation and Gravity Collapse.
3. Use Potions
Impact: Medium -- ~2-4% overall DPS.
No Potion of Recklessness usage detected across any analyzed pulls. The reference used 2 potions per kill. Pre-pot before the pull starts and use a second potion during a Demonic Tyrant window in P2 or P3 for maximum value.
4. Squeeze More Hand of Gul'dan Casts
Impact: Medium -- Hand of Gul'dan at 11.8 CPM vs 12.9 CPM reference (-1.1).
This is tied to the Shadow Bolt gap: fewer Shadow Bolt casts mean fewer Soul Shards generated, which means fewer Hand of Gul'dan casts available. Fixing the filler issue will cascade into more HoG casts naturally.
5. Address Zero-Damage Pulls
Impact: Unknown -- 6 of 20 pulls show zero damage.
Orichamaru was present in the roster for fights 2, 4, 8, 16, 18, and 22 but recorded zero damage. All were short wipes (60-139 seconds). If this is a loading/disconnect issue, it means 30% of pulls are wasted reps. If it is an intentional sit-out during resets, it is not a concern.
