Bottom Line

Augmentation Evoker is a support spec — personal DPS is intentionally low. The comparison that matters is support ability execution. Zinnks' Ebon Might uptime was 74.7% vs the reference's 95.9% — a 21.2 percentage point gap that directly reduces the damage of 4 buffed allies. Eruption CPM was 13.1 vs the reference's 18.9, a 30.7% deficit in the primary Ebon Might extender. Prescience usage (38 casts, 4.8 CPM) was close to reference (34 casts, 5.7 CPM) — the lower CPM reflects longer fight duration. Zinnks died at 6:40 to Fungal Bloom #3 + Rotting Pustules stacking damage, missing the final 77 seconds. Died in all 20 pulls. The DPS gap vs top Aug Evokers (108K vs 173K) is split between execution (Eruption CPM, Ebon Might uptime), fight length (477s vs 358s), and gear (286.9 vs 289.5 ilvl).

Actionable Improvements

1. Increase Eruption CPM from 13.1 to 18+ by using it on every Essence Burst proc. This is the single biggest lever. Eruption at 13.1 CPM means roughly one every 4.6 seconds — reference fires one every 3.2 seconds. Each additional Eruption extends Ebon Might by 1 second AND deals ~123K damage. Fixing this alone would raise Ebon Might uptime from 74.7% toward 90%+ and add approximately 5-8M total damage across the fight. Practice tracking Essence Burst procs and spending them immediately on Eruption rather than letting them sit.
2. Close casting gaps to raise total CPM from 37.2 toward 45+. The 20% total CPM deficit means Zinnks is spending roughly 1 out of every 5 GCDs doing nothing. Over 400 seconds, that is approximately 80 lost GCDs. Focus on always having a button queued — if Eruption is not available, cast Living Flame. If moving, use Hover to cast while moving (reference uses Hover 18 times vs Zinnks' 15). The ABC rule: Always Be Casting.
3. Use Breath of Eons on cooldown — reduce gaps from 86-106s to ~75s. Each delayed Breath of Eons pushes the next one later, cascading through the fight. Over a 400s fight, pressing Breath of Eons every 75s yields 6 uses instead of 5. That is approximately 2.5M additional damage plus better Ebon Might alignment with burst windows. Set a weak aura or timer to track the cooldown.
4. Pre-use Obsidian Scales or a defensive before Bloom #3 to survive. Zinnks died at 6:40 during Bloom #3. Zephyr was last used at 6:09 (on cooldown). Obsidian Scales was available but not active during the kill hit. Pre-casting Obsidian Scales 2-3 seconds before Bloom (which fires at a fixed time) would provide 40% damage reduction through the burst. Surviving 77 more seconds could add ~8-10M damage.
5. Improve Fire Breath and Upheaval usage to match reference CPM. Fire Breath at 1.9 CPM vs 2.3, Upheaval at 1.6 vs 2.3. These empowered casts are on short cooldowns and deal significant damage. Each missed Fire Breath is ~110K damage lost; each missed Upheaval is ~214K. Together, getting these to reference CPM would add approximately 2M total damage across the fight.

Context & Caveats

Fight length matters: Zinnks' kill was 477.5 seconds (7:58). The reference kill was 358.2 seconds (5:58). Longer fights mean more Rotting Pustules stacks, more Bloom cycles, more pressure on healers and defensives. DPS naturally decreases in longer fights due to CDs becoming a smaller % of total time.

Raid state: By the time Zinnks died at 6:40, the raid was already at 15/19 (4 dead). In the reference kill, the raid likely had fewer deaths earlier. Lower raid DPS extends the fight, which cascades into more damage taken, more healing pressure, and more deaths.

Aug DPS is support DPS: The 108K DPS number should NOT be compared to pure DPS specs. Aug Evoker's contribution is buffing 4 allies via Ebon Might (~20% damage increase each). The true raid impact is 108K personal + the buffed damage on allies, which makes Aug one of the highest total-contribution specs when played well.

Gear differential: 286.9 ilvl vs reference 289.5. Small gap (2.6 ilvl) — accounts for maybe 1-2% DPS difference, not the 38% gap. Asâp (#2 ranked) achieved 174.6K DPS at just 258.6 ilvl, confirming execution matters far more than gear for Aug.
