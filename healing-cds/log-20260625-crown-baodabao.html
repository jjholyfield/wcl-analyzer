Actionable Improvements

#1: Survival is Already Excellent

Only 1 actionable death in 19 pulls. This is the single best survival rate on the team. Baodabao is using Ice Block, Alter Time, and Blink effectively to stay alive through raid-collapsing situations. The focus should not be on "dying less" — that is already near-perfect.

#2: P2 Uptime Improvement

P2 DPS (44.5K avg) is 60% lower than P1 (111K avg). Some drop is expected from target switching and movement, but the range within P2 (27.7K to 58.7K) suggests inconsistency. The best P2 performance (58.7K, F28) shows what is achievable. Focus areas:

  • Pre-position for Rift Simulacrum spawns to minimize movement
  • Use Shimmer/Blink for repositioning rather than running — maintain Frostbolt casts during transitions
  • Ensure Frozen Orb is used on cooldown in P2 for consistent proc generation
  • Use Ice Lance and instant-cast procs during heavy movement windows
#3: P3 Damage Contribution

P3 avg DPS (15.8K) is very low, but this is heavily skewed by raid state — most P3 entries had 10-15 dead players. The best P3 (27.5K, F28) shows promise. As the raid survives longer into P3, Baodabao should:

  • Save second Icy Veins for P3 if the pull is going deep — P3 is where DPS matters most
  • Prioritize staying alive over maximizing damage — a dead Mage does zero DPS, and survival is already strong
  • Use Ice Block proactively for Cosmic Radiation overlaps rather than holding it for emergency
#4: Void Droplet Awareness (P1)

The one actionable death (F8) was from Bursting Emptiness — a Void Droplet explosion. While this only happened once, Droplet positioning is the one P1 mechanic that caught Baodabao out. Maintain awareness of Droplet positions and be ready to Blink or Ice Block if one detonates nearby.

#5: P1 DPS Variance

P1 DPS ranges from 78K (F1) to 136K (F14) — a 74% spread. The low end (F1: 78K, F18: 86K) may indicate suboptimal cooldown alignment or missed burst windows on some pulls. Ensure the opener is consistent: pre-cast Frostbolt/Frostfire Bolt into Ray of Frost, Frozen Orb, and the standard burst sequence every pull.

Overall Assessment

Verdict: Strong Performer, Raid Needs Cleaner Execution

Baodabao is one of the most reliable players on this team. With only 1 actionable death in 19 pulls, survival is not a concern. P1 DPS is competitive (111K avg, 136K best), and there is a clear upward trend through the session (+4.3% improvement, best pull was the last one).

The DPS gap vs reference kills (65.7K best vs 105.6K reference) is largely explained by two factors: (1) wipe pulls never complete a full damage cycle that kill pulls do, and (2) P3 raid state is consistently degraded with 10-15 dead players. As the raid improves P1 consistency (42% of pulls wiped in P1) and P3 survival, Baodabao's numbers will naturally climb.

The primary opportunity for individual improvement is P2 uptime consistency. The 27.7K-to-58.7K P2 DPS range shows room to stabilize around the higher end through better positioning and movement efficiency.
