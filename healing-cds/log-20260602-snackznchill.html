How Frost Mage Works

Frost Mage is a proc-based spec built around one mechanic: Freezing stacks. Your spells apply Freezing to the target (stacks up to 20), and when you consume those stacks with Ice Lance, it triggers Shatter — a big hit that also deals AoE damage to anything nearby based on how many stacks were consumed.

Your filler spell is Frostbolt. You cast Frostbolt when nothing else is available. Every Frostbolt builds Freezing stacks and has a chance to proc Brain Freeze, which lights up Flurry. When Brain Freeze procs, you press Flurry immediately — it’s instant, it dumps a burst of Freezing stacks onto the target, and it sets up your next Ice Lance to shatter for massive damage. That Frostbolt → Brain Freeze → Flurry → Ice Lance chain is the bread and butter of the spec.

You also get Fingers of Frost procs, which make your next Ice Lance act as if the target has full Freezing stacks even if it doesn’t. When Fingers of Frost lights up, press Ice Lance immediately — it’s free burst damage.

Thermal Void supercharges the Brain Freeze combo: when you Flurry with Brain Freeze active, your next Ice Lance within 8 seconds consumes double the Freezing stacks.

For cooldowns, you have Frozen Orb and Ray of Frost, both on roughly 60-second timers. Frozen Orb flies out and generates a storm of procs — press it on cooldown. Ray of Frost is a 4-second channel that builds Freezing stacks fast, and with the right talent you get 2 charges. Spend your existing Freezing stacks before channeling Ray, because it dumps so many new stacks that you’ll waste them if the target is already capped.

When your Icicle bar fills up (5 Icicles from Frostbolt casts), Glacial Spike becomes available. Press it the moment it lights up.

The Spellslinger hero talent lowers the Ice Lance threshold to 6+ stacks, so you fire Ice Lances more frequently.

The golden rule: always be casting. If Brain Freeze is up, Flurry. If Fingers of Frost is up, Ice Lance. If you have 6+ stacks, Ice Lance. If Glacial Spike is ready, press it. If Frozen Orb or Ray of Frost is off cooldown, use it. If nothing is happening, Frostbolt. Every empty GCD is damage gone.
