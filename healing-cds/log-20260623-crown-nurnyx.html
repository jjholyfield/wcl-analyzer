Actionable Improvements

P1 Use Defensive Cooldowns Proactively Before Every Major Mechanic

Shadow Priest has strong defensives: Dispersion (90% DR), Fade (10% DR with talent), Vampiric Embrace (self-healing), and Shadow Word: Death for movement. Nurnyx is dying to 11 different abilities across all phases, which strongly suggests defensives are either not being used or being used reactively after damage has already hit. On every known damage window (Grasp of Emptiness at ~25s, Void Expulsion at ~27s, Echoing Darkness every ~3s from Vorelus), pre-cast Fade or Dispersion. The Intermission deaths to Silverstrike Barrage and Orbiting Matter specifically call for Dispersion during the Stellar Emission channel. Impact: Eliminating even half of the 23 deaths would dramatically increase total damage output.

P1 Positioning During P1 Sentinel Mechanics

8 of 23 deaths occurred in Phase 1, primarily to Echoing Darkness (Vorelus orbs, 2 deaths), Void Remnants (void pools, 2 deaths), Grasp of Emptiness (pull-in, 1 death at 0:11), Bursting Emptiness (1 death), and Corrupting Essence (1 death). These are all mechanics that punish poor positioning. As a ranged DPS, Nurnyx should be positioned at max range from Vorelus (to avoid Echoing Darkness splash), away from Void Remnant pools, and ready to break Grasp of Emptiness immediately. The 0:11 death in Fight 11 to Grasp of Emptiness is particularly costly — this is the very first mechanic of the fight. Impact: Consistently surviving P1 (which 60% of pulls already achieve) would guarantee P2 contribution in every pull the raid reaches it.

P2 Cosmic Barrier and Bursting Emptiness in P2

Cosmic Barrier killed Nurnyx in 3 pulls (fights 6, 11, 17), all between 3:35-3:38 fight time. Bursting Emptiness killed in 3 pulls (fights 9, 10, 18). These are the two most consistent killers. Cosmic Barrier requires breaking the shield or moving away from the barrier source. Bursting Emptiness requires proper spread positioning. With only 9 pulls reaching P2 alive, Nurnyx needs more reps on these mechanics. Impact: These 6 deaths represent the P2 mechanic wall. Resolving them extends survival into late P2 where the raid needs sustained DPS.

P3 Intermission Survival Plan

3 deaths during the Intermission (fights 9, 15, 25) to Orbiting Matter, Silverstrike Barrage, and Unknown damage. The Intermission is pure survival — Stellar Emission channel from 2:22 to 2:52 with Silverstrike Barrage every 6 seconds. Shadow Priest should use Dispersion during the heaviest overlap of Stellar Emission + Silverstrike Barrage, and Vampiric Embrace for self-sustain. A healthstone or health potion at low HP during the channel is also an option. Impact: 3 deaths here are preventable with a defensive plan, converting 3 sub-3:00 deaths into P2+ pulls.
