Actionable Items

CRITICAL Survive P1 — 7 deaths in the lowest-damage phase

Senssay died 7 times in P1 (Sentinels, 0:00-2:14), the phase with the least raid-wide damage. Primary killers: Void Expulsion (dodge the frontal), Bursting Emptiness (kill or avoid Void Droplets), and environment/DoT damage. P1 deaths cost the raid a healer for the entire fight — Intermission, P2, and P3 healing is all done with 3 healers instead of 4.

Impact: Eliminating P1 deaths alone would improve phase reach rate from 60% to ~90% for Intermission and P2.

CRITICAL Use Fortifying Brew proactively before heavy damage

Fortifying Brew was cast only 2 times in the best pull (Fight 23). With a 6-minute cooldown and the pull lasting 6:18, there is room for 2 uses, but the timing matters. On pulls where Senssay dies early, Fortifying Brew is often not used at all. Pre-casting Fortifying Brew before known damage events (Void Expulsion at ~27s intervals, Silversunder Catastrophe at 2:14) would prevent many of these deaths.

Impact: Personal defensive CD can be the difference between surviving an overlap and dying to environment damage.

HIGH Use Celestial Conduit more frequently

Celestial Conduit has a 90-second cooldown but averaged only 1.2 casts per pull across the night. In Fight 23 (6:18 alive), it was cast only once at 4:23. A 90s CD should be cast at minimum 3 times in a 6-minute pull (at ~0:30, ~2:00, ~3:30 or equivalent). Each missed Conduit is a significant burst AoE healing window lost.

Impact: An additional 1-2 Celestial Conduit casts per pull adds ~15-25K effective healing per use during damage peaks.

MEDIUM Reduce P1 overheal to conserve mana for P2/P3

P1 overheal is 34.1% vs P2 at 22.3%. Vivify overheal is 46% fight-wide, and Enveloping Mist overheal is 39.7%. In P1, damage is predictable and spread thin — Renewing Mist maintenance plus occasional Vivify should suffice. Saving Enveloping Mist casts for P2+ damage events and reducing unnecessary Vivify casts on topped-off targets would preserve mana for the healing-intensive phases.

Impact: Shifting 2-3 Vivify casts per minute from P1 to DPS filler would save ~5% mana for P2/P3 while maintaining HPS through Rushing Wind Kick healing contribution.

MEDIUM P2 positioning awareness (Cosmic Barrier, Void Barrage, Falling)

9 deaths in P2 from varied mechanics: Cosmic Barrier (2x), Void Barrage (1x), Void Remnants (1x), Grasp of Emptiness (1x), Simulacrum Backlash (1x), Falling (1x), plus 2 environment deaths. The diversity of death causes points to general P2 positioning issues rather than one specific mechanic. Rift Slash dodging, Call of the Void awareness, and Voidstalker Sting spread are all P2 movement checks.

Impact: Reducing P2 death rate from 82% (9/11 pulls that reached P2) to under 50% would allow more P3 practice time.
