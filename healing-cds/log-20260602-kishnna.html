How Subtlety Rogue Works

Subtlety Rogue operates on a 90-second burst cycle. The spec revolves around stacking your cooldowns together — Shadow Blades with Shadow Dance — to maximize damage in burst windows, then managing energy between those windows.

Your core loop is: build combo points with Shadowstrike (during Shadow Dance) or Backstab/Shuriken Storm (outside Dance), then spend combo points with finishers — Eviscerate (single target), Black Powder (AoE), or Secret Technique/Coup de Grace (priority finishers during Dance).

Shadow Dance is your bread and butter — a 20-second recharge ability with 2 charges. During Shadow Dance, you gain stealth benefits and Shadowstrike becomes available. Secret Technique is your highest-priority finisher during Dance. Shadow Blades is your major 90-second cooldown — it doubles combo point generation and adds shadow damage. Always fit both Shadow Dance charges into every Shadow Blades window.

On LBV with 3 bosses active, top Sub Rogues use Shuriken Storm as their primary builder (generates more combo points on multiple targets) and Black Powder as their primary finisher (AoE). This is significantly higher DPS than single-target Backstab + Eviscerate on a council fight.
