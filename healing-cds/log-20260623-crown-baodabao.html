Actionable Improvements

Prioritized by impact. Survival items first (the only thing that matters until the death rate drops), rotation items after.

1. Cosmic Barrier — 6 deaths, all preventable
What: Cosmic Barrier ticks for sustained damage in P2/P3. Baodabao dies to it at 3:01, 3:38, 3:42 (x3), 3:46, and 6:09 — consistently in the first 30-70 seconds of entering a new phase.
Fix: Pre-cast Ice Barrier before phase transitions. When Cosmic Barrier appears, immediately Shimmer out of the affected area. If caught without Shimmer, use Alter Time to snap back. If health is critical, Ice Block (3-minute CD) removes all ticking effects. Plan one Ice Block for P2 entry and one for P3 entry.
Impact: Eliminating these 6 deaths would increase average alive time by ~45 seconds per pull, adding an estimated 5-7M total damage per fight.
2. Echoing Darkness — 3 deaths in P1
What: Vorelus' Echoing Darkness ticks every 2 seconds. Three consecutive unmitigated ticks at 20-40K each is lethal without healing or shielding. Deaths at 0:57, 1:22, 1:56 — all in mid/late P1.
Fix: Monitor positioning relative to Vorelus. When Echoing Darkness starts ticking, reposition with Shimmer immediately. Pre-shield with Ice Barrier. This ability accounts for 39% of ALL damage taken (56M) — reducing exposure by even 20% significantly improves survival.
Impact: Preventing these 3 P1 deaths means reaching Intermission more consistently (currently 7/20 pulls die before Intermission).
3. Intermission survival — 3 deaths (Silverstrike Barrage + Orbiting Matter)
What: The intermission is a 39-second survival check (Stellar Emission channel + Silverstrike Barrage every 6s). No targets to hit. Three deaths here (2:30, 2:31, 2:42) indicate insufficient defensive usage or bad positioning.
Fix: Pre-cast Ice Barrier as P1 ends (~2:10). Use Mirror Image at intermission start for passive damage reduction. Save Alter Time for emergency snapback. If health drops below 30%, Ice Block. The intermission is short — burning a 3-minute cooldown here is correct because P2 starts at 2:53 with fresh CDs.
Impact: Currently reaches P2 alive in 10/14 attempts (71%). Fixing intermission deaths raises this to 13/14 (93%).
4. Falling damage — 4.3M (3% of total damage taken)
What: Falling damage means getting knocked off platforms or failing to handle knockback mechanics. 4.3M is significant for a player who dies this frequently — it could be the difference between surviving a hit sequence and dying to it.
Fix: Position away from edges during Grasp of Emptiness and any knockback abilities. Shimmer cancels fall momentum and can be used while falling. Consider Alter Time as a safety net when positioned near edges.
Impact: Eliminating falling damage frees up ~4.3M of healing capacity that can go toward surviving actual boss mechanics.
5. Defensive cooldown plan (rotation item)
What: Frost Mage has strong defensives: Ice Barrier (absorb shield, ~25s CD), Ice Block (immunity, 3-4min CD), Alter Time (snapback, 1min CD), Mirror Image (threat/damage reduction, 2min CD), Shimmer (2 charges, blink during casts).
Fix: Build a defensive rotation plan keyed to fight phases:
• P1 (0:00-2:14): Ice Barrier on pull, refresh on CD. Shimmer for Echoing Darkness repositioning.
• Intermission (2:14-2:53): Ice Barrier + Mirror Image pre-cast. Save Ice Block as emergency.
• P2 (2:53-5:09): Ice Barrier on CD. Ice Block for first Cosmic Barrier if caught. Alter Time as snapback for Rift Slash movement.
• P3 (5:09+): All CDs available again. Ice Block for Cosmic Barrier in P3.
Impact: A planned defensive rotation is the single highest-impact change. Every second alive is ~300K more damage contributed.
