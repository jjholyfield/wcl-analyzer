Next Week: How We Read the Rematch
Every gap between this baseline and next week's numbers lands in one of three buckets from the review spec.
1. Reminders working?

The NSRT note now fires a TTS callout for every assignment on this page. The first question next week is simply whether the reminders move these numbers. High on-time on one assignment with low on-time on the rest of a player's sheet means they follow habits, not the plan, and the reminder should close that gap on its own. Recheck before coaching.

2. Still missed with the CD available = coaching item

An assignment that stays missed next week while the cooldown was sitting available is an execution problem. Those become named coaching items, and the fix is a coaching note plus a stronger reminder: add a countdown or move the callout earlier.

3. Consistent drift = move the plan, not the player

An assignment that keeps landing at the same wrong spot next week is telling us where the raid actually needs it. Those assignments get their plan time moved, and the NSRT string gets regenerated the same session so the raid hears the new time on the next pull.
