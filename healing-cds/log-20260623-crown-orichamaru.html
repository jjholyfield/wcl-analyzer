Actionable Improvements

Priority 1 — Critical

Survive Phase 2: Use Dark Pact Before Every Cosmic Barrier

3 deaths to Cosmic Barrier, all at exactly 3:37-3:38. This is a specific P2 mechanic occurring at a consistent timer. Pre-cast Dark Pact (45s CD) 2-3 seconds before each Cosmic Barrier cast. Dark Pact was only used 2 times across a 5:23 pull — it should be used 6-7 times. On a 45-second cooldown, there is no reason not to use it before every major damage event. Additionally, ensure raid positioning allows healers to top you before this mechanic.

Priority 2 — Critical

Handle Void Remnants in Phase 1

4 deaths to Void Remnants, all in P1 (1:02, 0:26, 1:14, 1:21, 2:00). This is an avoidable ground soak mechanic from Vorelus. Use Demonic Circle placement early in P1 as an escape route, or use Demonic Gateway for repositioning when Void Remnants appear. As a ranged DPS, maintaining distance from ground effects should be straightforward. Dying in P1 means zero contribution for the rest of the pull.

Priority 3 — High

Prioritize Rift Simulacrum in P2

3 deaths to Simulacrum Backlash in late P2 (4:52, 4:56, 5:23). Simulacrum Backlash deals increasing damage the longer the Rift Simulacrum lives. As Demonology, use Hand of Gul'dan and Implosion on the Simulacrum as a priority target when it spawns. Killing it faster directly reduces the Backlash damage to the entire raid. With Diabolist's Diabolic Oculi, your cleave on the Simulacrum should be high.

Priority 4 — High

Use Consumables Properly

No DPS potion was used in fight 25 (only a health potion at 4:57). On progression, using an Intellect potion on pull (pre-pot) aligned with Demonic Tyrant significantly increases P1 burst damage when there are 4+ targets active. A second potion during the next Tyrant window in P2 adds further value. Additionally, Healthstone was used at 5:14, only 9 seconds before death — earlier usage could have prevented the death entirely. Consider setting up a WeakAura to remind for pre-pot and health pot at 50% HP.

Priority 5 — Medium

Increase Demonic Circle Usage for Mechanics

Only 1 Demonic Circle: Teleport across 5:23. Demonic Circle has a 30-second cooldown and provides instant repositioning. Place it in a safe spot pre-pull and refresh placement during intermission. Use it reactively for Void Expulsion (2 deaths), Grasp of Emptiness (1 death), or any mechanic requiring rapid movement. This is one of Demo Warlock's best survival tools and it is severely underutilized.

What Orichamaru Does Well

Phase 1 DPS is strong. Averaging 353.8K DPS across P1 (events-based, including pets) with peaks at 399K. Demonology's multi-target burst with Hand of Gul'dan spam into Implosion plus Demonic Tyrant on pull is being executed well during the sentinel phase. The best overall pull (62.7K on F13) shows what the spec can do when survival extends into later phases.
Diabolist hero tree is correct for this fight. Crown of the Cosmos has significant multi-target in P1 (3 sentinels + adds) and in P2 (Rift Simulacrum + Undying Voidspawn). Diabolist's Diabolic Oculi explosions and Ruination AoE (6 casts in fight 25) align well with these windows.
Late-session improvement. DPS trended upward in the final 4 pulls (avg 48.8K vs 36.6K mid-session). Despite dying every pull, the improvement suggests fight knowledge is developing. Converting that knowledge into survival will compound the DPS gains.
