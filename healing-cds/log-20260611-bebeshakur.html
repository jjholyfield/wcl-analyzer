Bottom Line — Progression Context

Bebeshakur is outputting 181.7K DPS on the longest pull (Fight 23, 4:53) — a ~9% gap behind #1 ranked Unholy DK ChiZiShu (~200K events-based DPS in an 8:42 kill, 101.4K WCL active DPS). However, this is prog wipe data vs a full kill: Bebeshakur died at 4:24 to Bursting Emptiness (raid wipe), cutting the fight short. The reference played the full 8:42 with late-fight sustained phases unavailable to Bebeshakur. The primary gap is in total CPM: 40.3 vs 52.7 (−23.5%). Scourge Strike is the biggest shortfall at 12.5 vs 18.7 CPM (−33%), followed by Death Coil/Necrotic Coil (−35%/−39%) and Soul Reaper (−44%). With 10 talent differences, ilvl gap (282 vs 290), and 6 vs 13 Ebon Might applications, a portion of this gap is external. But the CPM deficit points to systematic uptime loss — likely from movement mechanics and unfamiliarity with boss timings during prog.

Actionable Items

1. Increase Scourge Strike frequency (−33% CPM gap = biggest single issue). Scourge Strike is at 12.5 CPM vs reference 18.7 CPM — roughly 30 missed Scourge Strikes over a comparable 8:42 fight. Each missed SS means missed pet Sweeping Claws damage (your 4.7% vs reference 8.1% damage share). In the current Unholy rotation, Scourge Strike should be pressed whenever you have 1+ Lesser Ghoul stacks from Festering Wound consumption. The gap suggests GCDs are being lost to movement or hesitation during Crown's positioning mechanics. Fix: Practice maintaining SS uptime during Grasp of Emptiness beam dodges and Void Expulsion puddle placement. Use Death Charge/Death Grip to close gaps after forced movement instead of walking back.
2. Spend Runic Power aggressively — Death Coil / Necrotic Coil at −37% combined CPM. Combined RP spending (Death Coil + Necrotic Coil) is 11.5 CPM vs reference 18.2 CPM. This suggests Runic Power is overcapping frequently, wasting potential Sudden Doom procs and Coil of Devastation damage. In Unholy's rotation, Death Coil should be used on Sudden Doom procs immediately, during Forbidden Knowledge windows as Necrotic Coil, and as a dump when approaching RP cap. Fix: Set a WeakAura or alert for RP > 80 to force spending. During Dark Transformation, prioritize Death Coil even more aggressively to benefit from Commander of the Dead.
3. Cast Soul Reaper outside Dark Transformation windows. Currently at 1.2 vs 2.2 CPM (−44%), Soul Reaper is only being cast during DT windows (6 casts aligning with 5 DT casts). The reference casts it 19 times, using it throughout the fight whenever the Reaping effect is available. In current-patch Unholy, Soul Reaper should be used: (a) during Dark Transformation on CD, (b) when target is below 35% HP during DT, and (c) whenever Reaping procs outside DT. Fix: Track Reaping proc availability and use Soul Reaper opportunistically between DT windows.
4. Remove Death and Decay from single-target rotation. Bebeshakur cast D&D 5 times (1.0 CPM); the reference cast it zero times. For Rider of the Apocalypse Unholy in single-target, Death and Decay provides negligible value and wastes a GCD + Rune that should go to Scourge Strike or Festering Strike. Those 5 GCDs represent ~7.5 seconds of lost rotational damage. Fix: Remove Death and Decay from single-target keybinds or add a modifier so it is only used on AoE pulls.
5. Use Icebound Fortitude during high-damage windows. IBF was unused (0 casts) while the reference used it 2 times. On Crown of the Cosmos, there are multiple heavy damage windows (Corrupting Essence, Simulacrum Backlash pulses) where IBF helps survive. Staying alive longer = more DPS uptime. The falling damage (382K, 2 hits) also suggests platform transition positioning could be tighter. Fix: Map IBF to Corrupting Essence or heavy Simulacrum Backlash windows. Fix platform transition movement to avoid falls.

Summary — Progression Context

Comparing prog wipe data to a kill is inherently apples-to-oranges. The ~9% raw DPS gap (181.7K vs ~200K events-based) breaks down approximately: ~35% CPM deficit (GCDs lost to movement/unfamiliarity with Crown mechanics), ~25% gear and stats (−8 ilvl, −12% Mastery, −4% Strength), ~20% external buffs (half the Ebon Might applications), ~10% talent differences (10 node diffs including missing Infected Claws), and ~10% fight duration context (wipe at 4:53 vs full 8:42 kill).
