Actionable Improvements

1
Close the Precise Shots Spending Gap
Aimed Shot at 6.3 CPM generates a Precise Shots proc every cast. Arcane Shot (the spender) is at 2.8 CPM -- less than half. Every unspent Precise Shots proc before the next Aimed Shot is wasted damage AND a weaker next Aimed Shot (Spotter's Mark buff). During movement phases (P2/P3), prioritize spending Precise Shots with Arcane Shot before casting Aimed Shot again. This is the single largest rotational improvement available.
2
Stat Rebalancing: Crit Over Mastery
Smokinthisza has 1,840 Mastery vs 1,395 Crit. The primary reference runs the opposite priority (819 Mastery / 1,522 Crit). For Dark Ranger MM, Crit drives proc frequency and Precise Shots damage. While gear choices are limited by what drops, any opportunity to trade Mastery for Crit should be taken. This won't change overnight but should guide upgrade decisions.
3
P3 Positioning for Gravity Collapse
2 instances of Falling damage (372K total) in fight 25 suggest displacement from Gravity Collapse is catching Smokinthisza near edges. In P3, position further from platform edges so the pull-in does not result in fall damage. Use Disengage (already at 3.3 per pull) reactively during Gravity Collapse to counteract the displacement.
4
Maintain P2 DPS Consistency
P2 DPS ranges from 7K to 83K. Excluding the death outlier (fight 5), the range is 56-83K. The variance comes from movement during Rift Simulacrum mechanics. As a ranged player, Smokinthisza should be able to maintain higher uptime during P2 repositioning. Focus on pre-positioning for Rift spawns and using instant-cast abilities (Arcane Shot, Volley, Rapid Fire) during forced movement.
5
Gear Progression -- 16 ilvl Gap to Close
At 274 ilvl, Smokinthisza is 16 ilvl below the top-performing reference (290). This is a ~10-15% raw stat deficit that directly impacts DPS ceiling. The closest ilvl comparison (Woogiee at 273) did 107K on a kill -- suggesting that when Smokinthisza's team gets a kill, DPS should be competitive at current gear level if execution is clean. Vault choices and targeted upgrades will help close this gap over time.
