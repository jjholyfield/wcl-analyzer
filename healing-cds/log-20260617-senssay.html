Actionable Items

1. Increase Spinning Crane Kick usage. SCK is 37% below the reference at 3.5 CPM vs 5.6 CPM. This is the single biggest cast gap and accounts for an estimated 1.9M missing damage. SCK should be used any time there are 2+ targets stacked (add phases, boss + adds), and with enough Chi, as a high-damage GCD even on single target with Dance of Chi-Ji procs (which Senssay does proc — 18 applications logged). Every SCK miss during add windows is a significant DPS loss.
2. Reduce movement GCDs. 13 Rolls (vs 7), 6 Flying Serpent Kicks (vs 0), and 3 Tiger's Lusts (vs 0) total 17 extra non-damage GCDs compared to the reference — approximately 25 seconds of lost damage. Krylest used zero FSK and half as many Rolls. This suggests Senssay is either repositioning too aggressively, not pre-positioning before mechanics, or using Roll reactively where the reference stays in melee. Practice minimizing movement by positioning tighter to the boss and trusting existing movement tools (Chi Torpedo if talented, or a single Roll).
3. Improve Fists of Fury target coverage. Despite nearly identical cast counts (42 vs 38), FoF dealt 9.5M less damage (15.4M vs 24.8M). Krylest's FoF hit 394 targets across all channels vs Senssay's 284 — 39% more target connections. This means Krylest is consistently channeling FoF while positioned to cleave adds/boss together. Before pressing FoF, confirm you are positioned to hit maximum targets. Even 1 extra target per FoF channel across 42 casts is massive.
4. Use Rushing Wind Kick and Whirling Dragon Punch on cooldown. RWK is 26% below reference CPM (2.0 vs 2.7), WDP is 17% below (1.9 vs 2.3). Both are high-priority damage abilities that should be used on cooldown. Combined, these two abilities account for roughly 1.0M missing damage. Set a WeakAura or track their cooldowns visually to minimize drift.
5. Interrupt more (Spear Hand Strike). Senssay interrupted once (1 SHS) vs Krylest's 3 times. Poison Burst from Sporecaps is a high-priority interrupt on Mythic Rotmire — each missed interrupt forces healing throughput and can cascade into deaths. Assign interrupt rotations in the raid team and ensure Senssay covers their kicks reliably.
