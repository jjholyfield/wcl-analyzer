Actionable Items

1. Increase Death Strike frequency from 9.9 to 14+ CPM
This is the single highest-impact change. Death Strike heals, generates Blood Shield (37.9% of all self-healing), and is the core of Blood DK's active mitigation loop. The reference casts DS every 3.8 seconds; Glazedwhole casts it every 6.1 seconds. More DS = more Blood Shield = more damage absorbed = fewer Purgatory procs (8 in one fight is unsustainably high). The fix feeds into Heart Strike as well: more Heart Strikes generate more Runic Power to fuel more Death Strikes.
2. Solve Echoing Darkness deaths in P1 (10 deaths, 37% of all)
Echoing Darkness from Vorelus killed Glazedwhole 9 times in P1 and once in intermission. This is a positional/mechanical issue — Echoing Darkness hits in rapid stacks of 15K–27K that overwhelm without absorbs active. Use Anti-Magic Shell for magic reduction during Echoing Darkness bursts (it was only used in 1.0 of the 10 P1-death pulls on average). Consider saving DRW for the Vorelus active window rather than using it on pull.
3. Use Icebound Fortitude on cooldown (12/27 pulls, avg 0.5 casts)
IBF is a 3-minute CD that reduces all damage taken by 30% and makes you immune to stuns. On a 6-minute fight, it should be used at least twice. It was used in only 44% of pulls and averaged just 0.5 casts per pull. In P2, plan IBF for the Simulacrum Backlash window (7 deaths to this ability in P2). In P3, use it during Cosmic Radiation overlaps.
4. Shift from Heart Strike and Marrowrend — fix rotation priority
Heart Strike is at 4.8 CPM vs the reference's 12.2 CPM (61% gap), while Marrowrend is at 2.0 vs 0.5 CPM (4x overcasting). Heart Strike generates Runic Power for Death Strike. Marrowrend should only be pressed when Bone Shield drops below 5 stacks. Every unnecessary Marrowrend is a Heart Strike that wasn't pressed, which is a Death Strike that won't happen. Similarly, Blood Boil is overcast (9.5 vs 6.6 CPM) — it should be used for Blood Plague maintenance and charge-capping only, not as a filler.
5. Use Anti-Magic Zone for raid-wide magic damage windows
AMZ was used in only 5 of 27 pulls. On a fight with heavy magic damage (Echoing Darkness, Stellar Emission, Cosmic Barrier, Voidstalker Sting, Cosmic Radiation are all magic), dropping AMZ during key windows helps not just the tank but nearby raiders. Coordinate with healers for intermission Stellar Emission and P2 Void Expulsion.
6. Strengths to maintain
Dancing Rune Weapon usage is excellent — used in all 27 pulls with consistent cast counts. Vampiric Blood usage is solid (21/27 pulls, 3.6 avg when used). Tank swap timing with Nucke is clean and consistent across phases. Session-long improvement trend is clear: the last 8 pulls all reached P2+, with 5 reaching P3. Blood Shield generation (37.9% of self-healing) shows the absorb system is working when DS is pressed. The learning trajectory is positive.
