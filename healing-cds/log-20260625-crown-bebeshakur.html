4 slots at 276 are the primary DPS bottleneck. Belt, Gloves, and both Rings are 13+ ilvls below the chest/weapon. Upgrading these 4 slots to 289+ would add approximately 3-5% overall DPS and close most of the gap with Unholyftw. The tier gloves especially should be catalyzed from a higher-ilvl token to maintain 4-piece set bonus.
Zero Versatility. Bebeshakur has 0 Versatility, trading all damage reduction for Mastery stacking (1,335 vs Unholyftw's 1,149). While Mastery is the best DPS stat for Unholy, having zero Vers means every hit hurts more. Consider picking up some Vers on new gear pieces as the raid pushes deeper into P3 where survival pressure escalates.

Actionable Items

1 Upgrade 276-ilvl Gear Slots (Belt, Gloves, Rings)
Four slots at 276 while other slots are 289-298. This is the largest available DPS gain. Belt and Gloves are highest priority (armor slots with more Strength per ilvl). Use Catalyst on a higher-ilvl glove token to maintain 4-piece tier. Replace both 276 rings with any 285+ drops. Estimated impact: 3-5% DPS, which would close the gap with Unholyftw despite the remaining ilvl delta.
2 Increase Haste (Currently 275 Rating)
Haste is roughly half of Unholyftw's (275 vs 428). Low Haste means slower GCDs, slower rune regeneration, and fewer procs per Dark Transformation window. When replacing the 276-ilvl pieces, prioritize Haste/Mastery or Haste/Crit itemization. Even reaching 350+ Haste would smooth the rotation and add 1-2 extra GCDs per burst window, which translates to 1-2 additional Scourge Strikes or Death Coils during each Army of the Dead cycle.
3 P3 Defensive Coordination (Raid-Wide Issue)
Bebe died in all 7 P3 pulls, but every death was cascade. The P3 wall is a collective problem, not an individual one. However, coordinating Anti-Magic Zone placement for specific P3 mechanics (Cosmic Radiation overlap, Bursting Emptiness) with the raid leader would add value. Having AMS available for the first Bursting Emptiness in P3 (~5:15-5:30) is high priority since it's the #1 P3 death cause across the raid.
4 Maintain Current Cooldown Discipline (Keep Doing This)
Army of the Dead on exact 90s cooldown, Dark Transformation on 45s cycle, Blood Fury and trinket stacked with Army windows. This is already textbook execution. The only potential optimization: if the raid plans Bloodlust for a specific P3 window, hold Army to align with Lust rather than using on pure cooldown. Otherwise, no changes needed.
5 Continue Death Grip Add Management in P2/P3
7 Death Grips in fight 14, including P2/P3 add management, is strong utility contribution. As the raid pushes deeper into P3, coordinate grip usage on Void Droplet spawns with Glazedwhole (Blood DK tank with their own grip). Stacking Droplets for cleave damage helps the entire raid's throughput.

Verdict

Bebeshakur is executing well above what the gear allows. At 287 ilvl with 4 slots at 276, Bebe is consistently top-3 DPS on medium-length pulls and was the #1 DPS in the raid on fight 23. The 4.6% P1 gap vs Unholyftw (292 ilvl) is gear-driven. In P2 and P3 where execution matters most during progression, Bebe outperforms the higher-geared peer by 5% and 34% respectively. Only 1 actionable death in 19 pulls is the best survival record among DPS. Cooldown alignment is textbook. The path to more DPS is gear upgrades (especially belt, gloves, rings), not rotation changes.
