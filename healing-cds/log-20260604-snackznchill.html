How Frost Mage Works

Frost Mage revolves around the Shatter mechanic — abilities deal massively increased damage to frozen targets. Your core combo is Flurry (freezes the target) immediately followed by Ice Lance (shatters the freeze). Every Flurry should have Ice Lances after it to consume the freeze.

Frostbolt is your filler that generates procs for Flurry and builds toward Glacial Spike. Glacial Spike stores up icicles and launches them all at once — you want to pair it with Flurry for a shattered Glacial Spike, which is your highest-damage combo.

Frozen Orb generates procs and deals sustained AoE damage. Ray of Frost is a channeled burst ability.

The key metric for Frost Mage is Ice Lance casts per minute. It’s your most-cast ability and indicates how many shatter combos you’re executing. More Flurry → more Ice Lance → more shattered damage. The Flurry-to-Ice Lance pipeline IS your DPS.

How It’s Affecting Your Damage

9 early deaths are the single biggest DPS destroyer. Dying in 30% of pulls obliterates average DPS. On death pulls, DPS drops catastrophically (86K on a death pull vs 265K on a clean pull). No amount of rotation improvement can overcome dying in nearly one-third of all attempts. This is problem #1 by a massive margin.
Flurry at 68% of #1 is cascading into everything else. At 5.2/m vs 7.6/m, Snackznchill is missing 2.4 Flurries per minute. Each Flurry enables 2-3 shattered Ice Lances, so the Flurry deficit IS the Ice Lance deficit. This single gap explains the 5.7 missing Ice Lances per minute — the primary damage ability.
Too much Frostbolt, not enough proc conversion. Casting 118% of #1’s Frostbolt rate while casting only 68% of their Flurry rate means procs are being generated but not consumed quickly enough. The rotation is spending too long in filler and not enough time executing the shatter combo that actually deals damage.
Glacial Spike, Frozen Orb, and Ray of Frost all below target. All three are at 79-85% of #1. These are the secondary damage sources that add up over a fight. Each one missed is damage left on the table. Combined with the Flurry/Ice Lance deficit, every part of the rotation is underperforming.

What You Need to Change

1
Stop Dying
9/30 early deaths is destroying your DPS. Use Ice Cold, Shimmer, and Ice Barrier before damage hits, not after. Use healthstones and health potions proactively — 47% HS and 53% HP means you’re leaving survival tools on the table. Multiple different mechanics are killing you, which means this is an awareness problem, not a mechanic-specific one. Every death is a pull where your DPS contribution goes to zero.
2
More Flurry Casts
Target 7+ Flurries per minute. You’re at 5.2, the #1 is at 7.6. This is the root cause of the entire rotation gap. Every Flurry you miss = 2-3 shattered Ice Lances that never happen. React to Brain Freeze procs faster. When Flurry lights up, cast it immediately — do not finish a Frostbolt first.
3
More Ice Lance
Target 22+ Ice Lances per minute. Currently at 18.6 vs 24.3 for #1. Ice Lance is your most-cast and most important damage ability. Fixing Flurry will naturally fix most of this, but also make sure you’re casting 2 Ice Lances after every Flurry to fully consume the shatter window.
4
Pre-Pot Every Pull
25/30 is decent but missing 5 pulls is unnecessary free damage left on the table. Pre-pot should be automatic — Potion of Recklessness every pull, no exceptions.
