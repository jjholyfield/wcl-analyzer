How Arms Warrior Works

Arms Warrior is a melee spec built around a rage economy. You generate rage through auto-attacks and abilities like Overpower, then spend it on Mortal Strike (your hardest-hitting single target ability) and Cleave/Whirlwind for AoE.

The core priority is: Mortal Strike on cooldown > Overpower (free, generates rage) > spend rage on Slam or Cleave depending on targets.

Colossus Smash is your debuff window — it amplifies all your damage on the target. You want to line up your hardest-hitting abilities during Colossus Smash windows. Demolish is a powerful talent that deals strong damage. Avatar is your major CD that increases damage and rage generation.

The spec rewards consistent button pressing and rage management. Never cap rage (waste), never starve rage (dead GCDs). Sweeping Strikes doubles your single-target abilities to hit a second target — critical for cleave windows on LBV.

How It’s Affecting Your Damage

Mortal Strike at 75% of #1 is the single biggest DPS loss. Starfighter is casting 10.1 Mortal Strikes per minute vs Kevln’s 13.5. Mortal Strike is the hardest-hitting ability in the Arms rotation — every missed cast is a direct chunk of damage gone. On a 5-minute pull, that’s 17 fewer Mortal Strikes across the fight.
Demolish at 71% means missing cooldown uses. Demolish deals massive damage and should be used on cooldown. At 2.0/m vs 2.8/m, Starfighter is leaving roughly one Demolish on the table every ~75 seconds — that adds up across a pull.
Sweeping Strikes at 67% is a big cleave loss on a boss with add phases. Sweeping Strikes doubles your single-target abilities to hit a second target. On LBV, where there are regular multi-target windows, missing a third of your Sweeping Strikes casts means losing substantial cleave damage during the windows where it matters most.
CPM and dead time are excellent. 53.8 CPM (96% of #1) and 3.2% dead time mean Starfighter is pressing buttons almost as fast as the best Arms Warriors. The issue is purely priority — shifting globals from lower-value casts (extra Cleave) to higher-value casts (Mortal Strike, Demolish, Sweeping Strikes) would close much of the DPS gap without pressing any faster.

What You Need to Change

1
More Mortal Strike — Target 13+ per Minute
This is the biggest gap and the biggest fix. Mortal Strike is the highest-priority ability in the Arms rotation and the hardest hitter. At 10.1/m vs 13.5/m, Starfighter is missing 3.4 Mortal Strikes every minute. Press it immediately when it comes off cooldown — before Cleave, before Slam, before anything except Colossus Smash.
2
More Demolish — Use on Cooldown
At 71% of #1, Starfighter is missing Demolish casts regularly. Demolish is a powerful damage ability on a cooldown — every time it’s available and not pressed, that’s free damage left on the table. Track the cooldown and hit it as soon as it’s up.
3
More Sweeping Strikes — Use Before Every Multi-Target Window
At 67% of #1, Starfighter is missing a third of his cleave windows. On LBV, adds show up regularly — Sweeping Strikes should be active for every one of those windows. The irony is Starfighter casts more Cleave than the #1, but gets less value from it because Sweeping Strikes isn’t up to double those hits.
4
Use Healthstones
5/30 (17%) is too low. Healthstones are a free heal on a separate cooldown from health pots. On mythic progression, using one every pull during heavy damage is the difference between dying and surviving. Two of his four deaths might have been prevented by a Healthstone.
