How Augmentation Evoker Works

Augmentation Evoker is a support-damage hybrid. Your job is to keep Ebon Might active on your allies while dealing damage yourself through Eruption and Living Flame. Your damage shows up partly on your meter and partly on the people you’re buffing through Ebon Might.

Ebon Might is the core buff — it empowers your 4 nearest DPS allies with primary stat. You cast it to apply it, and it extends automatically when you cast Eruption, Living Flame, Fire Breath, or Upheaval. The key is to NEVER let Ebon Might fall off. If you stop pressing damage buttons, Ebon Might drops and 4 DPS players lose their buff.

Eruption is your #1 damage button — it hits hard, extends Ebon Might, and should be pressed as often as possible. Living Flame is your filler that also extends Ebon Might — every empty GCD should be a Living Flame. Both are critical for maintaining Ebon Might uptime.

Prescience is your secondary buff — crit chance on allies, cast on CD, never overcap charges.

The core loop is: Ebon Might → Eruption spam → Fire Breath/Upheaval on cooldown → Living Flame as filler → Breath of Eons for burst windows. Every global spent on anything other than this loop is DPS lost for both you AND the raid.

The #1 Aug Evoker (Blueprínt, 281K DPS) casts 22.8 Eruptions and 12.5 Living Flames per minute. Mheeraidon is at 16.1 and 5.1 respectively. That’s 14 fewer damage globals every minute — roughly one missing damage cast every 4 seconds.

Where are those globals going? Two places: off-healing (Verdant Embrace 0.5/m + Emerald Blossom 0.4/m = ~1 wasted global per minute) and dead time (21.8% — more than 1 in 5 seconds doing absolutely nothing). The off-healing explains some of it, but the dead time is the bigger issue — 12 seconds of every minute spent idle.

How It’s Affecting Your Damage

Living Flame at 41% of #1 — this is catastrophic. 5.1/m vs 12.5/m. Missing roughly 7 Living Flames every minute. Living Flame is your filler — the button you press when everything else is on cooldown. At 41%, there are long stretches where nothing is being pressed at all, which is confirmed by the 21.8% dead time.
Eruption at 71% of #1. 16.1/m vs 22.8/m — missing roughly 7 Eruptions per minute. Each missed Eruption is both personal damage lost AND less Ebon Might extension for the raid. This is the same problem as Balecoda (the other Aug Evoker).
Dead time at 21.8% — highest of any Evoker on the team. More than 1 in 5 seconds doing nothing. At 55.3 minutes of active time, that’s over 12 minutes of total idle time across the night. Every idle second is a missed Living Flame or Eruption.
Off-healing is stealing damage globals. Verdant Embrace (0.5/m) and Emerald Blossom (0.4/m) together are ~1 wasted global per minute. That’s 1 Eruption or Living Flame lost every minute, which also means less Ebon Might extension. Trust the healers to heal.
Ebon Might at 127% of #1 — recasting more because it keeps falling off. 1.4/m vs 1.1/m. When Eruption and Living Flame rates are this low, Ebon Might doesn’t get extended enough and drops off, forcing extra recasts. Fix the Eruption/Living Flame issue and the Ebon Might issue fixes itself.

What You Need to Change

1
More Living Flame — Target 12+ Per Minute
Currently at 5.1/m, the #1 is at 12.5/m. This is the #1 priority. Living Flame is your filler — every empty GCD should be a Living Flame. If you’re not casting Eruption, Fire Breath, Upheaval, or Prescience, you should be casting Living Flame. Never stand still.
2
More Eruption — Target 22+ Per Minute
Currently at 16.1/m, the #1 is at 22.8/m. Eruption is your main damage button and your best Ebon Might extension. Cast it any time you have essence. More Eruptions = more personal damage + better Ebon Might uptime for the raid.
3
Stop Off-Healing
Verdant Embrace and Emerald Blossom together are ~1 wasted global per minute. That’s 1 Eruption or Living Flame lost every minute — DPS you and the raid never get back. You have 6 healers in the raid. Trust them to heal. Your job is to keep Ebon Might up and deal damage.
4
Reduce Dead Time — Target Under 12%
Currently at 21.8%. That’s over 12 seconds of every minute spent idle. The fix is simple — if you don’t know what to press, press Living Flame. There should never be a moment where you’re not casting something.
5
Health Pot More Often
6/14 pulls (43%) should be higher. Health Potion is a separate cooldown from your pre-pot. Pre-pot is perfect at 100% — apply the same discipline to Health Potion.
