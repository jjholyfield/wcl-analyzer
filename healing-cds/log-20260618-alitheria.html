Actionable Improvements

1. Survive Phase 2 — This is THE priority
Impact: Would transform DPS from 25.9K avg to potentially 35K+ avg.

8 of 11 deaths occur in P2 (2:53–5:09). The three killing mechanics are Grasp of Emptiness (3 deaths), Simulacrum Backlash (3 deaths), and Bursting Emptiness (2 deaths).
  • Grasp of Emptiness: Pre-position away from the grip zone. Use Divine Protection or Divine Shield if you get caught. Deaths at 4:00, 4:07, 4:27 suggest this happens around the 2nd Grasp cast in P2.
  • Simulacrum Backlash: Stay away from the Rift Simulacrum. This is 13.3M total damage taken across 9 pulls — the #3 highest damage source overall. Melee range on the Simulacrum is deadly.
  • Bursting Emptiness: Move out of void detonation zones. Use a personal CD if you cannot move in time.
2. Pre-plan defensive cooldowns for known damage windows
Impact: Would prevent 3+ deaths per session.

Ret Paladin has excellent defensives: Divine Shield (immune), Divine Protection (20% DR), Shield of Vengeance (absorb), Lay on Hands (full heal). Currently, these are being used reactively (panic-casting at low HP) rather than proactively before known damage.

Suggested defensive plan:
  • Divine Protection for every Grasp of Emptiness (the ~25s-interval ability in P2)
  • Divine Shield saved for the most dangerous Grasp overlap or if debuffed by Voidstalker Sting
  • Shield of Vengeance on cooldown during P2 as a rolling absorb
  • Healthstone/health pot reserved for P2, not used in P1 where damage is lighter
3. Maintain P1-level activity in P2
Impact: ~20% DPS increase during P2.

P1 CPM is high (86 CPM on best pull). P2 CPM drops significantly due to movement and mechanic handling. On Fight 27 (the pull with full P2 survival), overall CPM was 68.8 — a 20% drop from the best pull.

Ret Paladin has good tools for maintaining uptime during movement: Divine Steed for repositioning, and instant-cast generators (Hammer of Wrath, Judgment) during transitions. Focus on pressing something during every GCD in P2, even if it means using suboptimal fillers.
4. Avoid early P1 deaths
Impact: Eliminates the two worst pulls (F18 and F21).

Two pulls had deaths at 0:14 (Bursting Emptiness) and 0:40 (Void Remnants) — both in the first minute. These early deaths require a battle rez and result in massively reduced DPS (F21 was 9.4K). The first Void Remnants spawn and Bursting Emptiness are predictable at fixed timers. Pre-position before they happen.
5. Build P3 experience (aspirational)
Alitheria only entered P3 alive once (Fight 27, died at 5:13 to Dimensional Slash). Until P2 survival is consistent, P3 mechanics are secondary. But awareness of Dimensional Slash (ground void zones), returning Vorelus, and Cosmic Radiation will be needed once the P2 wall is broken.

Summary

What is already working
  • P1 execution is strong. Average P1 DPS of 45.3K with a peak of 80.4K shows solid add cleave and cooldown usage during the Sentinels phase.
  • Rotation is clean when alive. 86 CPM on the best pull, 5 Avenging Wrath uses in 5 minutes, proper Final Verdict as primary spender. The spec fundamentals are there.
  • Competitive within the raid. 6th of 13 DPS on the best pull shows Alitheria can keep up when surviving.
  • Consumables used. Pre-pot, healthstone, and trinket usage is present on best pulls.

The performance gap is 100% survival-based, not rotation or gear. Alitheria's ilvl (290) matches top-ranked Ret Paladins. Their P1 damage output is competitive. The single highest-impact change is surviving Phase 2 mechanics — specifically Grasp of Emptiness, Simulacrum Backlash, and Bursting Emptiness. Fixing this alone would likely bring average DPS from ~26K to 35K+ and open the door to P3 experience.
