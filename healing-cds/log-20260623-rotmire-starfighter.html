Actionable Improvements

1. Increase Rampage Usage (Highest Impact)
Estimated impact: +8-12% DPS from improved Enrage uptime
Starfighter cast Rampage 140 times (17.2 CPM) vs reference's 181 (20.7 CPM) — a 3.5 CPM deficit. Rampage triggers Enrage, which is a 30-50% damage multiplier on everything else. Every missed Rampage cascades: lower Enrage uptime reduces the value of every other GCD. Fix: Prioritize Rampage at 80+ Rage, especially outside Recklessness windows. Do not sit on high Rage waiting for a "perfect" moment — Enrage uptime is king.
2. Bloodthirst Priority Over Raging Blow
Estimated impact: +3-5% DPS from better Rage generation
Starfighter cast Raging Blow 68 times (8.3 CPM) — significantly more than the reference's 42 (4.8 CPM). Meanwhile, Bloodthirst was 87 casts (10.7 CPM) vs reference's 114 (13.0 CPM). Bloodthirst generates more Rage than Raging Blow and has a chance to proc Enrage directly. Fix: In the rotation priority, Bloodthirst should come before Raging Blow outside of Recklessness. Raging Blow is a filler GCD, not a priority ability. More Bloodthirst = more Rage = more Rampage = more Enrage.
3. Reduce Thunder Clap Filler Usage
Estimated impact: +1-2% DPS from better GCD allocation
Starfighter cast Thunder Clap 40 times (4.9 CPM) vs reference's 23 (2.6 CPM). Thunder Clap is the lowest-priority filler in Mountain Thane — it should only be pressed when literally nothing else is available. Several of these GCDs would be better spent on Bloodthirst or Raging Blow. Fix: Thunder Clap only when Rampage, Bloodthirst, Execute, Thunder Blast, and Raging Blow are all unavailable.
4. Festering Vines Uptime (Mechanic-Specific)
Estimated impact: +2-3% DPS during movement phases
CPS during Festering Vines windows was consistently the weakest area. Vines #1 (68%), #2 (83%), #4 (80%), #5 (73%) were all significantly below reference. Meanwhile, Awaken Fungi windows were generally strong (often above reference). Fix: Pre-position before Vines to minimize movement. Use Charge/Heroic Leap to quickly break free and return to melee range. Queue instant abilities (Rampage, Execute) while moving.
5. Add Cleave Commitment
Estimated impact: +3-5% total damage from better cleave allocation
Boss/adds damage split was 46/54 for Starfighter vs 38/62 for the reference. The reference committed significantly more damage to adds, which suggests more aggressive Whirlwind/Thunder Clap usage during add waves and better target-switching during Awaken Fungi. On Mythic Rotmire, killing adds cleanly (especially before Fungal Bloom converts corpses) is critical. Fix: During Awaken Fungi windows, focus damage on adds. Use Whirlwind before Rampage to apply Improved Whirlwind for cleave damage.
