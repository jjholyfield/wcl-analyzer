Actionable Improvements

1. Use Ice Cold proactively during P2 damage overlap
Impact: Prevent 8-10 of 12 deaths.
Ice Cold was only pressed 2 times across 12 pulls. With a 2.5-minute cooldown, it is available for the critical P2 window (3:30-4:30) every single pull. The death sequences show Voidstalker Sting + Simulacrum Backlash dealing ~45K sustained DPS -- Ice Cold's brief immunity breaks this pressure and lets healers recover. Plan to press Ice Cold at the first sign of heavy P2 overlap (around 3:30-3:45 when Sting + Backlash first stack).
2. Incorporate Alter Time into P2 survival rotation
Impact: Secondary defensive layer for P2 survival.
Alter Time was used only 2 times in 1 pull (F18). This is a powerful survival tool that snapshots your position and health, then lets you return if things go wrong. Activate Alter Time when healthy before a known P2 damage window (before Void Expulsion or when you see Voidstalker Sting applied), then release if health drops below 40%. Between Ice Cold and Alter Time, P2 should have two major defensive windows available.
3. Pre-shield Ice Barrier before P2 damage spikes
Impact: Absorb 1-2 ticks of incoming damage per shield.
Ice Barrier averages 3.7 uses per pull which is decent, but the death sequences show damage trains of 8-25 hits with no absorb shield active. The 25-second cooldown means Ice Barrier should be pressed every time it comes off cooldown during P2. Make it reflexive: every 25 seconds in P2, refresh Ice Barrier whether it has been consumed or not.
4. Use Healthstone and Potion in every pull
Impact: Extra ~80-100K healing per pull.
Healthstone used 8/12 pulls, Potion used 8/12 pulls. Both should be 12/12. During P2 progression, survival > DPS optimization. A Healthstone at 50% HP during Sting + Backlash overlap buys 2-3 extra seconds of survival -- which across 12 pulls could mean reaching P3 in 3-4 more attempts.
5. P1 execution is strong -- maintain it
No changes needed for P1.
400K average multi-target DPS in P1 with zero deaths across 12 pulls is clean execution. The Frost Mage cleave toolkit (Shatter, Frost Splinter, Frozen Orb) is being used well on the sentinel bosses. Intermission survival is also 100%. The problem is isolated to P2 survival, not DPS or P1 mechanics.

Verdict

Snackznchill's core DPS execution is competitive with the other Frost Mage when both survive to similar points. The 28.5% improvement from early pulls to late pulls shows active learning throughout the session. The problem is singular and clear: P2 survival. Every death occurs in Phase 2 from the combination of Voidstalker Sting, Simulacrum Backlash, and Void Expulsion creating sustained pressure that exceeds healing throughput without personal defensives active.

The fix is defensive cooldown discipline, not rotation changes. Ice Cold (2 uses across 12 pulls) and Alter Time (2 uses across 1 pull) are the two highest-impact levers. Using both proactively in P2 would create two 10-15 second survival windows per pull, which based on the death timing data would likely prevent 8-10 of the 12 deaths and allow consistent P3 entry.
