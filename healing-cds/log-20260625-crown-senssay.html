Actionable Items

1. Survive P1 Mechanics: Bursting Emptiness and Silverstrike Arrow

The 2 actionable deaths (F21 and F22) were both in P1 to avoidable mechanics. F21: first-to-die to Bursting Emptiness at 1:05. F22: second-to-die to Silverstrike Arrow at 1:03. Both pulls had no defensive CD usage before death. Use Fortifying Brew preemptively when these mechanics are casting. Dampen Harm or Diffuse Magic (depending on talent) can also mitigate.

2. Shift Revival from P1 to P2/P3

Revival was used 9 times in P1 vs 7 in P2 and 2 in P3. P1 damage is the lowest -- manageable with Yu'lon and Celestial Conduit. P2 and especially P3 have the heaviest raid damage. On 9 pulls reaching P3, only 2 had Revival. Target: hold Revival for the Intermission transition (~2:14) or early P2 (~2:53) on every pull.

3. Reduce Celestial Conduit Overheal (82.2%)

Celestial Conduit generated 1848.2K effective healing vs 8512.7K overheal. Align the 90-second CD with major damage events: ~1:05 Bursting Emptiness in P1, ~2:14 Silversunder Catastrophe for Intermission, or the heavy P2/P3 damage windows. Currently firing during quiet moments.

4. Strong Overall Throughput and Team Standing

Averaging 156.2K HPS with 32.7% overheal. Ranked #1 or #2 healer on 9/10 deep pulls, consistently competitive with Dueche (HPal, 158.5K avg). Yu'lon usage is excellent (18/19). TFT usage consistent at ~25s intervals. The fundamentals are solid -- gains come from CD timing optimization, not rotation mechanics.

5. Excellent Survival on Compromised Pulls

Of 18 deaths across 19 pulls, 16 were cascade deaths (death order #5+, well after the pull was lost). Senssay is consistently one of the last healers standing on compromised pulls. Only the 2 P1 deaths require individual correction.
