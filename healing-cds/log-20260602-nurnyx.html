How Discipline Priest Works

Discipline Priest heals through Atonement — when you deal damage, your Atonement buffs on allies convert that damage into healing. The more Atonements active, the more people you heal with each damage spell. This makes Disc a unique spec where DPS and healing are the same thing.

Core loop: Apply Atonement to allies with Power Word: Shield and Power Word: Radiance. Then deal damage with Penance, Smite, Shadow Word: Pain, Shadow Word: Death, and Mind Blast — that damage heals everyone with Atonement. Flash Heal is your direct heal for emergencies when Atonement healing isn’t enough.

Evangelism extends all active Atonements by 6 seconds — use it during burst heal windows when you’ve applied many Atonements. Power Infusion gives a haste buff — use on yourself or a DPS player.

Major defensives: Pain Suppression (single-target DR), Power Word: Barrier (raid-wide DR), Desperate Prayer (self-heal).

The key to Disc is that your DPS IS your healing. More Smite casts = more Atonement healing. PW:Shield uptime = more Atonements active = more people healed per damage spell.

How This Is Affecting Your Healing

PW:Shield at 47% of top (2.3/min vs 4.9/min). This is the biggest gap. PW:Shield applies Atonement. Fewer shields = fewer Atonements active = less Atonement healing from your damage spells. This is the foundation of Disc healing.
Smite at 60% of top (3.7/min vs 6.2/min). Lower DPS filler means less Atonement healing output. Smite is your bread-and-butter damage spell that heals through Atonement.
SW:Death at 44% of top (1.5/min vs 3.4/min). Another damage spell that feeds Atonement healing. Top players press it more aggressively.
Flash Heal at 168% of top (3.7/min vs 2.2/min). Casting more direct heals instead of damage-to-heal through Atonement. Flash Heal doesn’t benefit from the Atonement multiplier — it’s less efficient than trusting your damage to heal.

What You Need to Change

1
More PW:Shield — 2.3/min needs to be 4.9
Double your shield casts to double your Atonement coverage. Every shield you cast means one more person healed by your damage spells. This is the single biggest thing you can change. More Atonements active = more healing from every Smite, Penance, SW:Pain, SW:Death, and Mind Blast you cast.
2
More Smite and SW:Death — these feed Atonement healing
Smite at 3.7/min needs to be 5+. SW:Death at 1.5/min needs to be 3+. These damage spells are your healing spells through Atonement. Every cast you’re missing is healing you’re not doing.
3
Less Flash Heal — trust Atonement
3.7/min vs top’s 2.2/min. Flash Heal is your emergency direct heal, not your primary healing tool. Trust Atonement to do the healing through damage. If you need more throughput, the answer is more PW:Shield + more damage, not more Flash Heal.

Use health potion and pre-pot consistently
