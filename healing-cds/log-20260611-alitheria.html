Bottom Line

On the longest pull (Fight 23, 4:53), Alitheria dealt 231.7K DPS across 2:31 of alive time before dying to Silverstrike Barrage in the Phase 1 transition. During that alive window, rotational CPM was 77.5 vs 96.6 compared to Bilaevropa (#2 ranked, 99.2K DPS full-fight kill) in the same 0–2:31 window — a 20% CPM deficit. The gap is distributed across nearly every ability: Divine Hammer −32%, Crusading Strikes −21%, Hammer of Wrath −36%, and Empyrean Hammer −15%. With 6 talent differences and nearly identical ilvl (290.3 vs 290.0), the gap is execution-based. The early death at 2:31 is the biggest DPS loss — dying mid-fight on prog eliminates any contribution to the later phases entirely. Staying alive through the Silverstrike Barrage transition would immediately add 50%+ to effective fight DPS.

On longer pulls (F10, F12, F14, F18, F23–26), DPS drops to 94–170K — often because Alitheria died early and the fight continued without them. The pattern shows strong Phase 1 execution on shorter attempts (where the fight ends before the deadly transition), but consistent death around the 2:00–2:30 mark when longer pulls reach the transition mechanics. Survival through transitions is the bottleneck, not rotational throughput.

Fights 10, 12, 14, 18, 23–26 are longer (3:40–4:53) but show high boss % remaining (77–100%) — these are pulls where the raid reached later phases but struggled. Alitheria's full-fight DPS on these pulls is suppressed by death, not by poor play while alive.

Actionable Items

1. Survive the Silverstrike Barrage transition (2:00–2:40 mark). This is the single biggest DPS improvement available. Alitheria dies at 2:31 in Fight 23 and shows a pattern of dying around the same time in other long pulls. The Silverstrike Barrage hits for 365K in one tick. Fix: save Divine Protection or Shield of Vengeance specifically for this mechanic. Divine Shield was used at 1:55 — 36 seconds before death — consider holding it for the Barrage window. Also ensure you are not taking the 196K falling damage, which reduces your health buffer. Estimated gain: surviving to fight end would double effective fight DPS from ~106K to ~200K+.
2. Increase GCD efficiency between cooldown windows (77.5 CPM → target 90+ CPM). The 20% CPM gap vs the reference is across every ability, not concentrated in one spell. Divine Hammer at 15.9 vs 23.4 CPM (−32%) suggests Consecration is dropping during movement. Crusading Strikes at 16.3 vs 20.7 CPM (−21%) means auto-attack / melee downtime. Between major cooldowns, keep Consecration active (refreshes Divine Hammer) and stay in melee range to generate Crusading Strikes procs. The 12.6-second gap at 1:22–1:34 is a phase transition, but the ref still manages better filler around transitions. Estimated gain: closing to 90+ CPM would add ~20K to alive-time DPS.
3. Use Shield of Vengeance offensively. The reference dealt 735K damage from Shield of Vengeance reflected damage in the same time window. Alitheria never used it. Shield of Vengeance absorbs damage and then explodes for the absorbed amount — on a fight with heavy sustained raid damage (Echoing Darkness ticks), it deals significant free damage. Use it before major damage events. If you need to save it as a defensive for Silverstrike Barrage (see item 1), that is the higher priority — but if another defensive covers Barrage, Shield of Vengeance should be used offensively.
4. Increase Hammer of Wrath usage (2.8 CPM → target 4.0+ CPM). Alitheria used Hammer of Wrath 7 times vs the reference's 11 in 151 seconds (−36%). During Avenging Wrath, Judgment becomes Hammer of Wrath (via Radiant Glory), giving access to this powerful ability for 24 seconds per AW window. The reference fires more Hammers by using Art of War procs (which reset Blade of Justice, which enables more Holy Power, which enables more Final Verdicts, which trigger more Empyrean Hammers). Weaving Hammer of Wrath during AW windows and whenever targets are below 20% is a direct throughput gain.
5. Avoid falling damage (196K taken). This is a Crown-specific mechanic — likely falling between platforms or missing a feather during Devouring Cosmos. Taking 196K unnecessary damage reduces the health buffer needed to survive transition mechanics like Silverstrike Barrage. Use Divine Steed or feather pickups to avoid falling, and position for platform transitions early.

Best pulls show strong DPS: Fights 8, 9, 15, 17, and 21 all show excellent alive-time DPS (220–267K range), reaching sub-10% boss with strong Phase 1 contributions. The ceiling is clearly there — it is survival, not damage output, that is the limiting factor.

Judgment usage: The one ability where Alitheria exceeds the reference (+8% CPM). Good Judgment prioritization is important for Sanctification stacking and Holy Power generation.

Summary

Alitheria's alive-time DPS (231.7K) is competitive with ranked players' early-fight output. The 17.5% gap vs Bilaevropa in the same 0–2:31 window comes from ~60% GCD efficiency (20% lower CPM across all abilities) and ~20% proc/passive reduction (Highlord's Judgment, Empyrean Hammer) that cascades from lower cast volume, with ~20% from external buff differences (fewer Ebon Might/Prescience applications). However, the dominant issue is death at 2:31 — full-fight DPS drops to 106K because Alitheria is dead for 48% of the fight. Crown of the Cosmos is a 8+ minute fight. Surviving to the end is worth more than any rotation optimization. Priority: (1) survive through transitions with proper defensive timing, (2) tighten GCD usage between mechanics, (3) use Shield of Vengeance offensively.
