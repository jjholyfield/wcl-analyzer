P1 Performance: Strong

234.7K average DPS in P1 across all 11 pulls with high consistency (194K–286K range). This is a multi-target cleave phase with Sentinels dying sequentially, which suits MM Hunter's Trick Shots and Volley. P1 execution is solid — this is not where the problem is.

P2 Performance: 74% DPS drop from P1

DPS drops from 234.7K in P1 to 61.4K in P2 — a 74% decrease. This is partly expected (fewer targets, more movement from Rift Slash every 12s), but 61K is extremely low for an MM Hunter. Top-ranked MM Hunters on Crown maintain 90K+ in P2. Deaths account for some of this (4 P2 deaths cutting damage short), but even on longer P2 survival pulls (F18: 67s in P2, F19: 140s in P2), DPS was 86K and 29K respectively.

P2 DPS investigation

On fight 19 (longest P2 survival at 140s in P2), Darthurg only did 28.6K DPS. This suggests significant uptime loss from either: (1) running mechanics and not casting, (2) dying and being battle-rezzed (the second death in fight 19 was at 5:12 near P2/P3 boundary with Cosmic Radiation already active), or (3) unfamiliarity with P2 positioning causing extended downtime. On fight 18 (67s in P2), DPS was 85.7K which is closer to expected. The variance suggests inconsistent P2 execution.

Actionable Improvements

Prioritized by impact. Survival is the #1 priority — a dead DPS does zero damage. Rotation optimization is secondary until survival is consistent through P2.

1

Pre-assign Aspect of the Turtle to Intermission

3 of 10 deaths (30%) occurred during intermission, primarily to Orbiting Matter (310K single hit) and Silverstrike Barrage (537K). Aspect of the Turtle was used only 4 times across 11 pulls and was reactive on fight 20 (cast 1 second AFTER the killing hit). Plan: Turtle at the start of Orbiting Matter. This mechanic is on a fixed timer during intermission and can be anticipated.

Impact: Eliminates ~30% of deaths. Surviving intermission is the prerequisite for all P2/P3 learning.
2

Health management entering P2 — Cosmic Barrier transition

Fights 15 and 16 both show death at 3:02 to Cosmic Barrier ticking damage (26K/tick) immediately upon P2 entrance. Darthurg entered P2 without full health after surviving intermission. Use Exhilaration during intermission (it has no DPS cost since there are no targets) to enter P2 at full health, and have Survival of the Fittest ready for the P2 transition.

Impact: Eliminates 2 deaths. Cosmic Barrier is constant periodic damage in P2 — entering at full HP is the difference between surviving and dying in the first 10 seconds.
3

Eliminate falling damage

380K falling damage across 2 hits on fight 25. This is environmental damage from knockbacks or poor positioning near edges. As a Hunter, Disengage mid-air completely negates fall damage. There is no excuse for taking falling damage as a Marksmanship Hunter.

Impact: 380K is a significant health chunk that likely contributed to the P3 death at 5:58. Completely avoidable.
4

P2 uptime — maintain DPS through Rift Slash movement

P2 DPS averages 61.4K vs 234.7K in P1 (a 74% drop). Even accounting for fewer targets, this drop is excessive. On fight 19 (140s alive in P2), DPS was only 28.6K. MM Hunter can maintain DPS during movement using Arcane Shot (instant), Volley, and Rapid Fire while repositioning for Rift Slash (fires every 12s). Practice the P2 movement pattern to minimize GCD gaps during Rift Slash repositioning.

Impact: Improving P2 DPS from 61K to even 80K on a 2.5-minute P2 phase adds ~2.9M total damage to the raid.
5

Use Moonlight Chakram more consistently (Sentinel talent)

Only 1 Moonlight Chakram cast on fight 25 (the longest pull). Per the Sentinel hero tree guide, Moonlight Chakram should be used regularly and specifically at the end of Trueshot windows to extend value. It is an instant-cast ability that can be woven between other casts and used during movement.

Impact: Minor DPS gain per cast, but adds up over 6+ minute fights. Low effort improvement.

Summary

What's working well

P1 DPS is strong — 234.7K average across 11 pulls with low variance (194K–286K). This is a cleave-heavy phase that plays to MM Hunter strengths with Trick Shots and multi-dotting via Aimed Shot.

Cooldown discipline is good — Trueshot used 1.6x per pull average, Blood Fury tracked with Trueshot, Volley used frequently for Double Tap activation.

Slight improvement trend — late-session pulls averaged 4.4% higher active DPS than early-session, suggesting fight learning is happening.

The core issue

Survival, not rotation, is the bottleneck. Darthurg survived 1 out of 11 pulls (9%). The single survival was a short P1-only pull. The intermission is the primary wall: 3 deaths from Orbiting Matter and Silverstrike Barrage, all avoidable with proper Aspect of the Turtle timing. An additional 2 deaths at the P2 entrance to Cosmic Barrier suggest entering P2 at low health.

Once intermission survival is solved, P2 uptime needs work — the 74% DPS drop from P1 to P2 is excessive even with the target count reduction.

P3 experience is minimal (3 pulls, 1 death, minimal DPS). More P3 time requires solving intermission and P2 survival first.
