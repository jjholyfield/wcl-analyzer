How Retribution Paladin Works

Retribution Paladin is a melee spec built around generating and spending Holy Power. The core loop is: use generators (Blade of Justice, Judgment, Hammer of Wrath, Wake of Ashes) to build Holy Power, then spend it on Divine Storm (AoE finisher) or Templar’s Verdict (single target finisher).

Crusading Strikes is your auto-attack replacement — it fires automatically between abilities and generates Holy Power. The more abilities you press, the more Crusading Strikes fire. This is why Crusading Strikes per minute is the #1 indicator of Ret Paladin performance — it directly reflects your overall GCD usage.

Avenging Wrath is your major burst cooldown. Execution Sentence and Divine Toll are powerful cooldowns that should be used on CD. Hammer of Wrath becomes available during Avenging Wrath and against low-health targets.

The gap between average and great Ret Paladins is almost entirely CPM. Ret has a fast, GCD-locked rotation — if you’re pressing buttons fast enough, Holy Power generation and spending take care of themselves. Low Crusading Strikes means slow GCD usage, which cascades into fewer generators, fewer spenders, and less damage across the board.

What You Need to Change

1
Press Buttons Faster
Crusading Strikes at 66% means the GCD is empty too often. Target 35+ Crusading Strikes per minute. This single change will improve every other metric — more Crusading Strikes means more Holy Power generation, which means more Divine Storms, which means more damage. The Ret rotation is fast and GCD-locked. If there’s ever a moment where nothing is being pressed, something is wrong.
2
Stop Dying
5/16 (31%) is unsustainable. With Divine Protection (35 casts, clearly available), Divine Shield (13 casts), and Lay on Hands (13 casts), there are multiple tools to survive. Use Divine Shield proactively during Divine Toll if health is dropping. 3 of 5 deaths are to the same mechanic — this is a solvable problem.
3
More Hammer of Wrath
4.1/m vs 5.7/m (72%). Hammer of Wrath becomes available during Avenging Wrath windows and against low-health targets. Make sure it’s being used aggressively in both situations — it’s a free Holy Power generator with no GCD conflict.
4
Use Healthstones More
8/16 (50%) healthstone usage should be higher, especially with a 31% death rate. Healthstone is a free heal on a separate cooldown from Health Potion. With 5 deaths in 16 pulls, every available survival tool needs to be used. Target 80%+ usage.
