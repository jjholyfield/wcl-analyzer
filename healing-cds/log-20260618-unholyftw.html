Unholy DK Rotation Context (Patch 12.0.7)

Key rotation knowledge relevant to Crown of the Cosmos analysis. Sourced from Icy Veins and Wowhead current guides.

Core Rotation

  • Maintain Virulent Plague via Outbreak
  • Build Festering Wounds with Festering Strike (aim 2-4 stacks)
  • Consume with Scourge Strike / Clawing Shadows
  • Spend Sudden Doom procs with Death Coil immediately
  • Soul Reaper below 35% HP or during Dark Transformation
  • Swap Death Coil for Epidemic at 3+ targets (P1 add cleave)

Major Cooldowns

  • Dark Transformation (45s) -- primary damage amplifier, aggressive use during window
  • Army of the Dead (90s) -- aligns every other DT, triggers Forbidden Knowledge
  • Summon Gargoyle -- buff by spending Runic Power during uptime
  • Putrefy -- use charges during Dark Transformation window

Defensive Toolkit (Critical for Prog)

  • Anti-Magic Shell (AMS) -- absorbs magic damage, generates Runic Power. Key for Void Remnants, Bursting Emptiness, Stellar Emission.
  • Icebound Fortitude -- 30% DR on 3-min CD. Save for known damage spikes.
  • Death Strike -- self-heal based on recent damage. Use reactively after big hits.
  • Lichborne -- if talented, can break/immune fear effects.
  • Anti-Magic Zone -- raid CD if available, 20% magic DR.

Actionable Improvements

Ordered by impact. Survival is the entire focus for prog -- rotation optimization can wait until the fight is survivable.

1. Bursting Emptiness: 10 Deaths (32% of all deaths)

The Problem: Bursting Emptiness (Void Droplet detonation) killed Unholyftw 10 times across P1 and P2. This is the single biggest death source. Deaths range from 0:17 to 4:27, meaning this mechanic is dangerous throughout the fight.

The Fix: Void Droplets must be killed or avoided before they reach their target and detonate. Use Anti-Magic Shell proactively when Droplets are spawning. Stay aware of Droplet spawn locations and move away if they fixate. In P1 with multiple adds, Epidemic cleave should be hitting Droplets naturally -- if they are still detonating, positioning may be too far from the group.

Impact: Eliminating Bursting Emptiness deaths would add ~3 more minutes of alive time per session on average and dramatically increase P2/P3 reach rate.

2. Void Remnants: 7 Deaths (23% of all deaths)

The Problem: Void Remnants killed Unholyftw 7 times, all in P1, clustered between 0:50-2:11. This is likely the Sentinel death explosion or soak mechanic.

The Fix: When Sentinels die (Demiar ~0:43, Morium ~1:21, Vorelus ~2:05), they leave Void Remnants that deal heavy damage. Pre-position away from dying Sentinels. Use AMS for the magic portion. Icebound Fortitude if AMS is down. Deaths at 0:50-0:56 cluster around Demiar's death, and deaths at 2:09-2:11 cluster around Vorelus's death.

Impact: These 7 deaths are entirely in P1 and prevent seeing the rest of the fight. The player who dies at 0:55 to Void Remnants misses 5+ minutes of practice.

3. Voidstalker Sting: 3 Deaths in Late P2/P3

The Problem: Three deaths to Voidstalker Sting at 5:01, 5:16, and 4:27. This is an Alleria ability in P2 that applies a paired debuff requiring specific positioning.

The Fix: Voidstalker Sting targets two players who must manage their positioning relative to each other. When targeted, move to the designated position immediately. Use a personal defensive (AMS or IBF) to survive the initial application if health is low. This mechanic becomes more dangerous in late P2 / P3 when healing is strained.

Impact: These deaths cost the raid at 4:27-5:16 -- deep into the fight where every player matters. Fixing this converts good pulls into great pulls.

4. Intermission Survival (3 Deaths)

The Problem: Three deaths during the Intermission phase (2:14-2:53). The Intermission involves Stellar Emission (sustained AoE) plus Silverstrike Barrage every 6s. No targets to hit, pure survival.

The Fix: Pre-use AMS before Stellar Emission channel begins at 2:22. Save Icebound Fortitude for Intermission if not needed for P1. Death Strike any incoming damage for self-healing. Use healthstone or health potion during Stellar Emission if health drops below 50%. Coordinate with healers for this window -- it is a planned damage event.

Impact: Intermission survival is binary: live through it or don't see P2. Three deaths here cost three entire P2 practice attempts.

Learning rate is excellent. Survival time nearly tripled across the session (1:34 avg to 4:16 avg). P3 was reached 5 times in the last 9 pulls after not being reached at all in the first 18. Best boss % of 31.3% came on pull 27 near the end of the session.

P1 DPS is strong. When alive for the full P1 phase, average DPS is 313K+ with add cleave, which is excellent add damage. The Unholy DK toolkit (Epidemic cleave, Dark Transformation, Army of the Dead) is being used effectively for P1 add management.

P2 DPS is solid when alive. Best P2 DPS of 110K (F28) shows the rotation is functional in single-target phases. The 83K average is dragged down by pulls where the player died partway through P2.

Gear is not a limiter. At ilvl 290, Unholyftw is at or above most top-ranked Unholy DK kills (273-291 range). When this fight is survivable, the DPS will follow.
