How Augmentation Evoker Works

Augmentation Evoker is a support-damage hybrid. Your job is to keep Ebon Might active on your allies while dealing damage yourself through Eruption and empowered abilities. You’re not just a buffer — you deal real personal damage — but your biggest value comes from the primary stat boost Ebon Might gives to the entire raid.

The gameplay revolves around 30-second cycles. You cast Ebon Might to start the buff, then spend the next 30 seconds extending it by casting Eruption (your main filler), Fire Breath, and Upheaval. Every Eruption and every empowered cast adds time to Ebon Might through the Sands of Time talent. If you stop casting, Ebon Might falls off and your raid loses the stat buff.

Prescience is your other key buff — a 2-charge ability that gives Critical Strike to allies. Use it on cooldown, never overcap charges. It’s rotational, not something you save.

The core loop is: Ebon Might → Eruption spam → Fire Breath/Upheaval on cooldown → Breath of Eons for burst windows. Every global cooldown you spend on anything other than this loop is DPS lost for both you AND the raid.

How It’s Affecting Your Damage

Off-healing is the biggest DPS drain. Zinnks spends roughly 4 globals per minute on Verdant Embrace (1.6/m), Emerald Blossom (1.2/m), and Azure Strike (1.3/m). The top Aug Evokers don’t cast these abilities. Every one of those globals could be an Eruption or Living Flame instead, which would both deal more damage AND keep Ebon Might extended for the raid.
Eruption deficit is costing the entire raid. At 16.6/m vs 22.8/m for the #1, Zinnks is missing 6.2 Eruptions per minute. Each Eruption extends Ebon Might — fewer Eruptions means Ebon Might drops off more often (shown by the 155% Ebon Might recast rate), and when Ebon Might drops, the whole raid loses the stat buff.
Living Flame at 58% of #1. Living Flame is the secondary filler that should be cast between Eruptions when essence is recharging. Zinnks is casting 7.2/m vs 12.5/m — almost half the rate. This compounds the Eruption issue: lower Living Flame also means lower overall damage output during downtime between Eruption windows.

What You Need to Change

1
Stop Off-Healing
Verdant Embrace, Emerald Blossom, and Azure Strike are costing you roughly 4 globals per minute that should be Eruptions and Living Flames. You have 6 healers in the raid — they can handle healing. Your job is to keep Ebon Might up and deal damage. Every off-heal global is DPS lost for the entire raid, not just you.
2
More Eruptions
Target 20+ Eruptions per minute. You’re at 16.6, the #1 is at 22.8. Eruption is both your main damage and your Ebon Might extension — it’s the single most important button you press. Cast it any time you have essence and nothing else is critically needed.
3
Fill with Living Flame
When Eruption is recharging and Fire Breath/Upheaval are on cooldown, cast Living Flame. You’re at 7.2/m vs 12.5/m for the #1. This is your best filler — it deals solid damage and it’s always available. Never stand still.
