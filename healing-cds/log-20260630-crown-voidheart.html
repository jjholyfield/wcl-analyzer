Cast Profile Analysis

Power Word: Shield CPM Gap (0.76 vs 6.30)

This is the largest single rotation gap. PW:Shield applies Atonement and an absorb at no Atonement cost. Sadboyhorse casts it 6.3x per minute -- almost on cooldown. Voidheart casts it less than once per minute. This means significantly fewer Atonement targets during ramps and filler windows. PW:Shield should be pressed on cooldown between ramps to maintain Atonement blankets.

Shadow Word: Death Usage (1.33 vs 3.35 CPM)

SW:Death is Disc's highest-damage instant-cast spell and therefore the highest Atonement healing per GCD during ramps. Sadboyhorse uses it 2.5x more often. Each missed SW:Death during an Evangelism window is lost burst healing. Use SW:Death on cooldown during ramps and whenever execute range is available.

Penance Under-casting (7.63 vs 10.05 CPM)

Penance is the core rotational ability -- it should be pressed on cooldown. Voidheart is 24% behind the reference. Some of this gap may be explained by movement and prog mechanics, but 2.4 CPM fewer Penance casts per minute represents significant lost throughput. Prioritize Penance over Flash Heal whenever possible.

Shadow Word: Pain Over-Refreshing (2.69 vs 1.61 CPM)

SW:Pain at 2.69 CPM is 67% higher than the reference. The DoT has a 16-second duration and pandemic window -- refreshing it more than ~2x/min means clipping it early and wasting GCDs that should go to Smite or PW:Shield. Only refresh when it is about to fall off.

Actionable Items

1. Power Word: Shield on Cooldown (0.76 vs 6.30 CPM)

PW:Shield is being cast less than once per minute when it should be a near-cooldown ability. Each cast applies Atonement and provides an absorb shield. The reference fires it 8x more often. This single change would add thousands of Atonement healing per minute and bring PW:Shield absorb healing from 643K to the ~5-6M range. Press it on cooldown between ramps, ideally on targets without Atonement.

2. Shadow Word: Death Priority (1.33 vs 3.35 CPM)

SW:Death is the highest-damage instant available during Evangelism ramps. The reference uses it 2.5x more often. During ramp windows, the priority should be: Evangelism, PW:Radiance (x2 instant), then SW:Death before Mind Blast and Penance. Outside of ramps, use it on cooldown whenever a valid target is available. This directly increases Atonement burst healing during the moments it matters most.

3. Reduce Shadow Word: Pain Refreshes (2.69 vs 1.61 CPM)

SW:Pain is being refreshed 67% more often than the reference. With a 16s duration and pandemic window, it only needs ~2 refreshes per minute. Each extra SW:Pain is a wasted GCD that should go to Smite (Atonement filler), PW:Shield, or a damage spell. Use a DoT timer weakaura to track when SW:Pain actually needs refreshing.

4. Power Word: Barrier Deployment

PW:Barrier was used in only 2 of 15 qualifying pulls. This 3-minute raid CD provides 25% damage reduction. On Crown of the Cosmos, heavy raid damage events in P2 (Void Barrage, Umbral Tether) and P3 (Devouring Cosmos, Aspect of the End) are predictable. Coordinate with the healing team to assign Barrier to a specific mechanic -- Devouring Cosmos at ~6:45 in P3 would be a strong candidate given the current lack of CD coverage at that point.

5. Survivability is Excellent

Zero actionable deaths across 20 pulls is the strongest possible result. Average death order of #11.4 means Voidheart outlasts more than half the raid on every wipe. Pain Suppression usage (15 casts, mostly P2/P3) and Desperate Prayer (7 casts) show good defensive awareness. Continue this -- healers dying is the single most impactful failure mode in mythic prog.
