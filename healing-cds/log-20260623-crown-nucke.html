Key Phase Insights

  • P1 Echoing Darkness problem: 6 of 20 deaths occur in P1, all to Echoing Darkness (Vorelus add). This is a positioning or add-priority issue, not a defensive CD issue — Demon Spikes averaging 9.5/pull in P1 is solid.
  • P2 is the deadliest phase: 7 deaths (35% of all deaths) across 14 pulls that reached P2. Cosmic Barrier (3), Silverstrike Ricochet (2), Stellar Emission (1), and Void Remnants (1) are the causes — multiple overlapping mechanics.
  • P3 death rate is extreme: All 4 pulls that reached P3 ended with Nucke dying (6 deaths total including battle rez). Causes vary widely, indicating general survivability collapse.
  • Self-HPS drops through phases: 42.0K in P1 down to 27.7K in P3 (34% drop). Healing throughput needs to increase in later phases, not decrease.
  • Metamorphosis front-loaded: 2.7 Meta casts/pull in P1 vs 1.8 in P2 and 1.3 in P3. Banking Meta for harder phases would improve survivability where it matters most.

Actionable Items

1. Resolve P1 Echoing Darkness deaths (6/20 pulls). Echoing Darkness from Vorelus is the #1 single-cause killer. All 6 deaths occur before 2:00 in P1. This is Vorelus's persistent AoE — ensure proper positioning relative to Vorelus, use Demon Spikes and Fiery Brand during high overlap windows, and coordinate with healers for externals. Dying in P1 wastes the entire pull.
2. Increase Soul Cleave usage by ~45% (9.4 CPM → 14+ CPM target). Soul Cleave is the primary resource spender for Vengeance. At nearly half the reference rate (17.0 CPM), Nucke is losing significant self-healing through Soul Fragment consumption, damage output, and Soul Barrier value. This single change would improve both DPS and survivability simultaneously.
3. Fix Felblade usage (0.6 CPM vs 6.0 ref, -90%). Felblade is a core rotational ability that generates Fury, deals damage, and provides gap-closing mobility. At 0.6 CPM it is nearly unused. This represents a massive Fury generation loss that cascades into fewer Soul Cleaves and Spirit Bombs. This should be a high-priority button.
4. Close the 29% total CPM gap (101.2 → 130+ target). Every missing GCD compounds: more Fracture casts (-23%) generate more Soul Fragments, which fuel more Soul Cleave/Spirit Bomb, which heals more and deals more damage. Immolation Aura at -47% should be kept on cooldown for Fury generation and passive AoE damage.
5. Redistribute Metamorphosis usage toward P2/P3. Meta averages 2.7 casts in P1 (30% death rate) but only 1.8 in P2 (50% death rate) and 1.3 in P3 (100% death rate). The 40% max HP increase and 200% armor is most valuable in the harder phases. Consider using only 1 Meta in P1 and banking the second for the P2 transition or early P3.
6. Plan defensives for P2 Cosmic Barrier and Silverstrike Ricochet. 3 deaths to Cosmic Barrier and 2 to Silverstrike Ricochet in P2 suggest these mechanics hit without active defensives. Pre-plan Fiery Brand or ensure Demon Spikes is active for known Cosmic Barrier timings. Silverstrike Ricochet requires positional awareness to avoid bounce-point damage.
