Actionable Improvements

Priority 1 — Survival

Survive Silverstrike Barrage in Intermission

Both of Smokinthisza's only actionable deaths (F17 and F24) were to Silverstrike Barrage during the Intermission (2:14-2:53). This is the single biggest improvement opportunity. The fix: pre-use Survival of the Fittest as the Intermission begins (around 2:14), position away from barrage landing zones, and use Disengage or Aspect of the Cheetah to dodge between volleys. On F17, this death cascaded into 3 total deaths and reduced the pull's DPS from an expected ~48K to 13.8K. Surviving Intermission on every pull would eliminate the only recurring individual failure.

Priority 2 — Mechanics

Reduce Gravity Collapse Hits in P3

29 hits from Gravity Collapse across the session, and it was the killing blow on 2 late-pull cascade deaths (F26 and F28). While these were cascade deaths (not among first 2), reducing Gravity Collapse exposure will extend survival in P3. Pre-position before the cast, use Disengage to quickly move out of the impact zone. Only 3 Disengage casts on F14 across 7:12 is low for a 20s cooldown ability — aim to use it reactively on every Gravity Collapse.

Priority 3 — Mechanics

Mitigate Falling Damage

21 instances of falling damage (3.9M total) is significant. Hunters have a unique advantage: Disengage can be used mid-air to negate fall damage from knockbacks. When hit by Gravity Collapse or Void Expulsion knockback, immediately Disengage in the opposite direction. This is free survivability that many hunters miss.

Priority 4 — Throughput

Optimize P3 Trueshot Windows

P3 DPS varies dramatically: 50.8K on F14 vs 91.2K on F26. The 91.2K pull likely had a Trueshot window aligned with the start of P3. As the fight reaches a kill attempt (7:00+), plan the second or third Trueshot to line up with the P3 transition at ~5:09. With a 90-120s Trueshot CD, using it at ~3:30 in P2 ensures it is available again for the P3 push. This could close the gap between average P3 (50-52K) and peak P3 (76-91K).

Priority 5 — Defensive

Use Exhilaration More Proactively

Only 1 Exhilaration cast in a 7:12 pull is low. On a ~2 min cooldown, 3 casts are possible. Use Exhilaration proactively after heavy damage windows (post-Intermission, during P3 Cosmic Radiation stacking), not only as an emergency button. Combined with 5 Survival of the Fittest uses (already good), more Exhilaration would significantly increase P3 durability.
