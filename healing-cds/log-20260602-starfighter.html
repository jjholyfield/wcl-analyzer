How Arms Warrior Works

Arms Warrior is a burst-window spec built around Colossus Smash. Every ~20 seconds, you press Colossus Smash to debuff the target, then dump your hardest-hitting abilities into that window. Between windows, you manage Rage — building it through auto-attacks and spending it on priority abilities.

Your bread and butter is Mortal Strike — keep it on cooldown at all costs. Overpower is a proc-based filler you press when it lights up. When you have excess Rage, you spend it on Slam or Heroic Strike. Execute replaces Slam when the target is below 35% HP.

Cleave is your multi-target ability — it replaces Mortal Strike when there are 3+ targets and auto-casts Slam or Heroic Strike. On a single-target fight, Mortal Strike takes priority over Cleave.

Major CDs: Avatar (minor throughput, keep on CD), Ravager (sync with Colossus Smash), Demolish/Bladestorm (depending on hero talent). The Colossus talent build uses Demolish as a channeled burst ability.

Rage generation comes from auto-attacks, so Arms has natural pauses waiting for swings. But with proper filler usage (Slam, Heroic Strike), you should rarely have empty GCDs.

How This Is Affecting Your Damage

Mortal Strike at 74% of top — 9.7/min vs 13.2/min. This is the highest-priority damage ability and it’s being cast 3.5 fewer times per minute than top players. Mortal Strike is where the majority of Arms Warrior damage comes from. Every missed Mortal Strike is a significant DPS loss.
Cleave at 160% of top — 13.9/min vs 8.7/min. Casting significantly more Cleave than top players suggests using it as a primary filler instead of Mortal Strike on single-target. On LBV (primarily single-target with occasional add phases), Mortal Strike should take priority over Cleave. The extra Cleave casts are likely displacing Mortal Strike GCDs.
Heroic Strike at 34% — 1.5/min vs 4.4/min. Top players use Heroic Strike as a major Rage spender between Mortal Strikes. Starfighter barely uses it. This is Rage that’s either being dumped into Cleave or going unspent entirely.
Uptime is excellent. 2.8% dead time is actually better than the #1 ranked player. The problem isn’t casting frequency — it’s ability priority. The GCDs are being filled, but with the wrong abilities.

What You Need to Change

1
Prioritize Mortal Strike over Cleave on single-target
9.7/min needs to be 13+. Mortal Strike is higher damage per cast than Cleave on single-target. On LBV, Cleave should only take priority during add phases with 3+ targets. If Mortal Strike is available and there aren’t 3+ targets stacked, press Mortal Strike.
2
Use Heroic Strike as a Rage spender
1.5/min vs 4.4/min from top players. When you have excess Rage between Mortal Strikes, Heroic Strike deals more damage than another Cleave on single-target. Heroic Strike should be your go-to Rage dump on 1-2 targets, not Cleave.
