Actionable Items

1. Increase DPS Filler During P1 Downtime

2 total DPS filler casts (1 Wrath, 1 Moonfire) across 7:09 is the biggest efficiency gap. In P1 (0:00-2:14) where overheal is 55.6%, there are clear windows between Void Expulsion and Echoing Darkness casts where the raid is at or near full health. Apply and maintain Moonfire/Sunfire on primary targets, and cast Wrath during any 2+ second healing downtime. Even modest DPS filler during P1 would add 3-5K overall DPS without sacrificing healing output, since that healing was overhealing anyway. Across all 19 pulls, only 53 total Wrath casts — averaging less than 3 per pull.

2. Cast Swiftmend on Cooldown (2.66 vs 4.57 CPM, -42%)

Swiftmend is 42% lower CPM than the reference. Each Swiftmend triggers Soul of the Forest (+40% to next cast), Verdancy (HoT on nearby targets), and mastery stacking. Missing Swiftmend casts cascades into lower throughput from all associated talent synergies. The fix is straightforward: press Swiftmend on cooldown (~every 12-15 seconds), consuming a Rejuvenation or Wild Growth HoT. More Swiftmend casts will naturally increase Regrowth usage as well, since empowered Regrowths become the optimal follow-up.

3. Use 2nd Tranquility in P2 Consistently

Tranquility was only used a 2nd time in 5 of 11 pulls that reached P2. After the P1 cast at 0:10, Tranquility comes off cooldown at ~3:10 — right as P2 Rift damage ramps up with Void Barrage and Voidstalker Sting. In the best pull (F14), the 2nd Tranquility landed at 3:11 and P2 HPS was 175K (61% higher than P1). In the 6 pulls without a 2nd Tranquility, this major CD sat unused during the highest-damage phase. Target 3:10-3:30 in every P2+ pull.

4. Increase Regrowth Usage (13.29 vs 17.81 CPM, -25%)

Regrowth is the Abundance cycle payoff — after blanketing Rejuvenation, Regrowth becomes nearly free and crits frequently. The reference casts 25% more Regrowth per minute, which directly converts Rejuvenation blanket investment into effective healing. This improvement should come naturally from casting more Swiftmend (item #2 above) — each Soul of the Forest proc makes the next Regrowth significantly stronger, creating a natural incentive to cast it more.

5. Strengths to Maintain

Tranquility P1 discipline — 0:10-0:14 in every pull, near-zero variance. Convoke on CD — used in 19/19 pulls at consistent ~60s intervals with Nature's Swiftness pairing. Survivability — always among the last alive, zero early deaths across 19 pulls. Rejuvenation/Wild Growth/Germination CPM all match reference levels. Lifebloom maintenance — higher CPM than reference, showing consistent uptime awareness. Barkskin usage — 14/19 pulls with proactive personal defensive. Ironbark — 16/19 pulls, used on tanks and vulnerable targets.
