Actionable Items

1
Double Enveloping Mist Volume
Senssay cast Enveloping Mist 39 times vs the reference's 83 times (4.8 vs 10.1 CPM). For a Conduit of the Celestials build, Enveloping Mist is a core rotational spell, not just a spot heal — it triggers Strength of the Black Ox and procs Invigorating Mists (passive AoE healing). The reference's Invigorating Mists healing was 22.6M vs Senssay's 15.8M (+43%). Weave Enveloping Mist between every Rushing Wind Kick and Vivify cycle. This is the single biggest throughput gain available.
2
Use Mana Tea on Cooldown
16 Mana Tea casts vs the reference's 45 (-64%). Mana Tea stacks are generated passively from Renewing Mist and other abilities. Every time you hit 20 stacks without consuming, you waste mana return. Build a habit: consume Mana Tea every time it reaches 20 stacks. This directly enables the higher Enveloping Mist volume in item #1 without going OOM. The reference consumed Mana Tea roughly every 11 seconds on average.
3
Use Celestial Conduit Every 90 Seconds
Senssay used Celestial Conduit 3 times (once per Bloom, 14-17s late). The reference used it 6 times — every ~90 seconds as a rotational throughput CD. Reference Conduit healing: 6.0M vs Senssay's 2.5M (+140%). Celestial Conduit has a 90-second cooldown — in an 8-minute fight, that's 5-6 possible uses. Use it on cooldown, don't save it exclusively for Blooms. If it naturally lines up with a Bloom, great. If not, it still provides strong throughput.
4
Time Yu'lon and Conduit AT Bloom, Not After
All 3 Celestial Conduit uses were 14-17 seconds after Fungal Bloom hit. Yu'lon was deployed 16 seconds after Bloom #1 at 2:16 instead of at 2:00. The reference deployed Yu'lon at 1:58 (2s before Bloom) and Celestial Conduit at 2:00 (AT Bloom). Bloom is predictable — it fires at Rotmire's 100 energy. Watch the energy bar and pre-position CDs to land at or slightly before the damage event, not 15 seconds into the recovery.
5
Fit a Third Revival
Revival was used at 0:53 and 5:53 (2 uses). The reference used it 3 times at 0:32, 3:40, and 6:47. With a 3-minute cooldown and an 8:09 fight, 3 Revivals are achievable (e.g., 0:53, 3:53, 6:53). The second Revival at 5:53 was held too long after the first — a 5-minute gap instead of the 3-minute cooldown. That's 2 minutes of wasted availability. Each Revival is 1.3M effective healing and a raid-wide dispel.
