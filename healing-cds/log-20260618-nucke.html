Actionable Items

Zero Deaths — Survival Execution is Excellent

Survived all 27 pulls with zero deaths. Last Resort triggered once (F23) showing the cheat death talent did its job when needed. Demon Spikes uptime stayed above 80% in every phase. For prog night 2, this is the foundation everything else builds on — Nucke is doing the #1 tank job flawlessly.

Fel Devastation — 75% Below Reference (0.4 vs 1.2 CPM)

Fel Devastation was only cast 35 times across 27 pulls (1.3/pull average) and was completely absent from 9 pulls. The reference uses it at 1.2 CPM. This ability deals frontal AoE damage AND heals Nucke — it should be used on cooldown during active tanking windows, not saved for emergencies. On a ~40s cooldown, every long pull should see 5-8 casts.

Soul Cleave CPM Deficit — 24% Below Reference (10.2 vs 13.5 CPM)

Soul Cleave is the primary Soul Fragment spender and self-heal. At 10.2 CPM vs reference 13.5 CPM, Nucke is spending fragments ~3.3 fewer times per minute. This directly impacts both DPS and self-healing throughput. With Nucke's high Haste (1206), Fracture should be generating enough fragments to support higher Soul Cleave frequency.

Immolation Aura CPM Deficit — 29% Below Reference (3.0 vs 4.2 CPM)

Immolation Aura is a key DPS and Soul Fragment generation ability. At 3.0 CPM vs 4.2 CPM, Nucke is leaving damage and fragment generation on the table. This is a fire-and-forget ability that should be pressed essentially on cooldown.

Taunt Imbalance — Glazedwhole Taunts 2.5x More

Glazedwhole used 266 taunts to Nucke's 105 across all pulls. In 5 pulls, Nucke used zero taunts. This improved on later pulls (F23: 8 Nucke / 10 Glazed, F27: 13 / 11), suggesting the swap pattern is being learned. Equalizing the tanking burden will improve both tanks' DPS and distribute healer attention more evenly.

DPS Gap is Expected on Prog — Will Close Naturally

The ~20K avg DPS vs rank-1's 53.5K kill DPS is a large gap, but context matters: rank-1 had a full kill (8:26 with Bloodlust/Hero, more uptime), while Nucke is on prog night 2 with wipes. DPS already trended upward through the session (F28 hit 26.7K). As the fight becomes more comfortable and the rotation becomes automatic, DPS will rise naturally. The priority order remains: survive > mechanics > swap consistency > DPS.
