Actionable Improvements

1. Survive Simulacrum Backlash in P2 (4 deaths)
Problem: Simulacrum Backlash killed Orichamaru 4 times, all in Phase 2 (F15 @4:50, F21 @4:38, F22 @4:11, F24 @4:42). This is the #1 co-killer alongside Bursting Emptiness.

Fix: When Rift Simulacrum is low HP or finishing its cast, use Dark Pact (1 min CD) or Unending Resolve (3 min CD) proactively. Position away from the Simulacrum's death location. Use Demonic Circle pre-placed at a safe fallback position. Demo Warlock is a ranged spec -- there is no reason to be in melee range of the Simulacrum.

Impact: Eliminating these 4 deaths could mean 4 more P3 entries. Currently only 4/27 pulls reach P3.
2. Pre-plan Defensive Usage for Each Phase
Problem: Defensive usage is sporadic (Dark Pact: 1-3 uses, Unending Resolve: 0-1 uses per fight). On a 5-6 minute pull, Dark Pact (1 min CD) should be used 4-5 times. Unending Resolve should be used at least once, ideally timed for the intermission or a known damage spike in P2.

Fix: Build a defensive plan per phase:
  • P1 (0:00-2:14): Dark Pact for Void Expulsion or Bursting Emptiness
  • Intermission (2:14-2:53): Unending Resolve for Stellar Emission (the big channel)
  • P2 entry (2:53+): Dark Pact back up -- use for Simulacrum Backlash or Void Barrage
  • P2 mid (~4:00): Dark Pact again for next Simulacrum wave
  • P3 entry (~5:09): Unending Resolve back up -- use for Cosmic Radiation opener
3. Reduce Early Deaths (6 pulls lost before 60s)
Problem: 6 of 27 pulls (22%) ended with Orichamaru dying before the 1-minute mark. Death causes include Melee (F12 -- too close to boss), Silverstrike Arrow (F13 -- positioning), Bursting Emptiness (F6), Void Remnants (F2, F4), Echoing Darkness (F1), and Grasp of Emptiness (F17).

Fix: Ensure safe starting position -- max range from adds and Void Droplets. Use Demonic Circle at pull position as a safety net. On early pulls, prioritize survival over DPS to reach later phases and learn the fight.

Impact: 6 pulls of learning time lost. Even reducing early deaths by half would mean 3 more pulls of P1/P2 practice.
4. Maintain Rotational Consistency (Already Decent)
Context: Rotational CPM is fairly consistent across long pulls (29.1-32.8 CPM). Hand of Gul'dan and Demonbolt usage are stable. The main DPS variability comes from survival time, not rotation execution.

Opportunity: F27 had the lowest CPM (29.1) despite being one of the longer pulls -- likely due to movement during P3 mechanics. Practice using Demonbolt (instant cast with Demonic Core procs) during movement-heavy phases. Glide usage was notably high (4-10 per fight), which is wasted GCDs if used unnecessarily.
5. 0 Versatility -- Consider Gear Adjustment
Problem: Orichamaru has literally 0 Versatility rating. Versatility provides both damage increase AND damage reduction. On a fight where survival is the primary challenge, having some Versatility would help with both DPS and survivability.

Note: This is a lower priority than mechanical improvements, but if there are gear options with Versatility that don't significantly reduce ilvl, they would provide a small but free survival boost.
