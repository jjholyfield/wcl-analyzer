Bottom Line

Silencio died in every single pull across 20 attempts -- the only healer with a 0% survival rate. The cast profile reveals zero DPS filler casts (no Wrath, Sunfire, or Moonfire across the entire session) and zero Efflorescence uptime, while Phase 1 overheal sits at 65% -- meaning significant mana and globals are being wasted before the fight gets hard. Staying alive through P1 is the single highest-impact improvement available.

Actionable Items

1. Survive Phase 1

9 of 23 deaths occurred in Phase 1 -- the lowest-damage phase of the fight. Silverstrike Arrow (2 kills) is a dodgeable projectile. Void Expulsion (1 kill) requires the raid to spread. The remaining 6 deaths have unattributed killing blows, suggesting multi-ability combos or standing in avoidable ground effects. Action: Use Barkskin proactively before Void Expulsion casts (every ~27s). Position away from Silverstrike Arrow trajectories. Consider using Bear Form defensively when caught with low health. Barkskin was only used in 14/20 pulls despite dying every pull -- it should be used on cooldown during high-risk windows.

2. Stop Channeling Tranquility at 0:10

Tranquility is channeled at exactly 0:10 in every single pull, landing in Phase 1 where it does 89% overheal. This is a 3-minute cooldown generating almost zero effective healing. Action: Save Tranquility for the Intermission Stellar Emission channel (2:22-2:52), which is 30 seconds of sustained raid-wide AoE where the raid is most vulnerable. Second Tranquility should be available around 5:22 for P3 entry. This single change would dramatically improve mana efficiency and effective throughput when it matters.

3. Increase Swiftmend and Wild Growth Usage

Swiftmend CPM is 3.19 vs reference 4.72 (-32%), and Wild Growth is 4.25 vs 5.36 (-21%). Both are core rotational abilities in the Wildstalker build: Swiftmend procs Keeper of the Grove Guardians and feeds mastery, while Wild Growth is the primary AoE heal. Action: Prioritize Wild Growth on cooldown during any damage event, and use Swiftmend more aggressively rather than saving it. These two abilities together drive a significant portion of Resto Druid throughput through proc chains.

4. Maintain Lifebloom Uptime

Only 7 Lifebloom casts in 6:35 (1.06 CPM vs reference 1.92). Lifebloom should have near-100% uptime on a tank. With only 7 casts, it is falling off and being re-applied infrequently, which explains the 74% overheal (bloom heal landing on a full-health target when it falls off naturally). Action: Set up a WeakAura or tracking addon to monitor Lifebloom and refresh it before it expires. Continuous Lifebloom provides steady tank healing and generates clearcasting procs for free Regrowths.

5. Team CD Coordination for Intermission

The Intermission (Stellar Emission, 2:22-2:52) received zero major healer CDs on the longest pull. All four healers spent their first CDs in P1. Action (team-level): Coordinate with the healing team to save at least one major CD (Tranquility, Ascendance, or Aura Mastery) for the Intermission. Silencio holding Tranquility solves this directly. Brewtote's Ascendance at 0:06 could also be delayed. This requires a team conversation, not just individual adjustment.
