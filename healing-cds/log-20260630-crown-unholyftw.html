Actionable Improvements

1. Fix the P1 Bursting Emptiness Death (F17)

The only actionable death of the night: died at 0:40 to Bursting Emptiness as the first player down. This is a Void Droplet explosion in the P1 Sentinel phase. Anti-Magic Shell should be available at 40 seconds -- use it proactively when Void Droplets are about to burst. Icebound Fortitude is another option if AMS is on cooldown. One early death in 13 pulls is already excellent, but eliminating it entirely ensures the raid always has maximum DPS for the critical P1 transition.

2. Maximize P2 Sustained DPS

P2 (Rift phase) shows the widest DPS variance: 26.5K to 64.4K. The best P2 pulls (F24 at 64.4K, F6 at 56.5K, F12 at 53.0K) are nearly double the worst (F5 at 26.5K). P2 is primarily single-target against Alleria with periodic Rift Simulacrum and Undying Voidspawn adds. Consistent Dark Transformation and Army of the Dead alignment in P2 is key. On pulls where these CDs drift or are held for the wrong moment, DPS tanks. The P2 average of 44K has significant room to climb toward the 55-60K range with better CD timing.

3. Improve P3 Devouring Cosmos Uptime

P3 average of 21.5K is depressed by cascade wipes, but even on the deepest pulls (F25 at 31.3K over 2:47, F17 at 31.9K over 2:40), P3 DPS is well below P2. This is expected as P3 layers every mechanic simultaneously, but there is room to improve. The Unholy toolkit has strong spread damage (Epidemic, Dread Plague ticking on multiple targets). Prioritize uptime on Virulent Plague across all active targets in P3, and use Death Coil for Sudden Doom procs between movement phases. As the raid sees more clean P3 pulls, this number will naturally climb.

4. Session Trend is Positive

First half average (57.4K) vs second half average (58.4K) shows a +1.9% improvement through the night. The best pull (F25, 69.8K) came on the last pull of the night, and it was also the deepest. This suggests Unholyftw is learning the fight and improving as the night progresses. Keep this trajectory going into Night 6.

What's Working Well

Survivability is Excellent

Only 1 actionable death in 13 pulls. Every other death was a cascade event where 6+ players were already dead. Unholyftw is consistently one of the last DPS standing when the raid falls apart. This is the foundation for progressing deeper into the fight.

Strong P1 Burst

P1 average of 114K DPS with AoE is solid, and the best P1 (142.5K on F24) shows the ceiling is high. Opener CD alignment with Army of the Dead, Dark Transformation, and AoE cleave on Sentinels is being executed well.

Best Pull = Deepest Pull

Fight 25 (7:56) was both the longest pull of the night AND Unholyftw's highest DPS pull (69.8K). This means performance doesn't degrade under pressure -- it improves. Reaching P3 with the whole raid alive and executing well produces the best individual performance.
