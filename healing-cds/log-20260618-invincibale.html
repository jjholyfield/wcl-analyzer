Actionable Improvements

PRIORITY 1 — SURVIVAL
Eliminate P1 deaths from Void Remnants and Bursting Emptiness
14 of 27 first deaths happen in P1. The two biggest killers are Void Remnants (6 deaths, scattered 0:43–2:10) and Bursting Emptiness (4 deaths, always at exactly 1:05).

Void Remnants: These are void zones on the ground during Sentinel waves. Dying to them 6 times across the night suggests a positioning habit — likely standing too close to void pools or not moving fast enough when they expand. Use Gust of Wind and Spiritwalker's Grace proactively for repositioning.

Bursting Emptiness: This fires at a fixed timer (~1:05). Plan Astral Shift for this every single pull. The fact that it kills at the exact same timestamp means this is a predictable, plannable event. If Astral Shift is not enough, coordinate with healers for an external.
PRIORITY 2 — SURVIVAL
Handle Voidstalker Sting in P2/P3
Voidstalker Sting caused 5 P2 deaths and 1 P3 death (plus 3 total as first death cause). This is the P2 wall. Voidstalker Sting is a targeted DoT — when targeted, the player needs to use a defensive (Astral Shift) or a health-pot/Healthstone, and ensure they are topped by healers before the next tick sequence. On pulls where the player survived past Voidstalker Sting windows (F20, F21), DPS was 51K+. Surviving this mechanic is the difference between a strong pull and an early death.
PRIORITY 3 — MECHANIC
Plan defensive cooldowns on a per-phase timer
Astral Shift (90s CD) can be used at least 3–4 times in a 6-minute fight. Suggested plan:
Use 1: Bursting Emptiness at ~1:05 (P1)
Use 2: Silverstrike Barrage during Intermission (~2:20–2:50) or first Void Expulsion in P2
Use 3: Voidstalker Sting at ~4:20–4:45 (mid P2)
Use 4: Any P3 damage overlap
Earth Elemental can also be used as an emergency defensive. The player used it 0 times in the best pull (F27).
PRIORITY 4 — ROTATION
Use Chain Lightning during add/cleave windows
The reference player casts Chain Lightning at 1.3 CPM. Invincibale cast 0 Chain Lightning in the best pull. Crown of the Cosmos has frequent add spawns (Void Droplets in P1, Undying Voidspawn + Rift Simulacrum in P2). When 2+ targets are stacked, Chain Lightning should replace Lightning Bolt as the filler — it generates more Maelstrom and procs Tempest faster via Stormbringer. This is a free DPS gain that does not require any additional mechanical skill.

Summary

What is going well

Rotational execution is solid. On the best pull (F27), total CPM is 97% of the reference player. Lava Burst, Stormkeeper, Ascendance, and Elemental Blast usage all match reference rates closely. Cooldown alignment with PI and Time Warp is correct. DPS improved dramatically through the session (+53%). The player hit 0.31% boss health on the best pull — a kill is mechanically within reach.

What needs work

Survival is the entire gap. The player died in 27/27 pulls. The DPS numbers are artificially low because every pull ends early (for this player). When alive, P2 DPS averages 109K which is competitive. The two highest-leverage fixes are: (1) plan Astral Shift for Bursting Emptiness at 1:05 every pull, and (2) position away from Void Remnants in P1. Fixing those two mechanics alone would eliminate ~10 of 27 first deaths and dramatically increase time alive — which directly translates to higher DPS and better progression.
