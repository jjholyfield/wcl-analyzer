Actionable Improvements

1 P3 Survival and Uptime (Primary Focus)
P3 DPS averages 58.2K across 10 pulls, with a wide range from 14.5K (barely entered P3 before dying) to 79.2K (best pull F25). The P3 improvement trend is positive (+55% from early to late session), so the learning curve is working. Focus areas: positioning during Cosmic Radiation overlaps to minimize movement, pre-planning defensive cooldown usage so Survival of the Fittest and Aspect of the Turtle are available for the highest-damage windows. Blowe's F25 defensive usage (starting Turtle at 5:50, stacking SotF through 6:39-7:00, double Exhil) is a good template.
2 Reduce Falling Damage (5.6M across 14 pulls)
26 falling damage instances across 14 of 20 pulls at 213.8K average per hit. This is likely from Gravity Collapse knockbacks or platform edge positioning. As a BM Hunter, Disengage can cancel knockback momentum -- Blowe used Disengage in F25 (2 casts) but the falling damage persisted. Working on positioning further from edges during Gravity Collapse and reactive Disengage timing will reduce unnecessary damage taken and ease healer burden.
3 Intermission Survival (3 Deaths)
Blowe died in the Intermission phase 3 times (F11 at 2:30, F22 at 2:15, F24 at 2:42), with Silverstrike Barrage and Bursting Emptiness as the killing abilities. All were cascade deaths (#3+ in the pull), but surviving the Intermission is critical for reaching P2/P3. Saving Aspect of the Turtle for Intermission Silverstrike Barrage and using Survival of the Fittest during Stellar Emission channel would improve Intermission survival. BM Hunters can maintain DPS while moving during this phase, which is a class advantage to leverage.
4 Maintain DPS Consistency Across Sessions
Active DPS on long pulls ranged from 49.5K (F9) to 69.6K (F25), a 41% spread. The good news is the trend is clearly upward -- late-session pulls are the best. F17 and F25 (67.4K and 69.6K) show what Blowe is capable of when the fight is going well. The goal for next session is to start closer to 60K rather than needing 10+ pulls to ramp up. Reviewing notes on P2 target priority and P3 positioning before the pull could help establish the performance baseline faster.

What's Working

Strong Foundation
Zero wipe responsibility: In 20 pulls, Blowe was never one of the first 2 deaths. Every death was cascade -- happening after the pull was already compromised by multiple other deaths. This is exactly what a DPS should look like on progression.

Session improvement: +27% DPS improvement from first long pull to last. P3 DPS improved +55%. The learning trajectory is strong and accelerating, not plateauing.

Gear is competitive: At 292 ilvl, Blowe matches or exceeds reference players (avg 291). The DPS gap is not gear-related.

Rotation execution: Kill Command 12.0 CPM, Barbed Shot 8.8 CPM, good Dark Ranger proc usage (56 Black Arrows, 11 Wailing Arrows on the best pull). Core abilities are being pressed consistently.

Active defensive play: On the best pull, 9 defensive actions in P3 alone (Turtle, 2x SotF, 2x Exhil, Cheetah, Healthstone, Health Pot). Blowe is not passively dying -- active survival efforts are extending uptime.

BM Hunter mobility advantage: As the only spec that can execute a full rotation while moving, BM Hunter is well-suited for Crown's heavy movement requirements. This is being leveraged effectively in P2 (126.3K avg) where movement-constrained specs often see bigger DPS drops.
