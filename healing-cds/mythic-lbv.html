Mythic Lightblinded Vanguard — Full Raid Guide

Four Dads One Pizza — MW / RSham / RDruid / HPal / DPriest — 5 healers

Council of three paladin bosses, all active simultaneously. Kill order: Lightblood (Ret) → Senn (Holy) → Bellamy (Prot). Repeating spirit cycle (~160s) spawns Mender → Defender → Vindictive, each empowering a boss and activating an aura. Searing Radiance (15s ramping DoT) is the primary healing check. Mass Avenger’s Shield hits 19-20 players at ~1:10 and ~3:52 — Revival is the mass dispel. ~6 minute fight.

Bosses

  • General Amias Bellamy (Prot Paladin) — The tank boss. Casts Avenger’s Shield, Divine Toll, Judgment + Shield of the Righteous, and Divine Consecration. His Mass Avenger’s Shield hits 19-20 players and must be dispelled with Revival.
  • Commander Venel Lightblood (Ret Paladin) — The DPS boss. Kill first. Casts Sacred Toll (unavoidable raid nuke every ~18s), Judgment + Final Verdict, and Divine Storm. When empowered, Divine Storm spawns tornadoes melee needs to dodge.
  • War Chaplain Senn (Holy Paladin) — The healer boss. Kill second. Casts Searing Radiance (15s ramping DoT — MT pulls her out), Sacred Shield (absorb shield on herself), Elekk Charge, Exorcism, and Tyr’s Wrath (heal absorb on soakers).

Spirit Cycle (~160s)

Three spirits spawn sequentially in a repeating cycle. Each one empowers a boss and activates an aura. Light Infused pulses constant raid damage and ramps +25% each time an aura fires.

  • Spirit of the Mender spawns first → empowers Senn → Tyr’s Wrath (heal absorb on soakers)
  • Spirit of the Defender spawns second → empowers Bellamy → Aura of Devotion (75% DR on bosses, swap to adds)
  • Spirit of the Vindictive spawns third → empowers Lightblood → Aura of Wrath (doubles boss Holy damage) then Aura of Peace (Senn empowered → Tyr’s Wrath from Senn)

Kill Order & Timing

  • 1. Lightblood (Ret) — Kill before his 3rd ultimate. Empowered phase spawns Divine Storm tornadoes.
  • 2. Senn (Holy) — Kill before her 3rd ultimate. Most dangerous healer boss — Searing Radiance + heal absorbs.
  • 3. Bellamy (Prot) — Dies last. Gets a 3rd ultimate but Prot phase is the most survivable.
Retribution: When a paladin dies, the raid takes 5% stacking damage amp every 2 seconds. Ret must be dead before the final Mass AS (~6:35) so Revival is available for that dispel.

Key Mechanics

Searing Radiance (Senn)

15-second ramping DoT centered on Senn. Damage gets worse toward the end of the window. MT pulls Senn ~20 yards away from the raid at 3/4 of the cast bar. Fires at 0:11, 1:03, 1:55, 3:02, 3:54, 5:41.

Mass Avenger’s Shield (Bellamy)

Hits 19-20 players simultaneously. Fires at ~1:10 and ~3:52. Must be mass-dispelled with Revival immediately.

Sacred Toll (Lightblood)

Unavoidable raid-wide nuke every ~18 seconds throughout the fight. The constant ticking damage that makes everything else harder.

Tyr’s Wrath

Heal absorb applied to soakers. Comes from Spirit of the Mender (~0:34, ~3:13, ~5:52) and from empowered Senn (~2:23, ~5:02). Designated soakers stand in it so the rest of the raid doesn’t get hit.

Execution Sentence

Targets healers only. Damage splits across everyone nearby — whole raid stacks to soak.

Sacred Shield (Senn)

Absorb shield Senn puts on herself every ~55-60s. Needs to be stripped so she can take damage.

Dangerous Overlaps (prog walls)

  • 1:55-2:23 — SR #3 → Aura of Peace → Tyr’s Wrath (Senn) — W5 saves you
  • 3:02-3:17 — SR #4 + Tyr’s Wrath (Spirit at 3:13) — W6 saves you
  • 4:45-5:02 — Empowered Divine Storm + Aura of Peace + Tyr’s Wrath (Senn) — W8 saves you
  • 5:41-5:56 — Final SR + Retribution death stacks — W9, wipe point

MT = the mover. Pulls Senn for Searing Radiance. Holds Senn + one other boss.

Tanks swap Bellamy and Lightblood simultaneously every ~80-90s. Senn always stays on MT.
