Psychic Link — The Biggest Single-Ability Gap

4.89M vs 11.97M — 7.08M damage gap from one passive ability

Psychic Link is Shadow Priest's multi-target cleave engine. It replicates a percentage of single-target damage to all other targets with Vampiric Touch active. The reference generates 145% more Psychic Link damage because:

  • 12 manual Vampiric Touch casts vs 1. The reference actively maintains VT on multiple add targets beyond what Tentacle Slam covers. Nurnyx relies almost entirely on Tentacle Slam (30 casts, equal to reference) but does not manually apply VT to additional targets.
  • Higher overall cast volume. More casts on the primary target = more damage replicated via Psychic Link to all VT'd targets.
  • Better add target split. Reference puts 43% of damage into adds vs Nurnyx's 34%. More active add targeting means more VT applications and more Psychic Link replication.

This single ability gap (7.08M) accounts for 32% of the total damage deficit (22.15M total gap). Fixing VT multi-dotting alone would close roughly a third of the DPS gap.

Actionable Improvements

1

Multi-DoT with manual Vampiric Touch on adds

The single largest damage loss. Reference casts VT 12 times manually (in addition to 30 Tentacle Slams) to maintain coverage on Sporecaps, Shroomlings, and Funglings. Nurnyx casts VT once the entire fight. Each additional VT target multiplies Psychic Link damage on every single-target cast. On Rotmire with constant add spawns, this is a massive multiplier.

Impact: ~7M damage (32% of the total gap). This is the #1 priority.
2

Eliminate GCD gaps — always have the next cast queued

20 gaps >2.5s (vs ref's 14) add up to 57.8s of dead time (ref: 41.5s). 16.3s of extra downtime at 1.35 CPS = ~22 lost casts. The pattern shows 45% of gaps follow Shadowy Apparition resolving and 35% follow Mind Flay completing. Pre-queue the next ability before the current GCD finishes. Shadow Priest has a clear priority: check Mind Blast charges → Shadow Word: Madness if target needs it → Mind Flay: Insanity if Madness is active → Mind Flay filler. Knowing this priority cold eliminates decision-time gaps.

Impact: ~22 recovered casts, ~5-8% DPS increase from uptime alone.
3

Cast Vampiric Embrace on cooldown (5 uses, not 3)

VE is a 2-minute cooldown. Nurnyx used it 3 times (first at 2:16) with ~170s average interval. Reference used it 5 times starting at 0:04 with ~125s average interval. The first VE should go out in the opener alongside Voidform. Beyond personal damage, VE is a raid utility CD that heals the group — missed uses also hurt the healing team.

Impact: 2 additional VE windows of damage-to-healing throughput, plus better CD alignment with Voidform.
4

Improve quiet window uptime (pre-mechanic positioning)

11 of 18 quiet windows are flagged, with the worst being pre-Bloom #3 (-41% vs ref). These are free-casting windows where no mechanics are happening. The pattern suggests Nurnyx is moving or hesitating before mechanics arrive rather than casting until the mechanic forces a response. Pre-position early, but keep casting until the last possible moment. Shadow Word: Death and Shadow Word: Pain refreshes are instant casts usable during any forced movement.

Impact: Recovering half the quiet window deficit would add ~15-20 casts across the fight.
5

Reduce Shadow Word: Death usage (26 casts vs 14)

Nurnyx casts SWD almost twice as often as the reference. SWD does less damage per GCD than Mind Blast or Mind Flay: Insanity and inflicts self-damage (246K total). SWD is best used during movement (instant cast) or in execute phase (sub-20%). Outside those situations, it is a DPS loss compared to channeling Mind Flay or casting Mind Blast. The extra 12 SWD casts are GCDs that could have been higher-value abilities.

Impact: 12 GCDs redirected to higher-value abilities, plus 246K less self-damage to heal through.

Verdict

The 43% DPS gap is execution-based, not gear or spec

Nurnyx and the reference (Dlpsibm) run identical talents at virtually identical item level (291 vs 290). Nurnyx also had Augmentation Evoker buffs (Ebon Might) that the reference did not, making the normalized gap even wider. The three root causes are:

  1. Multi-DoT discipline — 1 manual VT cast vs 12. This single habit change would close ~32% of the gap through Psychic Link cleave alone.
  2. GCD uptime — 57.8s dead time vs 41.5s. Faster ability queuing and fewer hesitation gaps across all fight phases.
  3. Cooldown efficiency — 3 VE uses vs 5, plus one fewer Voidform (partially explained by shorter fight).

What Nurnyx does well

  • Survival. Zero deaths on the kill. Clean damage-taken profile with only one avoidable hit (Writhing Vines).
  • Mechanic handling during add phases. CPS during Awaken Fungi windows matches or beats the reference in 5 of 8 spawns (Adds #3-#6 all positive).
  • Halo usage. 8 casts matching the reference's rate perfectly. Tentacle Slam usage also identical at 30 casts each.
  • Bloom windows. Fungal Bloom CPS is within 7% of reference on 2 of 3 Blooms. No panic during raid-wide damage.
  • Consistent performance. Per-cycle DPS deficit is flat (-20% to -23%) with no catastrophic drop-offs in any particular cycle. The improvement from fight 2 death to fight 3 clean kill shows good adaptation.
  • Interrupt discipline. 3 Silence casts matching the reference exactly.
