Augmentation Evoker DPS Context
Augmentation Evoker is a support DPS spec. Its primary value comes from buffing allies with Ebon Might (+8% primary stat to all DPS) and Prescience (crit buff on 2 targets), not from personal damage. Raw DPS numbers will always appear lower than pure DPS specs. The real contribution is the damage amplification provided to the entire raid. Top-ranked Mythic Crown kills show Aug Evokers at 96-103K DPS on full-length kills. During prog, where pulls end at varying points in the fight cycle, per-pull DPS varies significantly based on where cooldowns land relative to the wipe.

Actionable Items
1. Survival: Outstanding -- No Patterns to Fix
18/19 pulls with no actionable deaths. The single actionable death (fight 22, Echoing Darkness at 0:53 in P1) is a one-off, not a pattern. Continue using Obsidian Scales and Zephyr proactively. Defensive usage profile is strong -- 6 Obsidian Scales and 3 Zephyr in the best pull shows active mitigation awareness.
2. Reduce Simulacrum Backlash Damage (P2)
1.19M damage from Simulacrum Backlash across 47 hits in fight 14. This is the most avoidable damage source. When Rift Simulacrums die in P2, they explode for proximity damage. Better positioning during Simulacrum kills -- backing away before the kill goes through or using Hover to reposition quickly -- would reduce total damage intake and lessen healing pressure during an already demanding phase.
3. P3 Experience -- More Reps Needed
Only 6/19 pulls reached P3. The P3 DPS variance is high (56.9K to 104.9K), which is expected when the raid is still learning the phase. The 104.9K pull (fight 17) shows the ceiling. More P3 reps will naturally stabilize this. Focus on understanding Cosmic Radiation positioning and Gravity Collapse timing to extend survival in P3.
4. Shoulder Upgrade Priority
The 276 shoulders are 15 ilvl below the overall average of 291. Upgrading this slot would provide a meaningful stat increase. All other gear slots are 285-298. This is the single largest gear improvement available.
5. Continue Strong Utility Play
Zephyr (3), Time Spiral (3), and Rescue (2) in the best pull show excellent support play. This is Aug Evoker's core value -- buffing the raid with Ebon Might and Prescience while providing defensive utility. Prescience at 5.0 CPM and Ebon Might at 1.7 CPM show active buff management. Continue prioritizing Prescience on the top 2 DPS in each phase.
