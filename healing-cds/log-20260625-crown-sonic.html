Actionable Items

1. Survival: No Significant Issues

Only 1 actionable death in 19 pulls (Fight 1, Void Remnants at 1:50 in P1). This was the 2nd death in the raid that pull. Every other death was a cascade wipe death. Sonic is consistently one of the last players standing in wipes (median: 6-16 players dead before Sonic dies). No mechanical failure pattern to address.

2. DPS: Solid Foundation, Room to Grow with Reps

57.4K avg effective DPS is a reasonable prog performance on night 4. The 66.5K best pull on a 6:53 wipe shows the ceiling when execution is clean. The gap to reference (97.3K avg on kills) will narrow naturally as the raid reaches deeper into P3 and Sonic gets more full-fight reps with second Tyrant windows and execute-phase damage.

Projection: On a clean kill (~8 min), DPS should land in the 70-85K range based on current trajectory.

3. DPS: P2 Movement Optimization

P2-death pulls average 50.3K vs P3 pulls at 60.5K. Some of this gap is structural (shorter fight = fewer Tyrant windows), but P2 is the most movement-heavy phase for ranged DPS. Demonology has tools to maintain uptime during movement: Demonbolt instant cast procs, pre-positioned Demonic Circle, and strategic Tyrant hold before Rift transitions.

Suggestion: Pre-place Demonic Circle before known Rift movement windows. Hold Tyrant if Rift transition is imminent within 10s. Bank Demonbolt procs for forced movement.

4. Fight 1 Void Remnants: Positioning Awareness in P1

The single actionable death (Fight 1, Void Remnants at 1:50) was a P1 positioning error. Void Remnants are ground effects left by Sentinels. This happened once as the 2nd raid death and never recurred under the 2-death threshold. Sonic corrected after pull 1 — no recurring pattern, but worth maintaining awareness on early pulls before the warmup kicks in.

5. Raid-Level Priority: P1 Survival

This is the biggest unlock for Sonic's DPS and the team's progression. 9 of 19 pulls died in P1 before Intermission. On pulls that get past P1, the raid consistently reaches P2, and Sonic's DPS is significantly higher. Getting the raid through P1 cleanly on more pulls will give Sonic (and all DPS) more opportunities to build the P2/P3 muscle memory needed for the kill.
