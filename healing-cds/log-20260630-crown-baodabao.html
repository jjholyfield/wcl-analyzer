Actionable Improvements

1

Survive Intermission Silverstrike Barrage

100% of Baodabao's actionable deaths (2/2) came from Silverstrike Barrage during the Intermission. The pattern is identical both times: Stellar Emission ticks chipping health, then a 450-480K Silverstrike Barrage hit killing through remaining HP. Pre-shield with Ice Barrier before the Intermission starts, and use either Greater Invisibility (60% DR) or Ice Cold (full immune) specifically for the Silverstrike Barrage cast. This is a pure survival phase -- no DPS is expected, so burning a 2-minute defensive is free.

Impact: Eliminates 100% of actionable deaths. Surviving Intermission means reaching P2 with full health, preventing the early-death cascades seen in fights 6 and 15.
2

P2 DPS Consistency (119K-168K Range)

P2 (Rift phase) has a 40% spread between worst and best pull DPS. Some of this is raid-state dependent, but the gap between 119K and 168K suggests inconsistent Frozen Orb and Ray of Frost alignment with the phase transition. On fights where Frozen Orb is available as P2 starts (2:53), P2 DPS is higher. Consider holding Frozen Orb in the last 10-15 seconds of P1 if it would come off cooldown during the Intermission anyway -- banking it for the P2 opener ensures a strong start to the longest damage phase.

Impact: Tightening the P2 floor from 119K toward the 140K average would add ~3-5K overall DPS on longer pulls.
3

P3 Experience and Survival

Baodabao reached P3 in 9 of 12 qualifying pulls (75%). P3 DPS ranges from 21K to 69K, but the low-end values correlate entirely with compromised raids (cascade deaths). The high-end P3 performance (69K in fight 12, 65-68K in fights 3, 7, 10) shows strong execution when the raid is intact. As the raid improves P2 survival and more pulls reach P3 with full roster, this number will stabilize around the 60-70K range.

Impact: No rotation changes needed for P3. The variance is a raid survival issue, not a Baodabao execution issue. Continue learning P3 positioning for Gravity Collapse (already using Slow Fall) and Cosmic Radiation.
4

Cooldown Alignment for Kill Attempt

When the raid reaches the point of kill attempts (8+ minutes), Icy Veins and Frozen Orb timing for the execute phase will matter significantly. Reference kills show 107K+ DPS over 8-9 minute fights, which means the P3 execute with lust should push well above the 69K P3 ceiling seen in prog. Plan Frozen Orb and Icy Veins usage in P3 to align with the burn window when lust is called. This is forward-looking -- not yet relevant during prog.

Impact: Proper CD alignment during the kill-attempt burn phase could add 15-20K DPS in the final 90 seconds.
