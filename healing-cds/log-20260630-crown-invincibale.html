Actionable Improvements

1Use Spiritwalker's Grace more aggressively during movement
Invincibale used SWG at 0.7 CPM vs Ghostsham's 1.5 CPM -- less than half the rate. SWG has a 1-minute cooldown and lets you cast while moving for 15 seconds. On Crown, there is forced movement from Aspect of the End tethers (P2/P3), Gravity Collapse knockback, and Cosmic Radiation spreading. Every SWG converts ~5-7 otherwise-lost GCDs into damage.
Estimated impact: 3-5% DPS increase in P2/P3 from recovered movement GCDs
2Shift Lava Burst casts to Lightning Bolt and Elemental Blast
Invincibale's Lava Burst CPM (10.2) is 36% higher than Ghostsham's (7.5), while Elemental Blast CPM (4.7) is 11% lower. With Stormbringer, Lightning Bolt generates Tempest stacks and Power of the Maelstrom procs. Overcasting Lava Burst (especially when it doesn't have a guaranteed proc from Flame Shock) reduces Tempest generation and means fewer Maelstrom-capped Elemental Blasts. Similarly, Flame Shock CPM (7.4 vs 5.3) suggests over-refreshing the DoT.
Estimated impact: 5-8% DPS increase from better Tempest generation and Maelstrom spending
3Reduce falling damage through better positioning
Taking falling damage in 5/5 deep pulls (10 total hits, 1.9M damage) means getting displaced off platforms or knocked into gaps regularly. This costs both health (healer attention) and likely GCDs running back into position. Pre-position closer to center during Gravity Collapse windows, and use Gust of Wind reactively if displaced.
Estimated impact: Survival improvement + 1-2 recovered GCDs per displacement event
4Improve P2 target acquisition speed
P2 DPS averages 78.8K vs reference 89.6K (-12.1%). P2 involves switching to Rift Simulacrum (spawns on the furthest platform), Undying Voidspawn, and Silver Simulacrum. The gap likely comes from slower target switching and movement. Pre-planning Flame Shock on new targets as they spawn and using Stormkeeper + Ascendance on the Rift Simulacrum window would help close this gap.
Estimated impact: Closing the 12% P2 gap would add ~2-3K to overall DPS on long pulls
5Continue building P3 familiarity -- performance will improve with reps
P3 DPS (58.3K avg) looks low vs reference (182K), but the comparison is invalid -- the reference has a full execute phase and clean P3. Invincibale's P3 samples are short windows before raid wipes. The best P3 pull (80K on Fight 15) is a solid starting point. As the team gets more P3 reps and the raid survives longer, P3 DPS will naturally rise from better CD alignment and reduced panic movement. This is not an execution problem -- it is a familiarity problem.
Expected outcome: P3 DPS will converge toward reference levels as the team stabilizes in the phase
