Slayer Respec: Working Well

The switch from Mountain Thane to Slayer between night 4 and night 5 correlates with a 25% jump in best-pull DPS (54K to 67.7K). 3

P2 DPS: Room for Growth as Fight Knowledge Improves

P2 average DPS (112.5K from raw events) drops 58% from P1 (270.6K). Some of this is natural (fewer cleave targets in P2 vs 3 Sentinels in P1), but the movement from Rift Slash and Voidstalker Sting management likely costs uptime. As the team sees more P2 time and mechanics become routine, maintaining Enrage uptime during Rift movement windows will be the main DPS lever. The Recklessness timing at P2 entry (2:56) is already correct.

4

P3 Experience: More Reps Needed

Only reached P3 in 9 of 20 pulls, and only had clean P3 time in about 5 of those. On the clean P3 pulls (F11: 97.6K, F12: 102.3K, F17: 111.6K, F25: 96.3K), DPS is solid. The gap to reference (~90-97K kill DPS including all phases) is closing when Starfighter gets a clean P3. The main need is simply more P3 reps -- the team needs to arrive at P3 with fewer prior deaths so Starfighter can practice the full phase.

Watch for Platform Edge in P3
