Mythic Crown of the Cosmos — Healing CD Plan

Four Dads One Pizza — HPal / RDruid / RSham / MW (4 heal)

Alleria Windrunner — ~8:20 fight across three phases plus transitions.

P1 — Sentinels: Kill three Sentinels (Demair → Morium → Vorelus) under constant Echoing Darkness pulsing and Void Expulsion pools (ranged only). Silverstrike Arrows break Umbral Tethers — mythic rule: only hit a Sentinel if below 30% HP when arrows appear, skip the set if above. Grasp of Emptiness slows 60% (vs 35% heroic).

P2 — Rift Simulacrum: 4 DPS take a gateway to a separate platform to kill the Rift Simulacrum. Main group stays on the main platform handling Grasp + Void Expulsion. Two Images of Alleria spawn and pulse ramping raid-wide damage until hit by Grasp beams — this is the sustained healing pressure at ~3:15. Arrows hitting Alleria remove 4 energy (8 per set). When the strike team kills the Simulacrum (~3:50), Simulacrum Backlash hits the entire raid — hardest healing check of the fight. Cosmic Barrier on the Simulacrum also pulses Shadow damage raid-wide while the shield is up.

P3 — Devouring Cosmos: Each platform spawns a Rift Simulacrum and an Undying Sentinel (from P1). Boss must stay 25yd from Simulacrums or Empowering Darkness stacks (10% damage done / 10% DR per second). Aspect of the End tethers three players every ~20s — breaking at 30yd triggers Gravity Collapse (raid burst + 300% physical damage taken). Devouring Cosmos drops void puddles covering entire platform sections at ~6:23, 7:23, 8:23 — take the gateway only when the puddle is already under you. By late P3 the raid is split across 2–3 islands. Mobility-limited classes need planned grips/rescues for platform swaps.

RDruid note: Top teams run zero Incarnation, Flourish, or Nature’s Vigil on this fight (0/5 teams across 18 exact comp matches). RDruid’s planned CD is Tranquility on cooldown every 3 min. The rest is throughput — HoT blanket + Ironbark reactively.

Tank Plan

Source: top kills (Who Asked 6:29 kill, Vex Thal kill) via tank-swap-audit. Our tanks already match this pattern. Taunts are event-driven and deliberately NOT on note timers.

P1 Council

  • Morium casts Dark Hand on his tank roughly every 27s.
  • One planned pickup/swap when Morium and Vorelus are picked up, around 0:47-1:16.

P2 Alleria (~2:20-5:00)

  • She runs a fixed ~22s tank combo: Rift Slash → Voidstalker Sting x2 → Rift Slash.
  • The off-tank taunts 1-8s after the second Rift Slash lands, after EVERY combo.

P3 (~5:00+)

  • Only Voidstalker Sting continues, at a ~14-17s cadence.
  • Swap every 1-2 Stings.

How to Use Them

  • 1-2 min DR (AMS, Dark Pact, Divine Protection, Barkskin, Obsidian Scales, Spell Reflection, Feint): use one before every window.

  • Shadow and Disc Priest have no proactive DR (Desperate Prayer and Dispersion are reactive).

Key Findings
RDruid Tranquility is the only planned CD — 5/5 teams use Tranquility exactly on cooldown at 0:10, 3:15, and 6:35. Zero Incarnation, Flourish, or Nature’s Vigil casts across all 5 teams. RDruid is valued for sustained HoT throughput and reactive Ironbark, not burst CDs.

Opener is Tranq + Asc + AW — 5/5 HPal AW, 5/5 RDruid Tranq, 4/5 RSham Asc at the opener. AM + SLT follow 15–20s later for Void Expulsion DR (4/5 each).

Backlash is still the hardest check — but without PEvo Rewind, it gets AW+AM+Asc+CC+Rally instead. 4/5 teams use MW CC here. 3/5 hold AW and AM for Backlash rather than using at Barrier #1.

P3 Barrier #3 (~6:50) is the second stack point — 4/5 use AMZ, 4/5 Darkness, 3/5 SLT, 3/5 VE. This is where raid externals cluster in P3.
