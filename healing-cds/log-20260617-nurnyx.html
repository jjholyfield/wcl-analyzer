Mind Flay Filler Gap (Critical)

Mind Flay is the primary filler spell for Shadow Priest — it should be channeled between cooldowns any time there is no higher-priority ability available. Nurnyx is casting Mind Flay at 5.2 CPM vs Salmer's 13.2 CPM, a 61% deficit. This means roughly 8 Mind Flay casts per minute are missing. Over the 6:07 alive time, that is approximately 49 missing Mind Flay casts. Since Mind Flay generates Insanity (for SW:Madness procs) and deals steady damage, this single gap cascades into lower SW:Madness procs and lower total damage output.

Total Active CPM: 44.9 vs 54.3

Across all actively-pressed abilities (excluding Shadowy Apparition procs), Nurnyx is pressing 9.4 fewer buttons per minute than the reference. This translates to 74 seconds of total dead time across 22 casting gaps longer than 2.5 seconds. The longest gaps occurred at 186-193s (6.3s gap) and 65-69s (3.7s gap). Every second without a cast is a second without damage — the gap is not about pressing the wrong buttons, it is about not pressing buttons fast enough.

Psychic Link: The Hidden Multiplier

Psychic Link transfers a percentage of single-target damage to nearby enemies affected by Vampiric Touch. Salmer's Psychic Link did 12.8M damage (17.5% of total) compared to Nurnyx's 2.5M (6.2%). This 5x difference is caused by two factors: (1) Nurnyx only cast Vampiric Touch once vs Salmer's 6 casts, meaning fewer enemies had VT active for Psychic Link to transfer damage to, and (2) the lower Mind Flay filler usage means less single-target damage to transfer in the first place. Better Vampiric Touch uptime on multiple targets would dramatically increase total damage through Psychic Link alone.

Actionable Items

1. Stay alive. This is the #1 priority by far. Nurnyx died in all 20 pulls — a 100% death rate. Even on the kill, death came at 6:07, leaving 1:51 of zero contribution. The causes vary across mechanics (Festering Vines, Bursting Shroom, Fungal Bloom, Pustules), which means this is not about learning one mechanic — it is a general awareness and positioning discipline issue. Use Fade preemptively, save Desperate Prayer for emergencies (only 2 uses across an 8-minute fight), and stay at max range to avoid Shroom explosions and melee splash damage (62 melee hits taken).
2. Fill empty GCDs with Mind Flay. Nurnyx cast Mind Flay at 5.2 CPM vs the reference's 13.2 CPM — a 61% deficit, approximately 49 missing casts. Mind Flay is the default filler when no other ability is available: if Mind Blast is on cooldown, no MFI proc, and no other priority — channel Mind Flay. Every empty GCD is zero damage. This single change would recover the largest portion of the DPS gap.
3. Maintain Vampiric Touch on all targets. Only 1 VT cast vs the reference's 6 means Psychic Link damage was severely limited (2.5M vs 12.8M, an 80% gap). On a multi-target fight like Rotmire with adds spawning regularly, VT should be active on the boss and each priority add for maximum Psychic Link transfer damage. This single DoT management improvement could add 10M+ damage over the fight.
4. Reduce Shadow Word: Pain re-application. SW:Pain at 3.6 CPM vs the reference's 2.3 CPM suggests unnecessary re-application. SW:Pain is a maintenance DoT — apply once, it refreshes through normal Shadow gameplay. Extra GCDs spent re-applying SW:Pain are GCDs not spent on Mind Flay or other damaging abilities.
5. Cast while moving. 10 Angelic Feather casts (vs 1 from reference) and the correlation between movement mechanics and casting gaps suggests significant uptime loss during movement. Shadow Word: Death, Shadow Word: Pain refresh, and instant-cast abilities should be prioritized during movement phases. The 6.3-second gap at 3:06-3:13 (likely during Festering Vines) represents a complete stop in damage output that could be partially recovered with instant casts.
Context

The 52% DPS gap (82.2K vs 171.5K full fight) breaks down as: ~24% from dying at 6:07 (no damage for 1:51), ~15% from ilvl difference (289.7 vs 272.8 — the reference has 15 ilvls more), and ~13% from execution gaps (CPM deficit, VT uptime, filler usage). Gear will improve naturally. Survivability and filler discipline are the actionable levers.

What is already good: Mind Flay: Insanity usage matches the reference (-3%), indicating proper proc usage when MFI is available. Halo usage is on-pace (1.0 CPM both). Voidform and Power Infusion alignment are reasonable. Shadow Word: Death usage is actually above the reference (+92%), showing good execute awareness. The core rotation priority is understood — the gap is filling the spaces between priorities.
